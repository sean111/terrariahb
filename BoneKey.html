This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Bone Key is a drop from the Dungeon Guardian. It is used to summon a Baby Skeletron Head pet that follows you until you cancel the buff icon, like other pets. It has not been determined if it has a use.

Because the Dungeon Guardian has an insanely high amount of defense and instant-kills any player, it is an extremely difficult item to get.

Do keep in mind that this item cannot be obtained in a world where Skeletron has already been defeated, as Dungeon Guardians will no longer spawn.
