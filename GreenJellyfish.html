The Green Jellyfish is found in underground and is far stronger than Pink or Blue Jellyfish. Depending on whether it's killed inside an Underground Hallow or Underground Corruption Soul of Light or Soul of Night can be dropped. They can be quite a shock to players first entering hardmode because they do a ton of damage; even more than lava!!
