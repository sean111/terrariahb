A Keg is a crafting station that is used to brew Ale.

The Keg is two blocks wide, two blocks high, and may be placed on top of solid blocks, flat surface items, and Wood Platforms.

The Keg can be found in randomly generated underground cabins.

Even though there seems to be an iron tap on the right side, iron is not in the recipe.

The keg cannot be turned around like chairs, instead the tap will always be on the right side no matter from which direction you place it from.
