PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Cutlass is a rare sword that is dropped by Pirate Deckhands in a Pirate Invasion. It appears to be a scimitar with a gold handle and an iron blade. The Pirate Invasion is summoned when using a Pirate Map, but it can also spawn randomly. The Cutlass deals very high damage, even higher than Excalibur. The Cutlass has a very fast attack speed and you can swing it continuously.

Trivia

  • This can be considered a Hardmode equivalent of the Muramasa - it automatically swings, has a lightning-fast attack speed, and relatively low knockback.
  • Strangely, this has a lower rarity than the Excalibur, despite it being stronger than the latter.
  • In the game, the white pixels in the Cutlass' sprite appear to be missing, making those parts completely transparent.
  • When talking to an NPC, you can only swing your Cutlass once per click, meaning you cannot swing it continuously.
  • The best prefix is Legendary
