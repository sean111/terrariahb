This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Pickaxe Axe is an alternative to the Drax, for those that prefer to use pickaxes, or find the drill's noise to be annoying. Appearances and sounds aside, the two tools are functionally identical. However, unlike drills, pickaxes are reforged with any Melee modifiers, including size, speed, and knockback, making Legendary the best possible prefix.

It can mine any block/ore except Lihzahrd Bricks.

Trivia

  • The tooltip mentions a hamdrill. That could be a reference to the old name of the Drax, which was Hamdrax.
  • While most of the hallowed items emit light when swung, the Pickaxe Axe does not.
