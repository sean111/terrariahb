The Clothier Voodoo Doll is a voodoo doll of the Clothier rarely dropped by Angry Bones and Dark Casters. It is used to summon Skeletron (only at night, as before) after the Clothier's curse has been removed. To use it, you must equip the voodoo doll in your accessory slot and then kill the Clothier.

To kill the Clothier with the voodoo doll equipped you need to use some form of blade. Other weapons such as boomerangs and guns will have no effect even with the doll equipped.

Unlike the Guide Voodoo Doll, the Clothier Voodoo Doll is not harmed by lava because the Rarity of this item is Blue.

  • The Clothier Voodoo Doll may be a reference to the Leather Doll called Wilson that the Clothier tells the player he made because he was lonely. This may be a reference to the volleyball named Wilson that Tom Hanks "made" in the movie Castaway.
