Neptune's Shell is a Hardmode Accessory that aids underwater performance by allowing indefinite breathing underwater, enabling swimming via the jump key, and eliminating the usual slow movement speed caused by water.

Entering water with this accessory equipped transforms the player into Merfolk, a buff with no time limit. While in this form, Water's usual effects are all but eliminated. The breath meter is eliminated entirely, and movement through water is as quick as it would normally be on dry land. Holding the jump key also makes the character swim (fly continuously upward) in water, while falling is just as fast as falls through the air.

Neptune's Shell can be considered an upgrade from the Diving Gear in most respects, however it does not activate in Lava, whereas the Diving Gear does (eg. while using an Obsidian Skin Potion).

Notes

  • While this renders the Diving Gear and all other water breathing/movement accessories and potions nearly obsolete, some may still be of use in Lava, or while wearing the Fish Bowl. Equipping the Shell and wearing the Fish Bowl in the equipment slot while out of water will not turn the player into a Merfolk. Players can also still drown in Lava while the Shell is equipped.
  • Swimming speed nears that of flying through air with Wings.
  • If under a Water Walking effect, players can hold the down key (default 's') to descend.
  • Landing in and "jumping" from water alone (by swimming up past the surface), without touching land, does not reset flight time for Wings, Rocket Boots, or Spectre Boots.
  • The Merfolk form overrides all clothing and equipment graphics. No armor or clothing will show on your character while Merfolk is active.
  • The Merfolk buff overrides the Werewolf buff, replacing the graphics and cancelling the bonuses granted by the Moon Charm during a full moon.
Bug: If you step into lava and catch fire while in water the transformation cancels and stays canceled until you leave and re-enter the water possibly leading to you drowning while still on fire.
Bug: If in water, with auto-pause on, pressing escape will display your normal form rather than the Merfolk form the Neptune Shell gives you when in water.
