Pinky is a rare Slime that drops about 1-2 Gold Coins.

Despite being the smallest slime and having an innocent-looking color, Pinky has one of the highest health values of all pre-Hardmode Slimes (besides the King Slime, and equal to the Dungeon Slime) and has decent Defense. Although it deals little damage, a new character may have trouble dealing with it, since it will take many hits from low-tier weapons.

Trivia

  • Pinky and Toxic Sludge are the only two members of the Slime family not to have the word "slime" in their name.
  • Pinky's rarity may be a reference to a variation of corundum called "padparadscha". Padparadscha is a pinkish-orange colour and is the rarest type of sapphire, just like Pinky is the rarest type of slime.

Notes

  • All Pinkies are pink, but if they spawn underground, the background may make them seem red or other colors, as they are translucent.
  • Pinkies are very strongly affected by the knockback of weapons and will fly very far if you hit them with a strong weapon.
  • Because of its size, this slime is very hard to hit with shortswords.
