PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Platinum Bars are crafted using 4 Platinum Ore at a Furnace and is an alternative for Gold.

Crafting all Platinum items requires 233 Bars, or a total of 932 Ore. Crafting only the weapons and defense armor will require 153 Bars, or 612 Ore.
