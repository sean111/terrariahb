Souls of Might are souls dropped by The Destroyer. The Soul of Might is used mainly for crafting the most powerful non-magic weapons such as Excalibur, Megashark, and Light Disc.

Notes

  • A player must defeat The Destroyer 3-5 times to acquire 90 Soul of Might in order to complete all the recipes.
  • To make five Light Discs for a complete set entails defeating The Destroyer 1-2 times to obtain the required 25 Soul of Might (included above).
