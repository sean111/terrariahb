Soul of Might is a Hardmode crafting material dropped by The Destroyer. Souls of Might are used mainly for crafting powerful non-magic weapons, such as the Megashark and Light Discs. Crafting all items, including a full stack of five Light Discs, requires 45 Souls of Might, meaning the player must defeat The Destroyer 2-3 times.

Tips

  • All types of Souls are very useful for selling as profit once your necessary items have been crafted but it may be inefficient to kill the bosses again.
  • To craft all items (including all 5 Light Discs and all 5 Keys) requires 50 Souls of Might, so you will have to fight the Destroyer at least twice.
