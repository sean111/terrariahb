Souls of Might are souls dropped by The Destroyer. The Soul of Might is used mainly for crafting the most powerful non-magic weapons such as Excalibur, Megashark, and Light Disc.

Notes

  • A player must defeat The Destroyer 3-4 times to acquire 70 Soul of Might in order to complete all the recipes once.
    • Making a full set of 5 Light Discs would require an additional 20 souls, bringing the total up to 90.
  • All types of Souls are very useful for selling as profit once your necessary items have been crafted but it may be inefficient to kill the bosses again.
