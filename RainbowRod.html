The Rainbow Rod is a Hardmode magic weapon that launches a mouse-controllable projectile, similar to the Flamelash or Magic Missile. The projectile is white, and emits a good deal of light that rotates through the entire color spectrum (when a color Lighting Mode is selected). Unlike the Flamelash, the Rainbow Rod's projectile can move through Water without dying, as well as Lava. It can be controlled for up to one minute before dissipating.

Notes

  • While holding the mouse and controlling the projectile, your mana will not regenerate.
  • It makes a large flash of light on impact, much larger than the Flamelash, making it useful for searching walls for caves and ore.
  • The Rainbow Rod is relatively slow-firing compared to other top-tier weapons.
Bug: On terraria XBLA, the rainbow rod can crash other players in online multiplayer when used.

Tips

  • With the proper equipment, the Rainbow Rod becomes one of the most damaging weapons in the game, and is useful in boss fights and other enemies with high defense.
  • The Rod can also be used to light areas remotely.

Trivia

  • The Rainbow Rod is unique in the fact that out of the three "magic missile" type weapons (the other two being Flamelash and Magic Missile), it is the only one that can be crafted.
  • Early pictures of 1.1 shows that the Rainbow Rod's sprite was identical to the Ice Rod, but red.
