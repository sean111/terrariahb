The Sunfury is a Flail weapon obtained from Shadow Chests in The Underworld. It emits light when thrown, and can also set enemies on fire. Like other Flails, it is a ball-and-chain that swings outward, returns when its maximum distance is reached, is largely affected by gravity, and will continue to hang off the player if the attack button is held, causing damage to enemies that touch it.

Notes

  • The Sunfury has the distinction of being the most damaging "ranged" weapon available prior to Hardmode (though it deals Melee damage) that can pierce through multiple enemies and consumes no Mana or Ammunition.
  • Reforged to a Modifier that offers a top Damage boost, like Demonic, Sharp, Godly, or Legendary, and while wearing a full set of Molten Armor, the Sunfury delivers a whopping 44 Damage per strike. With two Accessories equipped that have the Modifier of "Menacing", its damage rises to 47 (all while still consuming no Mana or Ammunition). These values rival most Hardmode weapons, so making these preparations when beginning Hardmode is a good strategy, as everything you need is available immediately without having to find the new Hardmode materials.
  • The Sunfury is even relatively effective in Hardmode without any Modifiers or other boosts.
