The Laser Rifle is a Hardmode magic weapon that auto-fires blue lasers that emit purple light and penetrate multiple enemies for magic damage. It is sometimes dropped by the Wall of Flesh. The Laser Rifle is similar to the Space Gun, but fires more rapidly, its lasers travel faster, and it causes more damage and knockback.

Being considered a magic weapon, rather than a Gun, the best Modifier the Laser Rifle can acquire is Mythical. It is unable to acquire Ranged modifiers.

Notes

  • Unlike the Space Gun, the Laser Rifle will consume mana even with Meteor Armor equipped.

Tips

  • This is a fairly powerful weapon against worms due to its penetration abilities and high fire rate (also making the Twins easier to hit) making good use of the time worms spend above ground.
  • This weapon is also fairly mana-efficient, as it can easily snipe some lesser mobs.
  • In PvP, due to its high fire rate, high damage, long range, high projectile speed, and high accuracy, it is extremely effective.
  • In Boss fights this can easily butcher the Eater of Worlds, or if you can dodge attacks well, can be effective against The Destroyer.
  • Its light, as with any other light emitting weapon, can be used while mining or exploring.

Trivia

  • The Laser Rifle bears similarities to the M41 Pulse Rifle used by Colonial Marines in Aliens.
