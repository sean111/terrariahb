The Iron Hammer is an early game hammer, being stronger than the Copper Hammer, and weaker than the Silver Hammer. It has an alternate ore version, the Lead Hammer, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all hammers, it is used to remove background walls and create sloped tiles and half tiles.
