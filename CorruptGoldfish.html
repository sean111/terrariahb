The Corrupt Goldfish is an enemy that results from a Goldfish that has been corrupted, either by the player using Vile Powder, by spawning within The Corruption, or by a Blood Moon event.

Notes

  • A Corrupt Goldfish can spawn out of a Fish Statue during a Blood Moon.

History

  • 1.2: No longer generates coins when spawned from statues or when a Goldfish becomes corrupted during a Blood Moon.
  • 1.0.4: Introduced.
