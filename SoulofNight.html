The Soul of Night is a crafting material dropped by all enemies killed in the Underground Corruption. This includes stray enemies not native to the biome, such as Goblins and Meteor Heads. The chance of dropping a Soul of Night seems to increase with depth. Enemies in the Underworld are very likely to drop Souls of Night when under Deep Corruption.

Notes

  • Although the tooltip says: 'The essence of dark creatures', souls of night can be dropped by any enemy as long as they are in the Underground Corruption biome.
  • An easy way to farm large numbers of Souls of Night is to summon the Eater of Worlds in the underground corruption area and quickly killing it with a Dao of Pow (Normally drops 8-10 souls per kill, and a Dao can kill him in 30 seconds just being left on the ground.) The same method, however, can not be used for Light Souls, as Eater of Worlds would flee due to not being in the Corruption.
