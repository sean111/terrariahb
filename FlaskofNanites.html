PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

While under the influence of a Flask of Nanites, melee attacks will inflict the debuff Confused to the enemy (Note: Does not work on bosses).

This item currently does not apply the debuff in player vs. player scenarios.
