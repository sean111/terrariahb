Demonite Brick

Demonite Brick
Statistics
Type Brick
Max stack 250
Use time 14

Demonite Bricks are crafted from Demonite Ore and Ebonstone. This is the only crafting use for Ebonstone at this time. A box of it also appears after the Wall of Flesh has been defeated. Its color is a white-blue, it is not resistant to explosions, and gives off a faint glow.

Notes

  • Demonite Brick is the only (craftable) brick that does not require 2 materials to make, instead requiring 6. Additionally, it is the only brick that when crafted, gives you more than one block, giving the player 5 Demonite Bricks.
  • It is impossible to make Demonite Brick Walls with Demonite Bricks.
  • Demonite Bricks (unlike Ebonstone) will NOT spread Corruption.
