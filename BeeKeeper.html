This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Bee Keeper is a weapon obtained by killing the Queen Bee in the Underground Jungle. Upon attacking an enemy it summons a friendly killer bee, and has a 10% chance of inflicting the Confused debuff on the struck enemy.

Notes

  • The Bee Keeper will not release bees while underwater.
  • It is the only source of the Confused debuff pre-hardmode.

Tips

  • Using the Bee Keeper on the Eater of Worlds will release many bees if it moves right through the swords swinging area.
  • Using the Bee Keeper on all enemies on your screen might release bees even though they don't have any enemy on your screen to damage. Instead they will fly to the air because they are targeting Harpies from islands in the sky. This is useful for finding Skyware Chests.
