PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Pumpkin Seeds are Seeds which are used to plant Pumpkins. They may be planted on green grass or hallow grass, and will eventually grow into Pumpkins. They need 2 blocks width and 2 blocks height of clear space to grow, and can grow anywhere grass will grow.

Pumpkin Seeds are available from the Dryad and do not drop from Pumpkins.

Trivia

  • In 1.2.1, Pumpkin Seeds had no value and were sold for free by the Dryad. This has been fixed as of version 1.2.1.1.

History

  • 1.2.1.1: Fixed a bug where pumpkin seeds were free.
  • 1.2.1: Introduced.
