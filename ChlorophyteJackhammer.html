This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Chlorophyte Jackhammer is a Hardmode Hammer crafted with 18 Chlorophyte Bar at a Mythril Anvil or Orichalcum Anvil. Even though it has a lower hammer power than other hammers, it destroys fairly quickly and serves practically all hammer purposes.

Notes

This tool functions much like a Drill or Chainsaw in that it mines and damages enemies via contact, and is the only "heavy machine" version of a Hammer in the game. As a result its best modifier is Godly, like the aforementioned tool classes.
