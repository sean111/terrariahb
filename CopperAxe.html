Copper Axes are the first tier of Axes, just below the Iron Axe. Like all axes, they are used to chop Wood.

A newly created character will spawn with a Copper Axe.

For list of modifiers, see reforging. Apparently due to the low base damage, it is unable to receive damage-affecting modifies that would change it by 15% or less.
