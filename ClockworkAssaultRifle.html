The Clockwork Assault Rifle is a gun that fires in three-round bursts. It is sometimes dropped by the Wall of Flesh. Only the first shot out of the three will consume ammunition. The attack button can be held for repeat fire.

Notes

  • This weapon has no knockback at all, and is thus incapable of acquiring the Unreal Modifier. The Demonic prefix is its best possible Modifier.
  • Meteor Shot has reduced effectiveness with this weapon, as only the first and sometimes the second shot of the three-round burst will hit.
  • The use time of 11 is between the last shot of the burst until the next 3 round burst. The actual use time, factoring in the time it takes for all three shots to fire, is around 24.
  • If the player has multiple different bullet types in their inventory, when the last bullet of a given type is fired, only the first bullet of the three-round burst will take on the proper appearance and effects; the other two bullets will be of the next type that would be used, but it will still only consume one bullet. This is most likely a bug related to the burst fire mechanic.

Tips

  • This weapon's high rate of fire and low ammo consumption make it ideal for mowing down large packs of enemies, especially if used with bullets that deal damage to multiple foes, like Crystal Bullets or Meteor Shot. This is especially useful during invasions, especially if the invaders can be grouped up in chokepoints.
  • These same strengths also make it effective against Bosses (though the Megashark is better, once acquired).
  • Despite being obtained by defeating the Wall of Flesh, this weapon is easily outclassed in terms of DPS by every Hardmode gun; generally becoming quickly obsolete.
Bug 1.2.1.2: Much like the Boomstick and Shotgun, when using the Clockwork Assault Rifle with Meteor Shot, only the first bullet will do damage unless the second and third shots are at a slightly different angle.
  • This weapon is superior to other firearms in terms of fortified defense as you can conserve ammunition firing while still being supported by cannons.

Trivia

  • This weapon is part of an ongoing steam-punk themed equipment set in the game.
