The Clockwork Assault Rifle is a gun that fires in three-round bursts. It is sometimes dropped by the Wall of Flesh. Only the first shot out of the three will drain your ammo, making this gun very conservative of your ammunition. You may hold down the attack button to continuously fire the weapon.

This weapon's high rate of fire and low ammo consumption make it ideal to mow down large packs of enemies, especially if used with bullets that deal damage to multiple foes like the Crystal Bullet or Meteor Shot. Additionally, its high damage makes it a close second to the Megashark for battling bosses.

This weapon has no knockback at all, and is thus unable to be found or reforged with the Unreal modifier. As a result, a Demonic Clockwork Assault Rifle is about the best that can be hoped for.
