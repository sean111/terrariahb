The Dungeon Slime is an enemy found in the Dungeon. It is a purple-ish slime with a Golden Key inside. It always drops the Golden Key when defeated. Unlike most other slimes, it does not drop Gel. It can spawn anywhere in the dungeon, but appears less frequently than other Dungeon enemies. The Dungeon Slime sprite, like the King Slime sprite, does not show the golden key in the source files.

Dungeon Slimes are immune to the Poisoned debuff.
