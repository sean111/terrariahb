The Rainbow Gun is a weapon found in the Hallowed Chest, which can be found in the Dungeon, and may be opened with the Hallowed Key. Firing the Rainbow Gun will consume 20 Mana, and fire a rainbow-like curving beam that deals continuous damage to any enemy that walks into it at a rate of approximately 5 "ticks" per second. The "rainbow" will persist for approximately 5 seconds before dissipating, or until another rainbow is fired; which will cause the first to fade.

The projectile will travel in a straight line before succumbing to gravity and curving downwards.

  • When firing 90 degrees, vertically, the beam will travel in a straight line for approximately 65 blocks before curving.
  • When firing 45 degrees, diagonally, the beam will travel in a straight line for approximately 4 blocks before curving, this is only because the length of the beam segments is approximately 3 blocks long.
  • When firing 0 degrees, horizontally, the beam will travel in a straight line for approximately 35 blocks before curving.


Notes

  • When fading, the beam will not fade all at once; but instead along its length similarly to the way it was formed.
    • Firing another beam will cause the first to instantly disappear without the previously stated "fading" effect.
  • The gun can be fired through one tile wide gaps in walls, floor and ceiling.
  • The best possible Modifier for this weapon is Mythical.
Bug : When firing at thin walls at certain distances; the beam will clip causing damage to any enemies on the other side.
Bug : When the beam strikes grass from a vertical position, it may cause to the grass to play the "destroying" animation and sound several hundred times within seconds before finally disappearing.
Bug : At the first end of the beam, there is a small area where mobs take no damage. This is mostly due to an incorrect hitbox.

Tips

  • This weapon will pierce infinitely until it fades, and is a great weapon against enemies with multiple segments; such as The Destroyer, as it will damage each section simultaneously inflicting tremendous damage.
  • The rainbow gun is also exceptionally powerful against walking creatures or teleporting creatures that have nowhere else to stand upon. In the hardmode dungeon, if the player puts their back to the wall, and has only one passage leading into the room they are in, they can lay down a rainbow that eliminates every enemy that comes at them besides the occasional Cursed Skull.
  • For the same reasons, the weapon is exceptionally potent against invasions.
