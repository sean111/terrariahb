Jungle armor consists of a Jungle Shirt, a Jungle Hat and Jungle Pants.

The Jungle armor grants the wearer 13 defense and is crafted from various materials, including Gemstones and items dropped from foes in the Underground Jungle biome - Jungle Spores, Stingers, and Vines, and is currently an early-game "Mage" armor, giving players an option to use magic instead of traditional combat.

When the whole set is worn it reduces mana usage by 16% and creates a grass particle effect when moving. Each individual piece increases maximum mana by 20 and magic critical strike chance by 3%.

The total material cost of making the full set is:

  • One gem of each six types
  • Stingers (12)
  • Vines (2)
  • Jungle Spores (32)

History

  • 1.1:
    • No longer requires Silver armor to be crafted.
    • Spore requirement reduced by 7/4/12 (from 15/20/20), respectively. Stinger requirement reduced by 8 (from 20).
  • 1.0.6:
    • Increases maximum mana by 60 (20 for each piece).
    • Increases magic critical strike chance by 9% (3% for each piece).
    • Set bonus: 16% reduced mana usage; formerly 25% reduction.
    • Defense of all pieces reduced by 2.
    • Jungle Roses in crafting recipes replaced by Jungle Spores.
  • 1.0.5:
    • No longer grants an unlisted 10% melee attack speed bonus.
    • Must be crafted, instead of dropping from Hornets and Man Eaters.
  • 1.0.3: Introduced. Replaced Cobalt armor.
