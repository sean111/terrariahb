Leather is a craftable item. It is obtained with Rotten Chunks, and can be crafted into the Archaeologist's Jacket and the Archaeologist's Pants.

It cannot be obtained in a Crimson world unless the player makes artificial Corruption due to the requirement of Rotten Chunks.
