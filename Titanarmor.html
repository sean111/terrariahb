Titan Armor is a top tier armor, which resembles a light blue version of Gold Armor. There is a 33.3% chance that a piece of Titan armor is dropped by Ocram.

It consists of a Titan Helmet, Titan Mail and Titan Leggings.

A full set grants a basic 45 defense, 30% increased ranged damage, 20% chance to not consume ammo, 10% increased movement speed and 20% increased ranged critical strike chance. It also gives a 28% chance to not consume ammo as a set bonus.

Trivia

  • Like the other console-content armor sets, Titan armor graphically uses recolored version of other armor pieces. The helmet being based off of the Gold Helmet and Titan Mail being based on Silver Chainmail.
  • A Titan is a mythical creature or being in Greek mythology that was even more powerful than a God. (The Titans came before the Greek Gods and were their ancestors)

Notes

  • Titan Armor is considered the ranged variant of the top tiers of console version armors.
  • Titan Armor goes well with the Vulcan Repeater and Megashark due to its "not to consume ammo" buff. Granting a 48% chance not to consume ammo, the Megashark has a 74% chance to not consume ammo with the full Titan Armor set.
