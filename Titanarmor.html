Titan armor is a top tier armor, which resembles a light blue version of Gold Armor.

It consists of a Titan Helmet, Titan Mail and Titan Leggings.

A full set grants a basic 45 defense, 30% increased ranged damage, 20% chance to not consume ammo, 10% increased movement speed and 20% increased ranged critical strike chance. It also gives a 28% chance to not consume ammo set bonus.
