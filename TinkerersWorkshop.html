The Tinkerer's Workshop is a crafting station that is used primarily to unite pairs of certain accessories into single items that provide the abilities of both. For instance, it can combine the Rocket Boots and Hermes Boots into a single item (the Spectre Boots) that provides the same rocket jumping and accelerated movement as the individual items did, without occupying two accessory slots. The Tinkerer's Workshop is sold by the Goblin Tinkerer for 10 gold.

When combining items, the Tinkerer's Workshop will assign a new random modifier to the crafted item, which can then be reforged by the Goblin Tinkerer for the optimal bonus. DO NOT waste money reforging items right before combining them.

The Tinkerer's Workshop is also used to craft a Boulder, and can be used to trade all 12 recorded Music Boxes for the unique unrecordable 13th Music Box (Title).

Notes

  • The Tinkerer's Workshop can also be used as a three-block-wide flat surface for placing furniture items.

Tips

  • The Tinkerer's Workshop can be a crucial step in game advancement, as it effectively allows players to enable more accessory-provided abilities at once.
