The Spectre Hamaxe is an endgame Axe/Hammer, hence the name 'Hamaxe'. It is craftable by bringing 18 Ectoplasm and 18 Chlorophyte Bar to a Mythril Anvil. It combines the functions of an Axe (115% power) and a Hammer (90% power).

Its highest modifier is Legendary.
