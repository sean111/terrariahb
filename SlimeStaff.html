This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Slime Staff is a summoning weapon that summon a Baby Slime minion to defend you. The Slime Staff is currently the rarest dropped item in the game, with a drop rate of 0.01% (1/10000) from most Slimes and the Slimed Zombies.

The best possible modifier for this weapon is Mythical.

Notes

  • The Slime Staff can be obtained before hardmode. An easy way to obtain the Slime Staff without much effort is to construct a statue farm with one or more Slime Statues, leave it alone for awhile, and occasionally check if the staff has been dropped yet. Another good way of farming these is killing king slime many times as it creates and spawns slimes that can drop it
Bug : Frequently causes enemies to avoid damage when being attacked by a pet slime, due to its high attack speed causing brief invulnerability to enemies.
Bug : In Multiplayer, can sometimes only be seen by the player who summoned it, and not others.
Bug : A rare glitch might occur where the game replaces the Slime Staff with an existing Shadow Orb (item) that you have in your inventory, resulting in the loss of the Slime Staff. (may have been patched.)

Trivia

  • The staff itself may be a Shillelagh.
