PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Beach Ball is found in Water Chests.Using it creates an interactive beach ball for players to bounce around on their worlds. After 3 seconds of sitting still it turns back into a lootable item. The ball is affected by walls, terrain, liquid, and the player/s, but not affected by any attacks (melee or ranged) or furniture.

History
