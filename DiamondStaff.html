This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.


The Diamond Staff is the strongest of the 6 gem/bar staffs. It does a high amount of damage, has a high fire rate, pierces up to 4 enemies and has a low mana cost, making it a very good magic weapon early on. However, the materials are rather hard to collect. The best prefix is Mythical. It is still effective well into Hardmode.

Notes

  • If your world generated with Gold Ore, you will have to make the Ruby Staff instead, unless you can gather enough Platinum Ore with the Extractinator.
Bug 1.2.0.3.1: The bolts fired from the Diamond Staff (and other gem staffs and the Spectre Staff) can pass through the slanted side of sloped blocks.
