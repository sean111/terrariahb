Meteor Shot is an ammunition item used by any gun that can use Musket Balls. It can bounce from any surface once or penetrate up to one enemy. In order to craft Meteor Shot, 1 Meteorite Bar and 25 Musket Balls must be present in the player's inventory or ammo slots.

Tips

  • Meteor Shot is useful for locating Floating Islands, since it will bounce off them when fired straight into the air.
  • Meteor Shot is suitable for PvP, with enough skill it is possible to hit an enemy which is not normally reachable with a straight line.

Bugs

Bug: As of 1.1, meteor shot will only hit one time per shot when used with the shotgun and clockwork Assault Rifle when used against most enemies, despite multiple bullets passing through the enemy.

History

  • 1.0.5: Crafting recipe changed from 1 Meteorite Bar (and no Musket Ball) for 100 Meteor Shot to 25 Musket Balls and 1 Meteorite Bar for 25 Meteor Shot. (Undocumented change.)
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
