The Blowpipe is a weapon that fires Seeds as ammunition. Seeds are context-sensitive items that will not begin appearing until you have a Blowpipe in your Inventory. The Blowpipe is a ranged weapon used by players starting out trying not to get hurt. In that sense, it is very useful.

If you have a Blowpipe with you, there is a chance that any vegetation growing on normal grass blocks will yield some Seeds. Flowers growing in Clay Pots can also yield Seeds. Both wild and cultivated flowers typically drop 4 Seeds at a time. Long grass typically drops 1-2 seeds.

The Blowpipe only appears in Chests near the surface, in caves that are very small and caves that lead on to bigger caves.

Notes

  • The seeds used by the blowpipe cannot be planted; and inversely all types of seeds that can be planted cannot be used as ammo for the blowpipe.
