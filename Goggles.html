Goggles are a Headgear that adds 1 Defense when worn. They do nothing aside from providing that very small amount of defense. They are typically used by the player for sense of style, especially since the goggles do not cover the player's hair, unlike most other Headgear.

Goggles are particularly easy to craft, and players should consider them for their very early protection, before acquiring their first Copper armor. As players with this sense of style progress, they move their Goggles to a vanity slot to keep the look and the bonus given by a set of armor. Goggles are usually replaced with Sunglasses depending on which item the player prefers.
