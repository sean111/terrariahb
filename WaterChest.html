This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Water Chests are a type of chest that can be found in pools of water, most commonly in the Ocean, and rarely underground. In addition to containing normal chest loot (based on where they are found,) there are special items that can only found in these chests, listed below.

Loot

Water Chest Items
Trident
Breathing Reed
Flipper
Water Walking Boots
Beach Ball

Note: Water Walking Boots have only 1:15 spawn chance compared to others.

Notes

Bug : Water Chests placed on cobweb by world generation will duplicate several times if you place or destroy any block/torch next to it.

History

  • 1.2: Introduced.
