This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Amber Mosquito is an item which summons a baby dinosaur to follow you around. Using the item does not consume it and it will remain in your inventory while the Baby Dinosaur is active.

If you jump too high into the air, a pterodactyl will carry your dinosaur by the tail. The baby dinosaur is immune to damage from enemies and despawns upon player death.

This item can only be obtained by putting Silt or Slush into a Extractinator, with a 1 in 16,872 chance.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the film Jurassic Park, where dinosaurs are cloned using blood found in mosquitoes trapped in amber.
  • The fact that it's carried by a pterodactyl when in the air might be a reference to Dino Run, a flash game.
