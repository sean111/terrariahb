This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Party Bullets are one of 6 types of craftable ammunition for bullet-firing weapons. They may be crafted at a Work Bench once the Arms Dealer begins to sell Empty Bullets after Hardmode has been enabled.

These bullets may still be crafted Pre-Hardmode, provided that the player has obtained the necessary items for crafting them.

The cost of Party Bullets per round is 5 . This means that a stack of 999 costs 49 95 .

Tips

  • Party Bullets are extremely cheap, costing less than even Musket Ball while having better stats than Silver Bullet. This makes it a great choice for players that want to save money.
  • While Party Bullets can be seen as an upgrade to Silver Bullets, players may find the confetti annoying. Also, they do not benefit from the high velocity perk of the Uzi, Venus Magnum, and Sniper Rifle like Silver Bullets do.
  • The cost of each party bullet is 5 , and the sell price is 8 . So you can profit 3 per bullet, or 29 98 per stack (999 bullets).

Trivia

  • They may be a reference to the "grunt birthday party" skull from the video game series "Halo".

Notes

Anomaly: The cost per round for a Party Bullet's materials is less than its sell price. This allows the player to continuously generate money by buying the materials, crafting the Party Bullets, and then selling them.
