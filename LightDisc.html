The Light Disc is a boomerang type weapon similar in design and functionality to the Thorn Chakram. The thrown weapons travel quite quickly for boomerangs and can ricochet repeatedly off of walls. The main difference is that multiple Light Discs can be thrown, up to a max of five. The extras must, however, be crafted and stacked. Holding down the use button allows them to be thrown automatically at the fastest rate.

Light Discs emit a very small amount of blue light, and have a uniquely phosphorescent (glowstick-like) appearance when thrown in the dark. At a full stack of five, you will be able to throw them at roughly the same rate as they come back, unless you are traveling quickly away from where you are aiming. They are incapable of having random properties when crafted, due to their stackable nature.

Notes

  • This weapon is a reference to the movie Tron.
  • Its high speed, bouncing nature, and long flight time make it create a fair amount of noise when thrown horizontally in a narrow vertical shaft.
  • Unlike other ranged weapons of the type, it cannot be reforged.
  • Counts as a Melee weapon. (%Increased Melee Damage increases damage of this weapon.)
  • Light Discs travel slower than normal when thrown in lava/water.
  • A Light Disc can travel travel horizontally through 1 tile, if you are standing right next to it.
