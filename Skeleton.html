Not to be confused with the boss, Skeletron.

Skeletons spawn in the Cavern layer. They follow the Fighter AI, and can open doors. Unlike Zombies, they do not require a Blood Moon to do so.

Skeletons are immune to the Poisoned debuff.

Notes

  • The skeleton is one of the three mobs that drop the Hook (the others being the Piranha and the Undead Miner), which is used to create the Grappling Hook. The drop chance is 4%, or 1/25.
