Skeleton

Not to be confused with the boss, Skeletron.

Skeletons spawn in the Cavern layer. They follow the Fighter AI, and can open doors. Unlike Zombies, they do not require a Blood Moon to do so. They can drop a Hook which is essential for making a Grappling Hook

Skeletons are immune to the Poisoned debuff.

They can also be spawned by powering a Skeleton Statue.
