Silt Block

Silt Block
Statistics
Type Block
Max stack 250
Use time 14

Silt Blocks were added in 1.1, and falls under the soils category. Like Sand Blocks, Silt Blocks are subject to gravity and will deal damage to any player or mob they fall on. Silt Blocks they look similar to Ash Blocks but are a lighter color.

Location

Below cavern level mostly around the point where the lava pools are underground.

Uses

Silt can be used in the same way as sand, and can be used to fill lava pits. It can also be used to function in various traps, since it causes damage to whatever it falls onto.

Notes

  • When the Wall of Flesh is defeated for the first time, and the "Spirits of Light and Darkness" are unleashed, Silt Blocks inside the new Corruption and Hallowed biomes will be converted to Ebonsand and Pearlsand respectively, much like regular sand. After this initial conversion however, silt blocks will not be affected by the spread of Corruption and Hallow.


History
