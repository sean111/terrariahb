PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Caged Lanterns are small hanging Light Sources found only in Dungeons and cannot be crafted, much like the Chain Lantern. They are placed like Chandeliers but take up much less space, which makes them ideal for small rooms or passages. Can be toggled with wires.
