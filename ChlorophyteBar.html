This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Chlorophyte Bars are crafted from 6 Chlorophyte Ore, an end-game Hardmode ore which can be found in the Underground Jungle, or grown in mud.

Crafting all Chlorophyte equipment, excluding the arrows, bullets, Spectre equipment and Shroomite Bars, requires 288 Chlorophyte Bars, or a total of 1,728 Chlorophyte Ore.
