The Hero's clothes is a set of vanity items made up of the Hero's Hat, the Hero's Shirt, and the Hero's Pants. Each piece can be crafted from 3 Green Thread and 20 Silk. In total, this requires 27 Jungle Grass Seeds and 420 Cobweb.

The console version requires Purple Dye instead of Green Thread, so it needs 27 Mushroom Grass Seeds and 9 Bottles instead of jungle grass seeds.

These items are all equipped in the Social Slots and only provide a visual appearance.

Trivia

  • This costume is a reference to the Legend of Zelda lore, Hero's Clothes, as well as the main protagonist of the series, Link.
  • The Hero's Hat and the Robot Hat are the only headgear that gives a different hairstyle.
  • On the console version, it is made with purple dye instead of green thread and is purple due to obvious issues that would follow adding a Link costume to an Xbox/PS3 game.
