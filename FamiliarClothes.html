Familiar Clothes

The Familiar clothes are a unique set of Vanity items that always resemble the original clothing of whichever character is wearing them. The set is made up of the Familiar Wig, the Familiar Shirt, and the Familiar Pants. Each piece can be purchased from the Clothier for 1 gold.

Familiar Clothes "hide" your character's armor and return display of your character's default clothes. Like all Vanity items, these items are equipped in the Social slots, and do not provide anything but a change in visual appearance.

Notes

  • Familiar Clothes always resemble the original clothes of the character wearing them. If you purchase a set of Familiar Clothes with one character, and then have another character pick them up and wear them, the clothing will resemble the default clothes of the current character, and not the former character. Therefore, Familiar Clothes can not be used to make one character look like another.
  • Invisibility Potion has a greater effect with Familiar Clothes, as Invisibility normally keeps armor visible while only hiding your natural character sprite. Since only your natural sprite shows while wearing Familiar Clothes, Invisibility makes you completely invisible. Enemies will still target you this way, but in PvP, other players will be unable to see you.
  • The Jungle Rose, Mime Mask, Sunglasses or Goggles can be used instead of the Familiar Wig to return the character's original hair, though with the added headwear graphics.
  • You cannot put Familiar clothes on a mannequin.
