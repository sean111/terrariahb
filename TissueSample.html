PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Tissue Samples are dropped from both the Brain of Cthulhu and Creeper. They can be used in conjunction with Crimtane Bars to create Crimtane tools and Crimson Armor.

Shadow Scales are used much like Tissue Samples for worlds that spawn with a Corruption biome instead of a Crimson.
