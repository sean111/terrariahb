This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Lead Bars are crafted using 3 Lead Ore at a Furnace. Lead has a chance to replace Iron in your world.

Crafting all Lead items, including the Lead Door, Lead Anvil, and Metal Fence, requires 138 Bars, or a total of 414 Ore. Crafting only the weapons and defense armor will require 128 Bars, or 384 Ore.

Notes

  • In a world with Lead Ore, players can obtain Iron Bar by simply crafting Metal Fence and crafting that into Iron Bars. This should only be done for aesthetics, as all lead equipment are superior to their iron counterparts.
  • Like gold, lead is extremely heavy and quite soft in reality, which would render it useless for making tools and armor. Of course, Terraria follows its own logic in that regard.
  • Lead is a neurotoxin, which in reality, would make it EXTREMELY unsuitable for making the cooking pot out of, as it would likely lace the soup with lead, and poison whosoever should eat it.
