The Old Man is an NPC whose primary function is to summon Skeletron (the hardest pre-Hardmode Boss before the Wall of Flesh). He can be found at the Dungeon's entrance. When spoken to at night, the Old Man will give the player the option to "curse", which summons Skeletron. The Dungeon cannot be fully explored until Skeletron is defeated.

When spoken to during the day, the Old Man will tell you to return at night. He will also judge players with less than 300 life (15 hearts) or less than 10 defense as unworthy, but still allows them to summon Skeletron.

The Dungeon can be entered without killing Skeletron, but traveling deeper than ground level within it (below zero depth) will spawn Dungeon Guardians that will instantly kill the player.

When Skeletron is killed, the Old Man is freed from his curse and will arrive as the Clothier (as long as a vacant House is available), in addition to allowing access to the Dungeon. Thus, Skeletron can only be fought once per world, as if you kill the Clothier, a new Clothier will spawn (if a house is still available), not a new Old Man.

Notes

  • The Old Man can be hurt with Lava (though he only takes one damage per second), Dart Traps, which cause normal damage and poison effects and Explosives. However monsters do not deal damage to him. Killing the old man is pointless though, since his death does not eliminate the Dungeon Guardians that will appear inside the Dungeon, nor does he drop any items.
  • If the Old Man dies, or the player fails to defeat Skeletron, the Old Man will respawn at the Dungeon entrance the following day.
  • Some Dungeon entrances spawn just barely above zero depth, meaning very little of them is accessible before Skeletron's defeat (without being killed by Dungeon Guardians).
  • The Old Man can fall through layer 0 of the dungeon, preventing Skeletron from being summoned until the Old Man returns. This is only possible when the player is not near the Dungeon.

Trivia

  • The Clothier will spawn only after Skeletron is defeated. Looking very alike, it's easy to assume that the Clothier is the same character as The Old Man. This is further proven by the Clothier himself, who will sometimes say: "Thanks again for freeing me from my curse. Felt like something jumped up and bit me", and in the console version, where one of the tips on the loading screen reads "The Old Man at the Dungeon is a clothier. If only someone could free him of his curse..."
