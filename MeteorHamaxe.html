A Meteor Hamaxe functions as both a Hammer and an Axe, allowing an extra space in your Inventory instead of carrying both. It is crafted from 35 Meteorite Bars at an Anvil. The Meteor Hamaxe is very good as an axe with 100% axe power. Although it does only have 60% hammer power it can still mine lots like shadow orbs and basic things. As a hammer, it is better than lots of hammers like the Gold Hammer and The Breaker.
