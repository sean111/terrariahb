PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Like the Bowl of Soup, Pumpkin Pie grants you the Well Fed buff upon consumption, which increases the following stats:

  • +2 defense
  • +2% critical hit chance on all attacks
  • +5% melee speed
  • +5% melee damage
  • +10% magic damage
  • +10% movement speed

The buff lasts for 15 minutes (as opposed to the 10 minutes granted by Bowl of Soup), but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon or death.

Notes

  • Due to how easy it is to get ten pumpkins and the 5 extra minutes of well fed, it is recommended that players should make pumpkin pies for well fed instead of bowls of soup and cooked marshmallows
