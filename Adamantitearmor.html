Adamantite armor is an armor which was introduced in 1.1. It requires Adamantite Ore to be crafted. Like all Hardmode-material armors, the Adamantite armor has three different headgear/helmet options, allowing players to focus on magical, melee or ranged combat; choosing one of the options changes the set bonus to the armor. Equipping the full set gives off a faint pulsing aura around the character.
