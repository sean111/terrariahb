Adamantite armor is an armor which requires 54 Adamantite Bars to be crafted. Like all Hardmode-material armors, the Adamantite armor has three different headgear/helmet options, allowing players to focus on magical, melee or ranged combat; choosing one of the options changes the set bonus to the armor. Equipping the full set gives off a faint pulsing aura around the character.

Notes

Bug: The Adamantite mask, when worn on a player, shows white structures on the back of it that do not appear in the sprite.

The Adamantite armor set appears to be a visual reference to the Dragoon class from the Final Fantasy franchise.
