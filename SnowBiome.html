Snow Biome

Snow biomes are generated in all worlds created between the 15th and 31st of December, and in roughly 33% of worlds at any other time of the year. Snow Blocks are generated in place of grass and dirt, and trees have a snow cover on them.

Notes

  • Water does not turn into ice blocks in a Snow biome.
  • Snow blocks can go down all the way to the cavern layer.
  • It is possible for the initial spawn point to be in a snow biome.
  • Acorns can be used on snow blocks to plant snowy trees, but snowy trees drop no acorns themselves.
  • Snowmen will not spawn in this area by default. The snow biome generates regular forest enemies and is immune to the Hallow and Corruption spread, although it can contain areas of Corruption at world gen.
Anomaly: Even a single snow block that is placed will cause snowfall. Therefore it is very easy to make an artificial snow biome.

History

  • 1.1.1: Introduced.
