This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Chlorophyte Pickaxe is equivalent to the Chlorophyte Drill, with some minor differences; One of which being that the pickaxe version produces more knockback, another being that it does more damage than the drill. Besides this, the Chlorophyte Pickaxe may be preferable to the drill because it makes a less obnoxious noise; however, this is a matter of the player's personal preference.

Because this pickaxe doesn't provide ability to mine any new vital ores, it is arguable easy to skip. The Spectre Pickaxe requires only 18 ectoplasm more than this pickaxe, and is far superior. However, Chlorophyte is no rare commodity, so wasting a few bars for a pickaxe that will only last you a few days is not such a shame.
