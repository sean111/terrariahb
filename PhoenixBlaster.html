The Phoenix Blaster is an upgraded version of the Handgun that can shoot any form of bullet with the exact same damage as the Musket. It has a base power of 23, yielding 30 damage with Musket Balls and 32 damage with either Meteor Shot or Silver Bullets. It deals 7 less damage than the Molten Fury with Hellfire Arrows, but the combination of very fast attack and projectile speeds along with easily acquired ammunition makes it a far more lethal weapon. It was, arguably, the strongest gun in the game compared to the Minishark. However, it got a damage nerf, making players consider the two and since the Wall of Flesh update (1.1) the Megashark easily out-damages the Phoenix Blaster in DPS.

Notes

Comparison to the Minishark:

  • Advantages of the Phoenix Blaster:
    • Deals more damage per shot
    • Bullets travel faster
    • Slightly higher knockback per shot
    • Semi-Automatic
  • Advantages to the Minishark:
    • Higher rate of fire
    • Fully-Automatic
    • 33% chance of not consuming ammo or 46.7% if you are wearing Necro Armor
    • Fires more bullets per shot
    • More bullets means higher chance of hitting target (especially in cases like the Wall of Flesh where The Hungry block most of your bullets so you want any chance you can get at shooting around them)
    • Great for obliterating swarms of weak enemies as well as doing extreme damage to groups of stronger enemies.
    • Can hold back stronger enemies
    • Can be used to search for Floating Islands with Meteor Shots (though not recommended).

Trivia

Its name may be a reference to the popular "Desert Eagle" pistol.
