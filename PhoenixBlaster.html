The Phoenix Blaster is an upgraded version of the Handgun that can shoot any form of bullet with the exact same damage as the Musket. It has a base power of 23, yielding 30 damage with Musket Balls and 32 damage with either Meteor Shot or Silver Bullets. It deals 7 less damage than the Molten Fury with Hellfire Arrows, but the combination of very fast attack and projectile speeds along with easily acquired ammunition makes it a far more lethal weapon. Before it was argueably the strongest gun in the game compared to the minishark. However it got a damage nerf, making players consider the two, and since the wall of flesh update; the megashark easily out-damages the phoenix blaster in DPS.

Notes

  • Comparison to the Minishark:
    • Phoenix Blaster deals more damage per shot.
    • Phoenix Blaster deals more damage per second (against any enemy, with any ammo type).
    • Phoenix Blaster deals more damage per unit of ammo consumed (against any enemy, with any ammo type, even in Necro Armor).
    • Shots fired from the Phoenix Blaster travel faster than those shot from the Minishark.
    • Phoenix Blaster deals more knockback per second (with any ammo type).
    • Minishark fires continuously while the attack button is held (Phoenix Blaster requires a separate click for each shot).
    • Minishark fires more separate projectiles, possibly resulting in less damage wasted on overkill when fighting swarms of weak monsters. This could also be advantageous when firing for some reason other than to cause damage (e.g. when searching for Floating Islands with Meteor Shot).

History

  • 1.0.5: Firing animation sprite reduced in size.
  • 1.0.4: Damage reduced from 28 to 23.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
