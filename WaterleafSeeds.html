Waterleaf Seeds are obtained by harvesting Waterleaf while it is blooming. A Waterleaf plant only blooms when it is in water. The easiest way to procure these seeds in a Desert biome is to place a block on either side of the plant (for example, a block of sand), then pour a Water Bucket into the gap submerging the Waterleaf in water. At this time it will drop 1-3 Waterleaf Seeds.

Waterleaf Seeds can be placed in empty Clay Pots or in Sand and grown into additional, harvestable Waterleaf. Potted Waterleaf will grow anywhere, not just in Desert biomes, but will only yield seeds if you submerge the plant, Clay Pot and all, in water. Use a Pickaxe and left-click on the flower or herb above the Clay Pot to harvest it.
