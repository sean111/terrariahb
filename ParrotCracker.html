This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Parrot Cracker can be obtained from the Pirate if he is living in an Ocean biome. When used it spawns a Pet Parrot which follows you around like other pets, dealing no damage to enemies.

Notes

  • When the Parrot pet is summoned, the tooltip reads, "Polly want's the cracker". "Wants" is misspelled; "want's" is a rare contraction for "want is" which makes no sense here.
  • The Parrot Cracker is currently the most expensive item to buy, surpassing even the Clentaminator.
