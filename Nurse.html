Nurse

Nurse
Statistics
Type Town
AI Type Passive
Damage 10
Max Life 250
Defense 15

The Nurse is an NPC that will spawn once the following criteria have been met:

  • There is an empty house.
  • You have more than 100 maximum health (use Life Crystals to increase your maximum health).

The Nurse will heal your wounds and cure you of any active debuffs, for a small price. Healing costs can range from a few copper to a few silver, depending on how much damage you've taken. She charges Copper equal to 75% of the health restored (e.g. 75 copper for 100 health), plus an additional 7 silver coins and 50 copper coins for every active debuff on your character.

Notes

  • There appears to be conflict between the Nurse and the Mechanic during a Blood Moon. When both are present, they have various hostile sayings that replace their standard quotes (see Quotes). During this time, the Merchant has a chance of saying, "Oh, great. I can hear (Name of Mechanic) and (Name of Nurse) arguing from here."
  • If you have full health and the Nurse requires some coins to heal you, this is because you have some debuffs.
  • If you are planning on healing and drinking Ale, make sure to drink the Ale after healing, as healing after using Ale will remove the Tipsy debuff and all its attack benefits.
