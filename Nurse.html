This is a town NPC. You may need a home to get him/her

The Nurse is an NPC that will spawn once the following criteria have been met:

  • There is an empty house.
  • You have more than 100 maximum health (you have used at least one Life Crystal to increase your maximum health).
  • Merchant is alive.

The Nurse will heal your wounds and cure you of any active debuffs, for a small price. Healing costs can range from a few copper to a few silver, depending on how much damage you've taken. She charges Copper equal to 75% of the health restored (e.g. 75 copper for 100 health), plus an additional 7 silver coins and 50 copper coins for every active debuff on your character.

Names

The nurse may have any of the following names: Molly, Amy, Claire, Emily, Katie, Madeline, Katelyn, Emma, Abigail, Carly, Jenna, Heather, Katherine, Caitlin, Kaitlin, Holly, Kaitlyn, Hannah, Kathryn, Lorraine, Helen, Kayla, and Allison.

Eight of the nurse's possible names are variations on "Catherine".

Anomaly: Two of the possible names, Amy and Kayla, are also possible names for the Mechanic.

Notes

  • If you have full health and the Nurse requires some coins to heal you, this is because you have some debuffs.
  • If you are planning on healing and drinking Ale, make sure to drink the Ale after healing, as healing after using Ale will remove the Tipsy debuff and all its attack benefits.
Bug : The Nurse will cure the Potion Sickness debuff, but only removes the icon. The effect of the debuff still remains.
Bug : The Nurse will charge you money for nothing when the Campfire buff is active.
Bug : On occasion you will be able to heal again after healing once. Doing so will do nothing but use your money and a green healing 0.
Bug : On the console version, when you have full health, if you talk to the nurse, she will sometimes charge you as if you had no health. This can be fixed by taking damage or collecting a healing heart or drinking a healing potion.
Bug : When there is a water candle nearby she will charge you extra to heal that.

Trivia

  • There appears to be conflict between the Nurse and the Mechanic during a Blood Moon. When both are present, they have various hostile sayings that replace their standard quotes (see Quotes). During this time, the Merchant has a chance of saying, "Oh, great. I can hear [name of Mechanic] and [name of Nurse] arguing from here."
  • The Arms Dealer has a crush on her according to one of his quotes. The Nurse apparently has a crush right back, judging by one of her quotes: "Has <Name of Arms Dealer> mentioned needing to go to the doctor for any reason? Just wondering." This could probably indicate that she wants him to need healing so that she can spend time with him.
  • The quote "Does it hurt when you do that? Don't do that." may be a reference to Hee-Haw, when a man is slapped repetitively by a rubber chicken.

Quotes

Note: The Nurse has many quotes that vary with the amount of damage you've taken, whether or not you have enough coins to pay, and whether it is a Blood moon.

Anytime other than during a Blood Moon:

  • "Show me where it hurts."
  • "That's not the biggest I've ever seen... Yes, I've seen bigger wounds for sure."
  • "Turn your head and cough."
  • "I wish [Name of Demolitionist] would be more careful. I'm getting tired of having to sew his limbs back on every day." (Only if Demolitionist is present.)
  • "Would you like a lollipop?"
  • "I need to have a serious talk with [Name of Guide]. How many times a week can you come in with severe lava burns?" (reference to the effects of the Guide Voodoo Doll when it is thrown into lava.)
  • "Hey, has [Name of Arms Dealer] mentioned needing to go to the doctor for any reason? Just wondering." (Only if Arms Dealer is present.)
  • "Have you seen that Old Man pacing around the dungeon? He looks troubled." (Only if Skeletron is not defeated.)

  • "Trouble with those bullies again?"
  • "That's gonna need stitches!"
  • "You look half digested. Have you been chasing slimes again?"
  • "Hold on, I've got some cartoon bandages around here somewhere."
  • "Walk it off, (Name of Player), you'll be fine. Sheesh."
  • "Does it hurt when you do that? Don't do that."

When player is badly injured:

  • "Dear friends we are gathered here today to bid farewell... oh, you'll be fine."
  • "I think you look better this way."
  • "Eww.. what happened to your face?"
  • "You left your arm over there. Let me get that for you."
  • "MY GOODNESS. I'm good but I'm not THAT good."

During a Blood Moon:

    • "If you're going to die, do it outside."
    • "Hurry up and stop bleeding."
    • "You better not get blood on me."

When player clicks Heal:

  • When player clicks Heal if moderately injured:

  • When player clicks Heal at full health:

    • "I'm sorry, but you can't afford me."
