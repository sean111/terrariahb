The Groom is a reasonably rare enemy that spawns during a Blood Moon, and killing him is the only way to obtain the Top Hat. As the blood moon is rare then it is very hard to find one, let alone kill one.

It is possible for several Grooms to be present at the same time during a Blood Moon. This may lead to more than one Top Hat but as top hats sell at a price of 20 Silver coins that's fine.

Trivia

  • The fact he's an undead groom may be a joke referencing the Corpse Bride story or the Jewish folklore in which it's based.
