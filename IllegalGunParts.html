Trivia

  • The Illegal Gun Parts resemble a pistol grip and muzzle device; in regions where "assault weapons" are prohibited, the definition of "assault weapon" often includes a rifle fitted with a muzzle device and/or pistol grip, so they are based on actual illegal gun parts.
  • As is stated in the Sandgun page, the Sandgun has a graphic that resembles the Illegal Gun Parts put together.
