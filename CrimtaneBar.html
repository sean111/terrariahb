This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Crimtane Bars are crafted using 4 Crimtane Ore at a Furnace, and are used to make Crimson Tier items. Crimtane is equivalent to Demonite, but cannot be used to make bricks.

It's most abundant within the Crimson biome, but can also be found in small veins outside. Crafting all Crimson equipment requires 120 Bars, or a total of 480 Ore.

Trivia

  • The texture for the Crimtane bar is a recolored version of the Hellstone bar.
  • The sword, Bow and axe variants of the Crimtane set may be references to Daedric swords and axes in the popular game series The Elder Scrolls.
  • Selling Crimtane Bars is more effective than selling the ore dropped by bosses. One ore sells for 9 silver and the bar sells for 40 silver (11% more per ore).
