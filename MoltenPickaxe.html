The Molten Pickaxe is the sixth and highest-tier of Pickaxe. Only the Drills are better for mining with in the game, however they provide no light making the Molten Pickaxe advantageous in some situations. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine a majority of blocks and ores. A Molten Pickaxe can destroy most blocks in one hit. If used as a weapon, it has a 10% chance of setting enemies on fire for a few seconds.

Notes

  • The Molten Pickaxe is actually slower than the Nightmare Pickaxe at mining the weakest block types: Ash, Clay, Dirt, Mud, and Sand. It is still faster at mining all other block types. Therefore, it is advisable to keep both Pickaxes around so they can be swapped as needed.
  • The Molten Pickaxe is the only pre-hardmode pickaxe that can mine Cobalt.
  • Adjusted damage from Molten armor set bonus: 14
