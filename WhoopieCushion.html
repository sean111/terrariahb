The Whoopie Cushion is an extremely rare item found by killing Giant Worms and Diggers. It makes a raspberry sound when used, and serves no other purpose. Each Giant Worm or Digger segment has a 1 in 500 chance of dropping a Whoopie Cushion.

Trivia

  • Consider equipping a Cloud in a Bottle and using this while performing a double jump for an added comical effect.

History

  • 1.1: Also dropped by Digger, which replaces Giant Worm in Hardmode.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
