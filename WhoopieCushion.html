The Whoopie Cushion is an uncommon item occasionally dropped by Giant Worms and Diggers. It makes a farting sound when used and can be used to make the Fart in a Jar. Each Giant Worm or Digger segment has a 1 in 100 chance of dropping a Whoopie Cushion.
