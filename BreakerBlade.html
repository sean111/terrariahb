The Breaker Blade is a Hardmode sword. It is the largest sword in the game, and also has the most knockback. It is one of the possible drops from the Wall of Flesh.

Notes

  • Due to its massive size, it can hit a single foe multiple times with only one swing.
  • While slightly weaker than Night's Edge, it is a much bigger sword and has stronger knockback.

Trivia

  • It is the largest sword in the game, and has the longest range. The Blade of Grass follows as the second-largest.
  • This item is a reference to the Buster Sword wielded by Cloud Strife in the game Final Fantasy VII and related movie Final Fantasy: Advent Children. It is similar in both name and appearance, even having what resemble a pair of "materia" attached, as can be done in the game.
  • Due largely to the teeth on its blade, it also resembles the chainswords of the Warhammer 40,000 universe.
