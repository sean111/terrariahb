The Spelunker Potion is a buff potion that buffs the player with Spelunker; it gives a slight glow to all nearby ores, gems, chests, Crystal Hearts, pots, and alchemical herbs. The buff lasts for five minutes, but can be canceled at any time by right-clicking the icon. Since it faintly illuminates the surrounding blocks, a player might be able to see caves that contain ore in walls.

Every type of ore will be displayed in a specific color:

  • Copper Ore has a reddish tone.
  • Iron Ore is red and blue, flashing.
  • Silver Ore has a blue shade.
  • Gold Ore acts like copper, but with a slight yellow tint.
  • Demonite Ore is very hard to distinguish from silver. Since it always gives off a glow, no Spelunker is needed for searching it.
  • Cobalt Ore has a very distinct turquoise color, making for easy identification.
  • Mythril Ore looks like silver, only the texture is slightly different and has a hint of green.
  • Adamantite Ore is like copper but darker and more red.

Notes

  • Spelunker Potions are best used while playing in high display resolutions.
  • Spelunker Potions do not light up Hellstone as it is not considered an ore. Though, this is not much of an issue due to their self illumination.
