Throwing Knives are thrown consumable weapons that can pierce up to two enemies. They have a 50% chance of being retrievable after use, even after hitting enemies. Throwing Knives are sold by the Merchant during Blood Moons, and may also be found in Chests.

Throwing knives are greatly affected by gravity, and arc similarly to Shurikens and Bones. They have the greatest range of the three consumable throwing weapons (Bones having the least).

Throwing Knives can be upgraded with one Vile Powder to make Poisoned Knives, which causes them to inflict the Poisoned debuff.

Notes

  • The Magic Dagger is a magical version of the Throwing Knife, behaving identically but consuming Mana instead of stacks of the item. It is only available in Hardmode.
