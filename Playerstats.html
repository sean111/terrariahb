Player stats

Numerical miscellany for basic player functions and movement.

Health

All characters start with 100 Health upon creation. The character gains 20 Health for every Life Crystal used. A maximum of 15 Life Crystals can be used per character, meaning a maximum of 400 Health for a character.

Mana

All characters start with 0 Mana upon creation. The character gains 20 Mana for every Mana Crystal used. A maximum of 10 Mana Crystals can be used per character, meaning a maximum of 200 Mana for a character (not counting Armor, Accessories, Buffs, or Modifiers)

Horizontal movement

All characters start with a horizontal movement speed of 11.36 blocks/sec, or 22.72 feet/sec (as one block in Terraria is a two foot square). These values are ignoring the small amount of time it takes to accelerate from a standing position. Horizontal movement speed is capped at 1.4*base speed, or +40%.

  • The three hook tools, the Grappling Hook, Ivy Whip, and Dual Hook reel in the player at a constant speed regardless of speed boosting accessories. The more passive speed boosts a player has, the less noticeable using a hook is to move quickly.
  • The Hermes Boots and Spectre Boots build up to a fixed speed of max speed. Using movement boosts with one of the two boots only speeds up the boots' effect.

Vertical movement

Players are able to jump once to a height of 6 blocks above foot level with no extra equipment. A Shiny Red Balloon will increase this to 10 blocks. A Cloud in a Bottle will provide a second jump which begins at a similar velocity to the first, and is also affected by the Shiny Red Balloon if the player has both equipped. There are currently no items that make you fall faster, though the Ivy Whip or Grappling Hook may be used to temporarily "fall" faster by grappling downwards while in water or Cobwebs (they are not significantly faster than falling in air). The numbers for Rocket Boots are at maximum velocity, not when decelerating.
