The Silver Shortsword is an early game metal shortsword, being stronger than the Iron Shortsword. It has a slightly stronger alternate version in the Tungsten Shortsword.

Like all shortswords, this sword has very limited range, attacking with a horizontal stabbing motion instead of an arc, which makes it almost useless against flying or jumping enemies, although its high attack rate makes it effective against some fighter type enemies.
