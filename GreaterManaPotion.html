The Greater Mana Potion is a potion that restores 200 mana upon use and is the most potent of all mana restoration items. Unlike most potions, Greater Mana Potions cannot be crafted. They can only be purchased from the Wizard, who only moves in after being rescued in a Hardmode world.

As of 1.2.1.2, consuming any Mana Potion (Greater, Regular, or Lesser) will not trigger the Potion Sickness debuff, allowing magic-oriented players greater longevity in long battles and boss fights. Equipping a Mana Flower lets a player consume mana potions automatically.

Notes

  • Unlike other potions (Lesser and Normal/Regular), Greater Mana Potions cannot be combined with a Greater Healing Potion to make a greater restoration potion, which does not exist at all.
  • Greater Mana Potions have a maximum stack of 50, which is twice the max of Mana Potions and nearly four times the max of Lesser Mana Potions. It would cost 5 to purchase a full stack from the Wizard.
  • Even though the Mana Potion is used to craft the Mana Flower, the Mana Flower's sprite shows both the shape and two yellow bands of the Greater Mana Potion.
  • Using the Greater Mana Potion gives the player a unit price of 20 mana per 1 .

Know Bugs

    • If a player has a stack of 99 Lesser, Mana or Greater Mana potions predating the release of patch 1.2, when combined with other stacks of 99 the left over amounts above 50 will be forced in to a stack of the combined left overs. I.e. a stack of 99 used on a stack of 99 will produce a stack of 50 and another of 148. This can be repeated indefinatly as the *Bugged* stack have no cap. This will also work on any stack above 50.
