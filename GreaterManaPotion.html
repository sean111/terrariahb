The Greater Mana Potion is a potion able to be acquired once entering Hardmode. It restores 200 mana of a character on use, and is the most potent of all mana restoration items.

It can be purchased from the Wizard.

As of 1.0.6, consuming any Mana Potion (Greater, Regular, or Lesser) will not enact the Potion Sickness debuff, allowing magic-oriented players greater longevity in long battles and boss fights. Wearing a Mana Flower lets a player consume mana potions automatically.

Notes

  • Unlike other potions (Lesser and Normal/Regular), Greater Mana Potions cannot be combined with a Greater Healing Potion to make a restoration-type potion.
  • Greater Mana Potions have a maximum stack of 99, which is nearly twice the max of Mana Potions and five times the max of Lesser Mana Potions.
  • Even though the Mana Potion is used to craft the Mana Flower, the Mana Flower's sprite shows both the shape and two yellow bands of the Greater Mana Potion.
