Green Slimes are the weakest of all the slimes and spawn at a rapid rate during the day. It drops Gel and coins when killed. They are the enemy a player will most likely come across first when starting off.

History

  • 1.0.6.1: No longer get stuck in the ground and start sinking.
  • 1.0.5:
    • Now floats in liquids and doesn't make a splash sound when hitting water.
    • Damage reduced from 8 to 6; health reduced from 15 to 14; defense reduced from 2 to 0.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
