The Bunny Statue is one of 43 Statues. It spawns bunnies when wired and activated. During a Blood Moon, they will instantly transform into Corrupt Bunnies.

Notes

  • Prior to 1.2, Corrupt Bunnies would drop coins in the range of 4-5 silver, allowing the player to make up to 75 gold coins per statue per Blood Moon night. As of 1.2, Corrupt Bunnies no longer drop coins.
Anomaly: The usual Statue spawn limits do not apply to Corrupt Bunnies.

Tips

  • Bunny Statues can be used during a Blood Moon to obtain a Bunny Hood. Like all Statues, these can be Wired to up to three 1-second Timers (or up to six by using different colors of Wire) and placed in a thin layer of Lava, which during a Blood Moon causes Corrupt Bunnies that can in turn drop the Bunny Hood. Vile Powder can also be used to turn Bunnies into Corrupt Bunnies, though that method can't be automated.
  • In Hardmode worlds, Bunny Statues can be used during a Blood Moon to farm Soul of Light or Soul of Night by placing them within the Underground Corruption or Underground Hallow.
