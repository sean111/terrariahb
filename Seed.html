Tooltip For use with Blowpipe
This article is about seeds that can be shot with a blowpipe. For seeds that can be planted, see Seeds.

Seeds are an Ammunition item fired from the Blowpipe. They can be gathered by cutting weeds growing on grass with any tool or weapon, or Clay Pots. Seeds will only drop when your character is carrying a Blowpipe.
