This article is about seeds that can be shot with a blowpipe. For seeds that can be planted, see Seeds.

Seeds are an Ammunition item fired from the Blowpipe. They can be gathered by cutting weeds growing on grass with any tool or weapon, or Clay Pots. Seeds will only drop when your character is carrying a Blowpipe in their inventory.

History

  • 1.2: Stack limit increased from 250 to 999, damage increased from -1 to 1.
  • 1.0.5: Introduced.
