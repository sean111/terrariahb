The Flower of Fire is a Magic Weapon that launches a slow-moving fireball that bounces along the ground, being strongly affected by gravity, and ricochets up to five times before dissipating. It has a 50% chance of inflicting the On Fire! debuff. The Flower of Fire cannot be used under Water or Lava, and dissipates instantly on contact with either. It does not pierce multiple enemies. The Flower of Fire is found in Shadow Chests in The Underworld.

Notes

  • The Flower of Fire does exceptionally high damage for a pre-Hardmode weapon. Prior to Hardmode, it is second only to the Star Cannon in per-hit damage, and even rivals many high-tier Hardmode weapons in that statistic.
  • The Flower of Fire looks much like the Jungle Rose, though it's bigger and brighter.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the Super Mario Brothers franchise, in which one of the 'power-ups' includes a flower (the "Fire Flower") that gives the player white clothes and allows the player to throw bouncing fireballs that behave in a similar manner.
