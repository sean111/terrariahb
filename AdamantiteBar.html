Adamantite Bars are crafting subproducts made from 5 Adamantite Ore. It is the best tier of the three hardmode ores that the player is able to obtain.

Adamantite equipment is colored crimson red. To craft all Adamantite equipment in the PC version the player needs to acquire 144 bars, or a total of 720 ore.
