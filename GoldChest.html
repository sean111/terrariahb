The Gold Chest is a Storage item. As an item, it is identical to the standard Chest, but it can only be found in the world, and typically contains a large sum of coins or valuable items.

They are found underground (and lower), on Floating Islands and in the Dungeon. Island and Dungeon chests are always locked, and require a Golden Key to open.

If you remove every item from the chest, you can then use a Hammer to pick it up. They can be placed just like a standard Chest, and they have 20 slots of storage space (again, the same as a standard Chest).

Rarely, Golden Chests can be found in underground cabins.

As far as appearance goes, they are golden colored and have a sparkle effect, shining when exposed to a light source.[1]

Notes

Anomaly: The gold chests are common, not uncommon.
Anomaly: The gold chests may appear at surface.
Bug: If a part of the Chest is corrupted placing another Chest there will duplicate the Chest.
Bug: Golden Chests can appear as Wooden Chests to other players in a server when first placed.
Bug: If a Golden Chest has an item in it and an Active Stone Block is underneath it, a power source can turn the Active Stone Block off to drop 5 Golden Chests and vice versa with the original Golden Chest still intact. It may also be hit with a hammer for the same effect.
Bug: If you open/close a door near a golden chest, it may revert to a regular chest.
Bug: If a Golden Chest has an item in it and an Active Stone Block is underneath it and in the inactive position, a player can hold a Golden Chest and click a few pixels horizontal from the base of the chest, Golden Chest items will pop out at the rate of about 100 items per second. (A faster method than using a hammer)

Trivia

  • Even when empty, Gold Chests are immune to all explosions.
  • Golden chests can't be broken with any item inside.

History
