Pearlwood Armor has an overall defense of 8 (wearing the whole set) making it slightly better than Copper armor or Tin armor, but not as easy to access. It can be crafted at any Work Bench and requires a total of 75 Pearlwood to craft.

Notes

  • One of the only armors craftable without an Iron Anvil.
  • Like other wood armors, this has no sell value.
  • Crafting this particular armor set would seem to serve little purpose outside of vanity or collectability, as the mere presence of The Hallow in a world means that Hardmode has already been triggered, and a defense rating of 8 (eight) is woefully insufficient for the tough mobs awaiting players.

Trivia

  • The headpiece seems to have a horn on it; this is likely due to the Hallow having Unicorns in it.
