This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Rich Mahogany Hammer is a low-tier Hammer. It is identical in utility to the Copper Hammer, so a beginning player wishing to conserve metal for armor might choose to make this hammer instead, assuming they can reach the Jungle.
