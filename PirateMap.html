PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Pirate Map can be dropped by any enemy in and close to the ocean during hardmode with a 1% chance. It summons a Pirate Invasion when used, which can be used to gain the Pirate vanity set and weapons.

Anomaly: Upon using the Pirate Map, the normal summoning sound for a boss is used, as opposed to it being silent, like the Goblin Army.

Notes

  • The map can actually drop in any biome, as long as the world is in hardmode, and the player is above the surface and within 380 tiles of the edge of the map.

Farming

It can be farmed by setting up an artificial Meteorite Biome and killing all of the Meteor Heads.If the player has made a Neptune's Shell and has any kind of attacking minion (such as the Pygmy Staff), they can also simply wait underwater, allowing the minion to kill enemies as they spawn. A Water Candle and/or Battle Potion will speed up the process.
