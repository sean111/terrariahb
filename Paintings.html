Paintings

PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Paintings are non-craftable Decorative Furniture. There are currently 70 obtainable paintings, of which 14 are sold by the Painter under certain circumstances, 15 are found in underground cabins, 23 are found in the Dungeon, 12 are found in the Underworld, and 5 can be gotten from Goodie Bags.
