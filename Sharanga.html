This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console and mobile version of Terraria.

The Sharanga is a top tier Bow, which looks like a red Demon Bow. Unlike the Molten Fury, the Sharanga will turn any suitable ammo into Spectral Arrows, as opposed to Flaming Arrows. Spectral Arrows produce a glowing effect and are able to inflict the Cursed Inferno debuff. As with other ranged weapons with knockback, the best modifier for Sharanga is Unreal. Making this bow takes 10 Hellstone bars in addition to two Molten Fury bows, for a total of 60 Hellstone bars (10+25+25)

Notes

  • This bow also has a chance of dropping the Spectral arrows it uses as ammo.
  • The Cursed Inferno debuff caused by the Sharanga may be a reference to Cursed Flames.
  • On iOS the Sharanga can not be crafted due to a bug.

Trivia

  • Sharanga is the bow of the Hindu god Vishnu.
