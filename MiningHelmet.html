The Mining Helmet is an item worn in the head armor slot which produces light around the player. It can be bought from the Merchant for 8 .

This item is extremely useful when exploring dark areas, and is probably worth keeping stored somewhere even if the player obtains the Orb of Light.

Trivia

  • Undead Miners are always found wearing Mining Helmets, but never actually drop them as loot.
  • This can also be worn as a vanity item but the light doesn't appear.
  • This hides all hair styles when worn, even the beard.
  • Wearing a vanity item over the mining helmet will make it so the light appears, but the character's hairstyle remains whatever is in the vanity slot.
