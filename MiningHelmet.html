Mining armor is an armor set consisting of a Mining Helmet, Mining Shirt and Mining Pants. Mining Shirt and Mining Pants are an uncommon drop by Undead Miners. Mining Helmet can be bought from the Merchant. Despite the sets low defense, the shirt and pants can be very time-consuming to collect, sometimes making them sought after by collectors. Usually, by the time one collects the full set (if they collect the whole set), they will have armor far stronger.

The Mining Helmet itself provides light when worn in the armor slot, but not when worn in the vanity slot.

A full set grants a total of 3 defense (not a set bonus) and 20% increased mining speed.
