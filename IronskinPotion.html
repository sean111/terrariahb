Ironskin Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Ironskin. The buff increases the player's defense by 8. This lasts for 5 minutes, but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon. It is possible to get Ironskin Potions from Shadow Chests in the Underworld
