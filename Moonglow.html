Moonglow is a type of Herb which appears as a bent stalk with a drooping blue-white flower. It grows in Jungle grass (surface Jungle and Underground Jungle) and can be cut with virtually any weapon or tool. It can also be planted in Clay Pots using Moonglow Seeds.

The moonglow only blooms at night, emitting its small pearls of blue-white light, and can be cut to drop 1-3 seeds of the herb.

Anomaly: When large quantities of Moonglow are planted in pots, as in a farm, Jungle Music can be heard playing while you stand near it. It is unknown if the Music Box can record the Jungle Song from here.
