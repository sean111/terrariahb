This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Bananarang is a Hardmode boomerang only obtainable as an uncommon drop from Clowns, which only spawn during a Blood Moon. Up to 10 Bananarangs can be stacked in one slot and thrown rapidly, similarly to the Light Disc. They return to the owner once they hit an enemy, come in contact with a block, or reach the maximum range.

In contrast with the Light Disc, Bananarangs cannot be auto-thrown by holding the attack button down, do not ricochet, and do not travel as far. However, the larger stack size and low delay between throws means that a player with a quick finger can keep an immense number of Bananarangs in the air at once. This makes them very good at dealing with flying monsters, as well as keeping crowds at bay.

Bananarangs do not come with a Modifier and cannot be reforged. Using an inventory editor, the highest modifier that will stick is Godly, like all other boomerang weapons.

Trivia

  • Bananarang is a portmanteau of the words "Banana" and "Boomerang".
  • The Bananarang is a possible reference to the bananarang from the game Ape Escape.
