NPC drops

For a list of dropped items see Drops.

Most monsters will drop coins when they are killed, and many also have a chance of dropping items as well.

Soul Drops

When in the Underground Hallow, all enemies have a 1/5 chance to drop Soul of Light, and Soul of Night when killed in the Underground Corruption or Underground Crimson. Even enemies spawned from other areas will drop souls based on the current area. For example, a Chaos Elemental in the Deep Corruption will drop a Soul of Night, not a Soul of Light.

Summon Drops

Once you enter hardmode all enemies have 0.1% chance to drop a Mechanical Eye, Mechanical Worm or a Mechanical Skull if you have not yet defeated the boss summoned by that item. If you wait some time before you start killing bosses this can potentially give you multiple free summoning items.

Dungeon Key Mold Drops

When in hardmode every enemy has a 0.025% (1/4000) chance of dropping a Dungeon Key Mold. The type of Dungeon Key Mold that is dropped depends on the biome you are currently in (Jungle, Corruption, Crimson, Hallow or Snow)

Christmas Drops

During the Christmas holiday season (December 15th-31st) all enemies have 7.69% chance (1 in 13) to drop a Present.

Halloween Drops

During halloween all weak enemies (<20 defence, <40 damage and <5 silver coins loot) will have a 1 in 2000 chance of dropping a Bloody Machete. Under the same conditions they also have a 1 in 2000 chance of dropping Bladed Glove. Furthermore all enemies have a 1 in 80 chance of dropping a Goodie Bag.

KO Cannon

During a blood moon in hardmode all enemies have 0.1% chance of dropping the KO Cannon.

Coin Drops

Most monsters will typically drop a few stacks of coins after dying. Monsters that drop a large number of coins have a higher chance of dropping multiple stacks.

The total amount of money dropped is calculated using a "base value", which depends on the monster. A Skeleton for example, has a "base value" of 100 . The exact amount of money dropped is this number multiplied by 80-120%.

After the amount of money is determined, there are then a few chances for the number to be increased.

  • 20% chance of increasing by 5-10%
  • 10% chance of increasing by 10-20%
  • 6.67% chance of increasing by 15-30%
  • 5% chance of increasing by 20-40%

These increases are not mutually exclusive, and a monster may have its money increased several times. It is possible to get all increases at once, each one is rolled individually. The average amount dropped, therefore, is 119.5% of the "base" value.

History

  • 1.2.1: Halloween drops introduced & Dungeon Key drops replaced by Dungeon Key Molds.
  • 1.2: KO Cannon, Summon & Dungeon Key drops introduced.
  • 1.1.1: Christmas drops introduced.
  • 1.0.5: Enemy coin drops have been adjusted.
