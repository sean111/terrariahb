Silver Ore is an early game ore, which spawns in the Underground and Cavern biomes as well as on Floating Islands. Its primary use is to make Silver Bars, which can be used to make the Silver tier of equipment. The equivalent of Silver Ore is Tungsten Ore, which will sometimes replace Silver on a world.

Silver has a similar color and texture to stone, and the two are sometimes confused.
