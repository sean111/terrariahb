An Iron Anvil is a crafting station used mainly to forge weapons and armor. Most of its recipes require metal bars, although is also used to make some without, such as the Jungle tier of equipment. It has an alternate ore version in the Lead Anvil, which functions identically to it. It is a vital crafting station for most of the game, as it is used to make items from every tier up to Cobalt and Palladium, after which it must be replaced by a Mythril Anvil or an Orichalcum Anvil. In addition to being crafted, it can also be purchased from the Merchant.
