The Iron Axe is an early game axe, stronger than the starting Copper Axe and weaker than the Silver Axe. It has an alternate ore version, the Lead Axe, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all axes, it is used to cut down trees, cacti and giant glowing mushrooms.
