The Bell is a Hardmode crafting material, used only to craft the Fairy Bell. While the Bell can be used itself, it has no functional use, and only makes sounds. It is sold by the Wizard.

When used, the Bell generates sound effects that resemble a bell. The sound's pitch is determined by the distance between your character and the point on the screen you click on.
