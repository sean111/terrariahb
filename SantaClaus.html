Santa Claus is an NPC vendor, and arrives after the Frost Legion has been defeated. He is only available during the Christmas holiday season (December 15th-31st). After the holiday season has ended, he will no longer spawn. If he was spawned at that time, he will die instantly.You can make Santa Claus to spawn, even it isn't Christmas holiday season. All you have to do is change the time of your computer to the 15th-31st December and he will spawn. Changing the time to normal will cause him to die instantly.
