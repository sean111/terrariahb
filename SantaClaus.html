Santa Claus is an NPC vendor, and arrives after the Frost Legion has been defeated. He is only available during the Christmas holiday season (December 15th-31st). After the holiday season has ended, he will no longer spawn. If he was spawned at that time, he will die instantly.

Santa Claus can be spawned at other times by changing your computer's date setting December 15th-31st. Changing the time back to normal will cause him to die at the next sunrise.

Notes

  • In the mobile version of the game, Santa Claus is available immediately when you first get the Christmas update (released around Dec. 5)This means that you can have all the fun of winter as long as you update the game.
  • Santa Claus is actually a re-skin of the Clothier. In other words, his skin is a copy of the Clothier, but with the Santa set.
  • Santa Claus sells the most items.
Anomaly: Instead of simply disappearing, Santa Claus is slain when the holiday ends.
Bug : Occasionally Santa Claus will keep spawning on a multiplayer world, however he will die instantly.
Bug : In the iOS version, attempting to kill Santa or him getting killed by other causes will almost always crash the game.
