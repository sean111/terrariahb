PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Hammush is a Hardmode Hammer that can be bought from the Truffle NPC. When swung, the Hammush produces spinning mushrooms like that of the Mushroom Spear, which damage enemies even through walls. This, in addition to retaining the ability to smash Demon Altars, makes the Hammush a direct upgrade from the Pwnhammer.

Trivia

  • It is a reference to the web comic The Legend of Maxx. Maxx creates a tool in the Mushroom Biome made with two mushrooms and a stick, which he calls Ham-Mush.
