The Moon Charm is a Hardmode Accessory that, when equipped during a full moon, turns the player into a werewolf, and applies the Werewolf buff. The item is a rare drop from the Werewolf enemy. The resulting werewolf looks slightly different in color than the enemy. Players with this item equipped during a full moon can still be attacked by other Werewolves.

During a full moon, the buff provides these bonuses:

  • +1% Melee critical hit chance
  • +5% Melee damage
  • +5% Melee speed
  • +5% Movement speed
  • +1 Defense
  • Slightly increased jump height and jump speed

Tips

  • The Moon Charm is a reasonably good accessory for melee fighters. Non-melee characters shouldn't bother with it.

Notes

  • The Merfolk buff (granted by Neptune's Shell) overrides the Werewolf buff, replacing the graphics and cancelling the bonuses granted by the Moon Charm during a full moon.

History
