Statistics
Type Brick Crafting material

Pearlstone Bricks are bricks crafted from pearlstone and pearlsand. In large amounts they can spread The Hallow, so if you are going to make a house out of it, you should take note that if there are any adjacent dirt, grass, stone, or sand, then your house will be in the center of a hallow biome very soon.
