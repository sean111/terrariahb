The Bowl of Soup grants you the Well Fed buff upon consumption, which increases the following stats:

  • +1 defense
  • +1% critical hit chance on all attacks
  • +5% melee speed
  • +5% melee damage
  • +10% magic damage
  • +10% movement speed

The buff lasts for 10 minutes, but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon or death.
