This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Platinum Pickaxe is a fourth tier pickaxe, being an alternative to the gold pickaxe. Like all other pickaxes, it can be used to mine a majority of blocks and ores. The Platinum Pickaxe mines slightly faster than the Silver and Tungsten Pickaxes, and has a slightly longer range.
