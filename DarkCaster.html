Dark Casters are one of the six enemy types you encounter in the Dungeon. They shoot Water Spheres that go through walls.

The Water Spheres reveal the map as they travel, even while traveling through solid blocks. Therefore, Dark Casters can be used to map areas just outside the dungeon walls.

Dark Casters follow the Caster AI.

Dark Casters are immune to the Poisoned debuff.

Trivia

  • The Dark Caster appears to be wearing the Robe, which players can craft with 20 Silk.
  • If the Dark Caster were wearing the Wizard Hat, it would have the appearance of Tim.
