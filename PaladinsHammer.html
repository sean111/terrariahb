The Paladin's Hammer is a rare drop from the Paladin, who is only found in the Hardmode Dungeon. It is an extremely fast returning boomerang, which has one of the shortest ranges for a boomerang-classed weapon, but if it gets the proper equipment, this weapon can become one of the deadliest available, and with the right modifiers and equipment, can reach or exceed 220 damage. This weapon hits through enemies, proceeding until the hammer hits a solid wall or reaches the limit it can go before returning, allowing for insane accumulative damage against enemies such as the Destroyer. As with all modifiable boomerangs, the best prefix for the Paladin's Hammer is Godly.

Notes

  • Cannot autoswing
  • If standing at a certain distance (about 4 blocks) from a 1 block thick wall, the hammer may pass through
  • Increasing melee speed also increases the range of boomerangs, which is very effective with this boomerang since it is already fast. It passes through every enemy and deals really good knockback
  • It is a very good Weapon for PvP

Trivia

  • The Hammer is a common choice of weapon for paladins in many fantasy games. In most traditional cases hammers aren't throwing weapons, nor do they function like boomerangs without the aid of magic.
  • The "Flying Hammer" is most likely a nod toward "Diablo 2". The Paladin character has a magical skill which causes a hammer to swirl around. Builds centered around this ability have been coined "Hammerdins".
