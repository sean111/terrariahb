A Phaseblade is a colorful Lightsaber-like Broadsword weapon with a relatively long reach. It is crafted with 20 Meteorite Bars and 10 of any given Gem (e.g., 10 Diamonds and 20 Meteorite Bars makes a White Phaseblade). All Phaseblade colors have the same stats. Phaseblades emit a small amount of light when swung. Like other Broadswords, Phaseblades are swung over the head.

Tips

  • The different colors can be used to separate teams in player vs player games.

Trivia

  • In addition to the weapon's visual appearance, Phaseblades emit a sound effect reminiscent of a Lightsaber from the Star Wars franchise.
  • The Phaseblade's reference to Star Wars is further reinforced by the introduction of the Crystals in the Recipe to make a more powerful blade. In Star Wars, a Lightsaber is made out of specific crystals which will determine the color of the blade.
  • Furthermore, the upgraded version of the phaseblade, the phasesaber, is similar in name to "lightsaber".
