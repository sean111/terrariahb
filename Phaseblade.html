A Phaseblade is a colorful Lightsaber-like Broadsword weapon with a relatively long reach. It is crafted with 20 Meteorite Bars and 10 of any given Gem (eg. 10 Diamonds and 20 Meteorite Bars makes a White Phaseblade). All Phaseblade colors have the same stats. Phaseblades emit a small amount of light when swung. Like other Broadswords, Phaseblades are swung over the head.

Tips

  • The different colors can be used to separate teams in player vs player games.

Bugs

  • Sometimes in the Dungeon the phaseblades can glitch and can not be swung. Reselecting them after clicking another item should stop this(uncomfirmed).

Trivia

  • In addition to the weapon's visual appearence, Phaseblades emit a sound effect reminiscent of a Lightsaber from the Star Wars franchise.
  • The Phaseblade's reference to Star Wars is further enforced by the introduction of the Crystals in the Recipe to make a more powerful blade. In Star Wars, a Lightsaber is made out of specific crystals. The use of gems to create different colored blades is also a reference to the method use to create a lightsaber in Star Wars, where the color of a lightsaber's blade is dependent on the color of crystal used to create the blade.
  • Even furthermore, the upgraded version of the phaseblade, the phasesaber, is similar to "lightsaber".
