Arch Demons are found in The Underworld in the console and mobile version only. In addition to causing physical damage if contacted, they shoot accelerating purple blades at the player, similar to the Demon Scythe spell, in volleys of four. These blades are stopped by barriers such as walls and ash blocks, and have a chance of blinding the player. The blades cannot be destroyed if they are hit by a weapon, unlike the fireballs of the Fire Imp which can be destroyed.

Arch Demons are immune to water.

Trivia

  • The Arch Demon's Demon Scythe projectile can hit walls and do nothing.
  • The Arch Demon's wings are different in shape compared to normal Demon Wings.
  • The Arch Demon wears gold bracelets instead of the normal blue bracelets.
  • They may also drop the Demon Scythe upon defeat on iOS version.
