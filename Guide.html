This page is about the NPC. For Terraria Online's Guide articles, see Terraria Wiki:Projects/Guides.

The Guide is the first NPC that you encounter. He provides tips that are mainly for new players to learn the basics of the game, and can show you all crafting recipes involving an item you show him.

Unlike other NPCs, the Guide spawns on World creation, before an appropriate House is available, but will move into the first one you build, and will teleport there if he can't get there otherwise. If he is killed, he will need proper housing before he respawns.

When the Guide Voodoo Doll is equipped, the player can attack the Guide.

Players can attack and kill him with their weapons while equipped with the Guide Voodoo Doll, though he will respawn if there is a house available.

Notes

  • The Guide's crafting tips for weapons will show the adjusted damage with any bonuses for your currently equipped items, such as the Molten armor set bonus. Unequip damage adjusting items while speaking to the Guide to see base damage. This also applies to critical strike chance.
  • Sometimes the Guide just gets in the way, where a Guide Voodoo Doll is useful.
  • When the guide is dead the other NPC's know the name of the next Guide that will spawn, and will use it freely when talking about him.
  • The Guide must be alive to summon the Wall of Flesh, otherwise, the Wall of Flesh will not spawn.
  • The Guide is very important in the game, and pretty mysterious. He appears out of nowhere when the Player first starts, giving him tips and apparently knowing about everything that is happening on the Map (including things like the presence of a tied Wizard, or his knowledge on the Corruption), and is the NPC that is represented on the Voodoo Doll. Finally, he must be killed in order to summon the Wall of Flesh and proceed into Hardmode
Bug: The Guide only suggests you to fight the Eye of Cthulhu when you have Fallen Stars in your inventory, while he should suggest it then you have Lenses/Suspicious Looking Eyes in your inventory.
Bug: He says that crafting a dirt block into a mud block does not need a crafting station when it needs water in the crafting.
Bug: The Guide sometimes won't respawn after summoning the Wall of Flesh.
Bug: He says "You wil want to increase ..." while it should be "You will want to increase'..."
