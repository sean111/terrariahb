Flaming Arrows are a type of Arrow. They are an upgrade to the Wooden Arrow, doing slightly more damage, with a chance to set enemies on fire. They are easy to craft, although they require Torches, which are used for many other things in the early game. Still, they are useful to player's who have not yet acquired the materials to make large amounts of Unholy Arrows and Jester's Arrows. They are also useful if you have large amounts of them and have no torches while in a dark cave, as they provide light when fired.

Flaming Arrows emit light when fired or dropped, making them useful for temporary lighting in a cave. However, they will not emit light if fired into water. Wooden Arrows fired from the Molten Fury, if recovered, have a 1 in 3 chance to come back as Flaming Arrows.
