This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Orichalcum Drill is an alternative to the Orichalcum Pickaxe. Appearances and sounds aside, the two tools are functionally identical. However, unlike pickaxes, most drills cannot be reforged with size, speed, or knockback modifiers, making Demonic the best possible prefix. Drax is the one exception, as it can be reforged with knockback, allowing the Godly prefix.

Can mine any block except Chlorophyte Ore and Lihzahrd Bricks.
