A Bottle is a crafting material required to make most potions. Along with the Pink Vase and the Mug, placing it (usually on a Table, a Work Bench, or a Platform) allows it to act as a crafting station where the player can craft potions. Bottles can also be 'filled' when standing near water or honey, turning them into Bottled Water or Bottled Honey.
