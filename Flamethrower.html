The Flamethrower is a weapon that uses Gel for ammunition. It fires similarly to the Aqua Scepter but with fire, and each gel makes more lines of fire. It inflicts the On Fire! debuff to enemies.

Tips

  • Because this weapon uses plenty of Gel, one could make a slime grinder using Slime Statues to farm easily for Gel.
  • Flamethrowers are exceptional for bosses; the most effective being The Destroyer because the flamethrower can hit multiple segments at once.

Notes

Anomaly: While the Flamethrower cannot shoot from under/into water, it can shoot from within/into lava.
