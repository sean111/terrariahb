PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

If your world has Lead Ore as an alternative to Iron Ore, then you can either craft a lead anvil, or buy an iron anvil from the merchant for 50 . The specifications are identical to the iron anvil with the exception of a darker tint. In other words, it does everything that an Iron Anvil can make and craft. The anvil is used to craft things out of metal bars and it's supplements and whenever you see a recipe that has an Iron Anvil as it's crafting station, then you could also do it at this anvil.

Trivia

In reality, a lead anvil would not keep its structure very long, due to the low Mohs hardness (1.5) and melting point (600 K) of pure lead.
