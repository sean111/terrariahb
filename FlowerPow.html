The Flower Pow is a Hardmode Flail that is occasionally dropped by Plantera (16.67% chance, or 1 in 6). In addition to the usual flail mechanics, it has the unique ability to shoot petals toward enemies. It will launch the petals at a steady rapid pace for as long as the fire button is held and there are targets to shoot at. It is heavily affected by gravity, but holding the mouse can allow it to be held and dragged.

Like all Flails, the best Modifier is Godly.

Notes

  • The petals that the Flower Pow shoots are classified as ranged damage, rather than actual melee damage. It is not known whether it is affected by any Shroomite type helmets.
  • When pressed against a 1 block wide wall, a player can launch their Flower Pow through the wall, making it helpful for destroying enemies like the Paladin while sitting in a box.

Trivia

  • Its name is a reference to the motto "Flower Power".
  • The flower in the icon somewhat resembles the Weighted Companion Cube from Portal.
