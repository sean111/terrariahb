This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Platinum Bow is one of the stronger pre-hardmode bow you can get. It has a base damage of 13, one less damage than the Demon Bow and 5 less than the Tendon Bow. It is crafted from 7 Platinum bars and is relatively easy to get early on. Due to its easy requirements, it is recommended to get this until you have a better ranged weapon.
