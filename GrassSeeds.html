Statistics
Type Seed

Buy/Sell 20/4
Grass Seeds are used to plant Grass on Dirt Blocks. They are sold by the Dryad. Grass Seeds can be used to start Tree and Mushroom farms.

This item becomes obsolete once the player finds a Staff of Regrowth as it has the same effect for free and with unlimited uses.
