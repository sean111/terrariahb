This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Lime Kelp is found underwater underground and in the cavern layer. It is most common in the Snow Biome, and spawns very rarely in the regular underground and cavern layer. Lime Kelp has a tendency to blend in with the background thanks to the water, so locating them may prove difficult.
