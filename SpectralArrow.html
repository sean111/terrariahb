This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console and mobile version of Terraria.

Spectral Arrows are created from firing a Sharanga, any suitable ammo turning into a Spectral Arrow. They produce a glowing effect and have the ability to inflict the Cursed Inferno debuff to enemies. Like other arrows, they have a small chance of not breaking after being fired. It can no longer be crafted in iOS.

Notes

  • The drop rate from the Sharanga is 25%.
  • These arrows are the strongest arrows in the game as of now based on highest impact damage and the cursed inferno debuff it causes. Though getting large amounts requires shooting the Sharanga and hoping it gives you a large amount to recover. On the up side using wooden arrows isn't that big of a loss.

History

  • Console-Release: Introduced.
