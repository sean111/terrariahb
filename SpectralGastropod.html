The Spectral Gastropod is a console exclusive flying Hardmode enemy that appears frequently at the surface of The Hallow at night, and fires lasers. They resemble Gastropods but deal more damage.

Spectral Gastropods are immune to the Confused debuff.

Notes

  • Spectral Gastropods will enter water to chase the player, and can continue flying and firing while submerged.
  • Striking the Spectral Gastropod while it is preparing its laser will interrupt the attack and prevent it from firing.

History

  • Console-Release: Introduced.
