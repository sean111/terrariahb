This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Amethyst Robe is a robe that reduces mana usage by 5%, and increases your maximum mana by 20. It requires 10 Amethyst and a basic Robe to craft at a loom.

Wearing a Wizard Hat alongside the Amethyst Robe provides a set bonus of +10% magic critical strike chance. Any leg-slot equipment can be worn with the set bonus intact.
