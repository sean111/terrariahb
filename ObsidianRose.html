This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Obsidian Rose is an accessory that reduces lava damage by approximately half. It is rarely dropped by Fire Imps. Despite the name, it is not crafted from Obsidian.

Tips

If you have access to a Lava Charm, it is suggested to use that in place of the Obsidian Rose, as the Lava Charm allows temporary immunity to lava, rather than decreased damage.
