Gravitation Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Gravitation. This buff allows the player to invert gravity with the up and down keys (W and S by default), making the character appear upside-down, fall upwards, and walk on ceilings. The effect can be toggled while in midair.

The effect lasts for 3 minutes, but can be canceled at any time by right-clicking the buff icon.

Gravitation in 1.2

The gravitation effect was changed greatly with the 1.2 update. From 1.2 onward, instead of the player flipping upside-down, the player stays put on the screen, and the view of the world flips upside-down. The down key no longer returns gravity to normal; instead, the up key does this. Relative to the flipped view, other entities appear to fall upward.The minimap is not flipped.

Notes

  • While flipping gravity resets your fall distance, this potion does not negate fall damage on its own. Featherfall potions, Angel/Demon wings, a Lucky Horseshoe or an Obsidian Horseshoe will make using Gravitation potions a lot safer.
  • Falling upwards to the map's height limit will not damage you, but it is possible to die by smacking into the underside of a Floating Island, or any other ceiling.
  • Falling to the map's height limit will allow you to move left and right along its edge, but you will still be considered "falling". Flight time for Wings or Rocket Boots will not be reset.
  • The sound effect of the player flipping gravity has the same sound effect of the Shadow Orb (item), Vilethorn, and Demon Scythe as well as when a Fire Imp teleports.
Anomaly 1.2.1.2: The player can not land or walk on Wood Platforms when gravity is reversed.
Bug 1.2.1.2: The player's head seems squashed while wearing a Wizard Hat when moving in the upside down view.
Bug 1.2.1.2: When the player moves the cursor over a NPC with the view upside down, the speech bubble is misplaced.
Bug 1.2.1.2: The player can not jump while on a solid block underwater when gravity is reversed.
Bug 1.2.1.2: Flight time can be refreshed while still in the air. By running and falling down (or flying up on the player's view), flight time can be reset indefinitely.
Bug 1.2.1.2: Using Gravitation Potions on hammered platforms will make the player rapidly (one frame of animation) "fall" and land.
Bug 1.2.1.2: Attempting to move an NPC while upside down will tell the player the following message: This is not valid housing.

Tips

  • It is helpful for finding Floating Islands by hovering up and down at the right altitude.
  • Gravitation effectively surpasses the Rocket Boots and Wings combination as the air travel method with the most range, though it is more difficult to control as a flight method.

History

  • 1.2: The Gravitation effect now flips the view of the world, rather than the character's sprite.
  • 1.0.5: Introduced.
