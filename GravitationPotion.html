Gravitation Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Gravitation. This buff allows the player to invert gravity with the up and down keys, making them walk on the ceiling (W and S by default). The effect is player-controlled and can be toggled while in the air, surpassing the Rocket Boots and Wings conbination as the most effective way of air travel currently available. This lasts for 3 minutes, but can be canceled at any time by right-clicking the icon.

It is also helpful for finding Floating Islands by hovering up and down at the right altitude.

Notes

  • While flipping gravity resets your fall distance, this potion does not negate fall damage on its own. Featherfall potions,Angel/Demon wings, or a Lucky Horseshoe will make using Gravitation potions a lot safer.
  • Falling upwards to the map's height limit will not damage you, but it is possible to die by smacking into the underside of a Floating Island, or any other ceiling.
