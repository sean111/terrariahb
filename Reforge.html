Reforge

Reforging is a service provided by the Goblin Tinkerer to add, or randomize existing, mods to a base item. Reforging allows the player to "reroll" new modifiers without having to find or craft more items.

Important: Don't reforge in German/Italian/French/Spanish versions, there is a bug which deletes your item!

Accessory modifiers will grant a bonus to one of a player's stats, such as defense, movement speed, or max mana, which is applied as long as it is equipped. Weapon modifiers alter the stats of that particular weapon and may be positive or negative, with up to 5 positive or 3 negative bonuses at once. Modifiers can raise or lower the tier (rarity) of an item by up to 2 tiers, based on how good or bad the modifiers are. The displayed stats may vary depending on the item's base values, which are multiplied with a fixed value and then rounded. For example, the Murderous prefix on a Handgun will display a 7% damage increase, while a Magic Missile will display a 9% increase.

The process is expensive, and is probably intended as a "gold sink." If the item is crafted, one means of generating new mods is to rebuild the item; that may not be practical, hence the "Reforge" option on the Tinkerer. Currently, the Goblin Tinkerer charges the item's current value (5 times the sell value). A modifier will raise or lower the value of an item, so items with poor modifiers are cheaper to reforge, while reforging an already well-modified item will be more expensive.

It should be noted that an item with mods can be used in the crafting of other items if it is marked as a material. Doing so does not preserve the mods; an Enchanted Boomerang's mods are lost when used to create a Flamarang, which will get new mods randomly assigned upon creation. Thus, if you have a weapon or accessory that already has useful mods, you may wish to consider finding another of the same to use in crafting.

There are 112 different reforge modifications in total at the moment.

Weapon Mods

These mods only apply when the weapon is being used. Weapons that have no Knockback cannot be reforged with mods that change Knockback, such as Drills, Magical Harp and others.

The Light Disc cannot have modifiers.

Flails, Spears, Boomerangs (except for the Light Disc), Chainsaws and Drills (including the Hamdrax) can only have universal prefixes (because they can't have their speed modified). The best prefix for a Drill without knockback is Demonic. Universal mods can also be found on all other weapon types. The best universal prefix is Godly.
