This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Tin Chandelier is a piece of Furniture that can be placed on ceilings. It is the equivalent of a copper chandelier for worlds generated with Tin Ore. It functions the same as other chandeliers. It can sometimes be found unlit in underground cabins. It is a valid light source for NPCs.
