The Death Sickle is a melee weapon that is dropped by Reapers during a Solar Eclipse. Like most melee weapons of its tier, it can shoot projectiles. The 'deathly sickle' it shoots has a fair range, passes through blocks, does a slight knockback and leaves a fair amount of light. The Death Sickle can be swung automatically by holding down the attack button, similar to the Excalibur, Muramasa, Cobalt Sword and Phasesaber.

This weapon is excellent for mining and fighting as the 'sickles' hover for a second or two when they reach maximum range. The scythes emitted from the sickle can potentially hit enemies multiple times, unlike the Terra Blade. The scythe's projectiles also have no recharge time, again, unlike the Terra Blade. The projectiles prove to be very effective against worm enemies like The Destroyer, or crowd control, such as during a Blood Moon or Solar Eclipse. For the stats it puts out, its continuous swinging abilities and the fact that the projectiles can penetrate both ground and enemy alike, it is easily one of the best melee weapons you can obtain, and is very useful even throughout late hardmode.

Notes

  • The projectiles from the Death Sickle can hit targets six times before disappearing.
  • The best prefix this weapon can obtain, like most other melee weapons, is "Legendary".
  • Monsters killed with this have a greater chance of dropping souls. It is recommended you use this when farming souls.
  • It should be considered as a mining weapon, as this weapon's projectiles light up slightly, and will reveal caves if shot through walls.
  • Using Fire Gauntlet or Magma Stone which will cause this weapon to inflict fire damage on attack makes its projectiles light up a lot. This combination makes this weapon very good at clearing black spots on the map.
  • The sickle's projectiles travel further if the player equips glove-type accessories such as the Mechanical Glove, Fire Gauntlet, Feral Claws, and Power Glove. With no gloves equipped, the projectiles will fly ~12 blocks before dissipating; all four extend the range to ~20 blocks. With a pair of Frost Leggings and another non-glove accessory reforged to Violent, the projectiles can reach ~24 blocks in distance away from the player.
Bug : In Multiplayer, only the user will see, and hear, the sickle swing repeatedly; other players will see it remain at the end of its animation cycle while in continuous use and will only hear it swing once.

Trivia

  • Despite its name, it resembles a scythe, which is a specific type of sickle. These are used for harvesting grains and other crops.
  • It is comparable (both in name and in use) to the Demon Scythe, this being the less ranged, more powerful version which uses no mana.
  • The Death Sickle's projectiles are similar to main attack of the Grim Reaper in the Castlevania series.
