The Harpy is a flying enemy that spawns at high altitudes, typically above the height of Floating Island. It uses the Bat AI, and fires Harpy Feathers ().

Harpies can spawn at any time, day or night. Up to 7 Harpies can exist on screen during the day, 10 at night, and 15 during a Blood Moon.

The red areas in the sky marks the locations that Harpies can be found in

Notes

Anomaly: They don't make a splash sound, but still enter the water.
Bug: Sometimes a harpy will spawn very close to the ground (around 40 feet above the ground)

If standing on a tall mountain, it is possible for Harpys to spawn and attack you.
