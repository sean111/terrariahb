Shurikens are thrown consumable weapons that can pierce several enemies. Shurikens have a 50% chance of being retrievable again after use, even if they've hit enemies. Shurikens can be found in pots and chests around the world, and can be purchased from the Merchant. Since it is easy to obtain Shurikens, they are an effective early game ranged weapon, and are very effective against the Eye of Cthulhu.

Notes

  • Like Boomerangs, Shurikens can be thrown through single-block-thick walls, at certain angles.

History
