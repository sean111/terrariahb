Shurikens are thrown consumable weapons that can pierce enemies. Shurikens have a 50% chance of being retrievable again after use, even if they've hit enemies. Shurikens can be found in pots and chests around the world, and can be purchased from the Merchant.

Shurikens are greatly affected by gravity, and arc similarly to Throwing Knives and Bones. Of the three consumable throwing weapons, they have a range slightly better than Bones, but slightly less than Throwing Knives.

Notes

  • Like Boomerangs, Shurikens can be thrown through single-block-thick walls, at certain angles.

Tips

  • Effective against the Eye of Cthulhu.
