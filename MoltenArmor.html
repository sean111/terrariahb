Molten armor has the highest defense out of all the pre-hardmode armors in the game, but it is also the most difficult to obtain.

It takes a total of 45 Hellstone Bars to craft a full set of Molten armor. This means you need 135 Hellstone and 45 Obsidian to make a whole set.

It consists of a Molten Helmet, Molten Breastplate, and Molten Greaves.

A full set grants 25 base defense, plus 17% melee damage bonus. If set is visible, it will leave a fire trail, the same as Meteor armor.

Notes

  • The Molten Breastplate has a marking on it that strongly resembles a cross on male characters. This is not visible when worn on female characters.
