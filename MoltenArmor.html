Molten armor was the highest defense armor in the game, and also the most difficult to obtain, until the introduction of several sets of armor only obtainable in hardmode, such as Adamantite armor.

It takes a total of 90 Hellstone Bars to craft a full set of Molten armor. This means you need 360 Hellstone and 90 Obsidian to make a whole set.

It consists of a Molten Helmet, Molten Breastplate, and Molten Greaves.

A full set grants 25 base defense, plus a 17% melee damage bonus.

History

  • 1.1:
    • No longer requires Gold armor to be crafted.
    • Hellstone Bar requirement reduced by 5 for each piece, bringing set cost from 105 down to 90 bars.
  • 1.0.6:
    • Set bonus damage increased from 15% to 17%.
    • Set bonus light effect while moving removed.
  • 1.0.5:
    • Now requires Gold armor to be crafted.
    • Defense for each item increased by 1.
    • Set bonus changed from 5 defense to +15% damage and light effect while moving.
  • 1.0.3:
    • Defense for each item reduced by 2.
    • Set bonus reduced from 10 to 5 defense.
    • Armor graphics changed.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
