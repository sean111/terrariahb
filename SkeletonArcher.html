The Skeleton Archer is a hardmode enemy that spawns in the Cavern layer and shoots Flaming Arrows at the player (but does not drop any of them). Their attacks have a chance of inflicting the On Fire! effect.

Notes

  • Several simple pit traps (7 blocks deep) can be very effective against such enemies as Skeleton Archers and Armored Skeletons.
