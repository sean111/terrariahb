For strategies on how to gather Souls, see Guide:Harvesting Souls.

Souls of Blight are dropped by the console-exclusive boss Ocram. They are used to create any console-exclusive weapon and armor items with the exception of Sharanga. Creating all weapons and armor sets costs 225 souls, meaning you must defeat Ocram a total of 23-45 times.

Notes

Bug : Souls of Blight fall to the ground and are not lit up, unlike other souls.

Trivia

  • The "Blight" in Soul of Blight means any cause of impairment, destruction, ruin, or frustration.
  • When you defeat Ocram, he drops 1 piece of either Dragon, Titan, or Spectral armor sets, so you may not have to collect as many Souls of Blight to get all the armor sets.
