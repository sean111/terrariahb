Statistics

Tooltip Increases length of invincibility after taking damage
Sell 3

The Cross Necklace is an accessory that increases the duration of the brief invincibility after taking damage from 2/3 of a second to 1 1/3 seconds. It has no effect in PvP. It is dropped by mimics. It is useful in fights where the player gets hit in rapid succession, such as against The Destroyer.

Trivia

This may be a reference to the first Castlevania, having nearly the same image and making you invincible for a short duration.
