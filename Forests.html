Forests

Forests comprise the majority of the surface world, and are where the player initially spawns. The landscape of a forest includes lots of grass, plenty of trees, several lakes ranging from small to large and, sometimes, Sunflowers. Dayblooms and Pumpkins can be found growing in this biome. There is a small chance for low tier ores to spawn on the surface in a forest.

The background of forests are randomly generated upon world creation.

During hardmode, regular Forests can be erased by the spread of the Hallow or Corruption.

Spawn Rates

  • During the day:
    • 100% spawning speed
    • 4~ max spawns
  • During the night:
    • 166% spawning speed
    • 5~ max spawns
  • During a Blood Moon:
    • 333% spawning speed
    • 7~ max spawns
  • Having town NPCs can lower spawn rates in this area. (Blood Moon is an exception however.)
