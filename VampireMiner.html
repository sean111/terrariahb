Vampire Miners appear in cavern-level biomes and follow the Fighter AI. They wear (but do not drop) a Mining Helmet, which provides a small amount of light around them. This light can sometimes reveal sealed caves, much like blooming Blinkroot. Vampire miners can break down doors.

Vampire Miners are immune to the Poisoned debuff.

Notes

  • Vampire Miners are console AND mobile exclusive stronger versions of Undead Miners, which seem to appear more often than their cousins. (?? -- confirmed on higher spawn rate?)
