This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Titanium Bars are crafted from 5 Titanium Ore. It is used in the crafting of the Titanium Forge, thus, making it equivalent to Adamantite.

Crafting all Titanium equipment requires 174 Titanium Bars, or a total of 870 Titanium Ore.
