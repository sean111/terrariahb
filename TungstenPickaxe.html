This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Tungsten Pickaxe is an early-game Pickaxe, being an alternative of the Silver Pickaxe. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine a majority of blocks and ores. The Tungsten Pickaxe has a slightly longer attack range than the Iron/Lead Pickaxe, and mines blocks at a faster speed.

For a list of modifiers, see Reforging.

Notes

Anomaly: For some reason the Tungsten Pickaxe can mine Meteorite even though it is the alternative of the Silver Pickaxe, which is not able to mine meteorite. This means that if you get Tungsten Ore in your world instead of Silver Ore, you can skip getting a Gold Pickaxe or Platinum Pickaxe to mine Meteorite.
