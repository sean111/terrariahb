The Silver Hammer is an early game hammer, being stronger than the Iron Hammer, and weaker than the Gold Hammer. It has an alternate ore version, the Tungsten Hammer, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all hammers, it is used to remove background walls and create sloped tiles and half tiles.
