This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console and mobile version of Terraria.
Dragon Hornets are powerful flying enemies found only in the Underground Jungle. They follow the standard Flying AI, floating just out of reach and then charging the player, while also firing stingers which have a 1 in 3 chance to poison the player for 10 seconds. These projectiles can NOT be destroyed with weapons and travel at about the same speed as the darts from a Dart Trap.

Dragon Hornets are in many ways overpowered since they don't drop stingers, have a lot of health and spawn frequently in the Underground Jungles

Notes

  • Dragon Hornets are slightly tougher console exclusive variants of Hornets, that do not drop stingers. Compared to normal hornets they are slightly stronger and are somewhat green.
  • Dragon Hornets are immune to the Poisoned debuff.
  • They are somewhat similar to Moss Hornets of the PC version, but fire stingers slower and appear darker.
