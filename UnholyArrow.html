Unholy Arrows are a type of Arrow. Compared to the Wooden Arrows used to craft them, they are stronger and have a piercing effect, allowing each arrow to penetrate up to three monsters. Against multi-section monsters, a single Unholy Arrow can damage multiple sections. A fired Unholy Arrow has a 25% chance of being recoverable after hitting an enemy or a wall.

In addition to being crafted, Unholy Arrows are dropped by the Eye of Cthulhu, but only on worlds that generated with Corruption. After defeating the Eater of Worlds, the Arms Dealer begins selling Unholy Arrows at night. After activating hardmode, they become available during the day as well.
