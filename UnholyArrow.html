Unholy Arrows are arrows that have a piercing effect, allowing them to penetrate five monsters with a total damage of up to 48 per arrow. Jester's Arrows have a similar effect. Against multi-section monsters, a single Unholy Arrow can damage multiple sections. Once they are fired, Unholy Arrows have a 1/4 chance of dropping and can be recovered for reuse.

Unholy Arrows can be crafted from Wooden Arrows and Worm Teeth at an Iron Anvil. They are also dropped by the Eye of Cthulhu and can be found (rarely) in chests underground. If you have defeated the Eater of Worlds, Unholy Arrows can be purchased at night from the Arms Dealer for 40 copper each, and at any time in hardmode. In hardmode, Unholy Arrows replace Wooden Arrows that drop from pots.
