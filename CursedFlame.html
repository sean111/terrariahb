This article is about the crafting material. For the magic weapon, see Cursed Flames.

The Cursed Flame is a crafting material. It is found by killing World Feeders and Clingers, both of which can be found in the Underground Corruption.

Notes

  • While important as a crafting recipe for Cursed Flames (magic weapon) and Flamethrower, cursed flame can be used for making Cursed Torches, Cursed Arrows, and Cursed Bullets. Since these are all consumeable items, having a large collection is important for exploring caves and using any ranged non-magic weapons.
