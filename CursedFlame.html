This article is about the crafting material. For the magic weapon, see Cursed Flames.

The Cursed Flame is a Hardmode crafting material. It is found by killing World Feeders and Clingers, both of which can be found in the Underground Corruption.

While important as a crafting material for the Cursed Flames (magic weapon) and Flamethrower, Cursed Flame can also be used for making Cursed Torches, Cursed Arrows, and Cursed Bullets. Since these are all consumable items, having a steady supply is important for exploring caves and using many ranged ammo-based weapons.
