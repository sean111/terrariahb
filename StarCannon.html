The Star Cannon is an upgraded version of the Minishark that shoots Fallen Stars. The Star Cannon deals 62 damage per shot, making it one of the most powerful weapons in the game.

For finding ammunition, the player may regularly pick up Fallen Stars even after maxing their mana. A skybridge can be used to farm them efficiently.

The Star Cannon launches Fallen Stars at a speed of 52.5 blocks or 105 feet per second. A star survives exactly 1 minute after being launched, which means it can travel 3150 blocks or 6300 feet. After the star reaches this point or upon striking a solid block, it will explode with a firework animation, very similar to the Magic Missile.

Despite the additions of the Megashark and the Shotgun, even with the new ammo, the Star Cannon remains one of the most powerful Ranged Weapons for sheer Damage per Second, being beaten by the Candy Corn Rifle, the Megashark with Chlorophyte Bullets and Shroomite Armor, and the SDMG.

The Star Cannon cannot be reforged with any modifiers that affect knockback, as it has none. The best modifier prefix for a ranged weapon without knockback is Demonic.

Notes

  • The Star Cannon is a good weapon for boss fights and PvP since the increase of firing speed from very slow to very fast in the 1.1 Terraria Update. Some multiplayer servers ban this item for this reason.
  • The Megashark can easily surpass the Star Cannon's DPS while using Chlorophyte Bullets and Shroomite Armor. However, the components listed above are not a straight upgrade to the Star Cannon, seeing as the Star Cannon can pierce through enemies, or segments of worm type enemies, causing the Star Cannon to be extremely useful.
  • The Star Cannon can be obtained by opening presents (mobile version).
Bug : Like Boomerangs, it is possible to shoot stars through walls. In this case, the walls can be up to three blocks thick.
Bug : When reforging the Star Cannon to Demonic and equip Hallowed Helmet, Adamantite Breastplate, Hallowed Greaves and 5 Menacing accessories (of which one a Ranger Emblem), you will do over 100 damage, causing the game to think the projectiles are Fallen Stars, meaning they won't disappear and can be picked up again (during nighttime).

History

  • 1.2: Damage increased from 55 to 62.
  • 1.1.1: Speed slightly decreased but still Very Fast, Damage reduced further from 60 to 55.
  • 1.1: Speed increased to Very Fast.
  • 1.0.5: Damage reduced from 75 to 60, rate of fire drastically reduced to Very Slow.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
