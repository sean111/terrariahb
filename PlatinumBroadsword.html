PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Platinum Broadsword is made using an Iron Anvil or Lead Anvil with 8 Platinum Bars. Like all broadswords, this sword has an arc motion, which is more useful for killing flying and jumping monsters than short swords.
