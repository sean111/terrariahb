This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Orichalcum Bars are crafted from 4 Orichalcum Ore. They are necessary to make the Orichalcum Anvil, which is needed to create most high-end equipment, and is the equivalent to the Mythril Anvil, thus, making Orichalum equivalent to Mythril.

Crafting all Orichalcum equipment, including the anvil, requires 155 Orichalcum Bars, or a total of 620 Orichalcum Ore. Without the anvil, you will need 145 bars, or 580 ores.
