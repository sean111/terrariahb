The Silver Broadsword is the third-strongest craftable non-hardmode metal broadsword in the game, behind the Gold Broadsword by 2 damage. It is made using an Iron Anvil with 8 Silver Bars. Like all broadswords, this sword has an arc motion, which is more useful for killing flying and jumping monsters than short swords.
