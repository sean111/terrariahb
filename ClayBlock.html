Clay Block

Clay Block
Statistics
Type Block Crafting material
Max stack 999
Use time 14

Clay blocks are needed to make Clay Pots, Red Bricks, Bowls, and Pink Vases. Clay is found primarily in surface-level (above 0 elevation) areas, and is more rare at the upper parts of the underground. Because of this, the largest deposits of clay can be found simply by exploring above ground for the differently-colored dirt.

Notes

  • Sometimes, Clay can be a natural blocker of the Corruption, Crimson or Hallow. All 3 biomes cannot spread through clay and can thus make a natural barrier against spreading.
