This tool functions as both a Hammer and an Axe. It is the most powerful Hammer before hardmode, and the most powerful Axe in the game discluding The Axe with 175% axe power. It bests all of the Chainsaws -- even the legendary Drax's 110% axe power. It is crafted with Hellstone Bars. This tool has a chance of burning enemies when it hits them. This tool is an upgraded version of the Meteor Hamaxe.

Notes

Although it is slightly slower than the Drax, the Molten Hamaxe is capable of getting the *light* modifier, unlike the Drax. This will push the Molten Hamaxe's speed so it can break trees just about as quickly as the Drax. This ability can prove to be a very effective substitute for a Drax, from pre-hardmode onward.

History

  • 1.0.6:

  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
