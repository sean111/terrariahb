This tool functions as both a Hammer and an Axe. It is the most powerful Hammer before hardmode, and the most powerful Axe in the game. It bests all of the Chainsaws -- even the legendary Hamdrax's 110% axe power. It is crafted with Hellstone Bars. This tool has a chance of burning enemies when it hits them.
