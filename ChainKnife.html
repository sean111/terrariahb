This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Chain Knife is a new melee weapon introduced in version 1.2. It can be found from Cave Bats and Skeletons as a rare drop, and from chests. The Chain Knife has a very short range of only twelve blocks. However, its quick use time and velocity makes it a very excellent early to mid-game weapon. It is good at killing quick flying monsters. It is also good at killing large groups of wall creepers.

It is similar to flails and the harpoon weapon in the fact that while the weapon is retracting to the player it can deal additional damage to monsters caught in its path.

Notes

  • The best Modifier for this weapon is "Godly".
Bug 1.2.2: When aim and fired at a single waist-high block, it appears to make a small sphere around you that causes damage to enemies two to three blocks away.

Trivia

  • Might be a reference to Scorpion from Mortal Kombat and his signature hook attack (rope with a dagger on the end).
