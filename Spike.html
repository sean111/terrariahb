Spikes are blocks found in a Dungeon. They deal 40 damage to the player on contact. They can be collected by using a hammer or explosives, then placed for use as a PvP barricade or used to create elaborate decorations. They are one of the few items that are immune to Lava, making useful traps possible.

Notes

  • Spikes seem to deal no damage to monsters and friendly NPCs.

History

  • 1.1: Bombs can destroy them.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
