PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Web-Covered Chests can be found in Spider Nests.

Loot

Web-Covered Chests have a 100% chance of containing a Web Slinger, and normal loot of other chests found in the layer (Underground or Caverns) they spawn in.

Bug : Sometimes, it will be replaced with a water chest.
Anomaly: It is impossible to be mined, even with Dynamite or a Picksaw.

History

  • 1.2: Introduced.
