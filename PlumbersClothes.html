The Plumber's clothes is a set of vanity items made up of the Plumber's Hat, the Plumber's Shirt, and the Plumber's Pants. The Plumber's Hat is very rarely dropped by Fire Imps, at only a 1 in 500 (0.2%) drop rate. The shirt and pants can be purchased from the Clothier for 25 each during a full moon.

These items are equipped in the Social slots as they do not provide anything but a change in visual appearance of the character.

Trivia

  • This set of vanity clothes is a reference to the most well-known video game character, Mario, and a reference to Andrew "Redigit" Spink's previous fangame Super Mario Bros. X.
  • The fact that you can only purchase the Plumber gear on the night of a full moon is likely due to the old stereotype of the 'Plumber's Crack'.
