A Wire Cutter is an accessory that is shaped like a cutting wrench. Having the Wire Cutter equipped shows the location of Wires on screen (including those that you did not place). As the name suggests, the Wire Cutter allows you to 'cut' and gather wires for use elsewhere. It also allows you to remove placed actuators.

It is sold by the Mechanic (who is initially found in the Dungeon).

Tips

  • Since the wires are expensive (buying 1 cost 5 silver, meaning 20 cost 1 gold, and 2000 cost a platinum), it is useful to take the wire cutter into caves to harvest the components of traps. You might also find dart traps, boulders (although they cannot be harvested), active and inactive stone blocks, etc.
