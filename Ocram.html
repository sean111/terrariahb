Ocram is an exclusive Boss in the Console Version of Terraria. He is summoned with the Suspicious Looking Skull at Night. As of the 1.2 release, Ocram is the most difficult boss in the console game.

Attacks

Ocram's attack style is very similar to that of Eye of Cthulhu. Like the Eye of Cthulhu, it charges repeatedly at the player while summoning Servants of Ocram (which act similarly to Servants of Cthulhu). Additionally, it fires numerous red laser beams. After its health is halved, it will, like several other bosses, switch into its second form. However, it will still continue to summon servants, unlike the Eye of Cthulhu. It will also constantly charge at the player(s), and the laser changes to a more damaging blue laser, with accompanying Demon Scythes.

When Ocram is summoned you will get a message saying "Ocram has awoken!"

Notes

  • Ocram can only be summoned on a Hardmode world. Attempting to use the Suspicious Looking Skull on a world not in Hardmode will do nothing.
Anomaly: Sometimes your attacks will not work, as his hitbox is a bit inaccurate, but aiming for the eyes will hurt him for sure.

Tips

  • Crystal Bullets with an Unreal Megashark are recommended for the fight against Ocram, as his servants do not have much health.
  • Melee is not recommended for the fight, due to you being barely able to touch him. However, it is effective if you can dodge the servants of Ocram and repeatedly melee attack. Use an Excalibur or Tizona, and have powerful armor. If you need a little extra range use the Gungnir or the Tonbogiri to hit Ocram while he is above or below you. For his servants use the light disk to keep them at bay. Simply grapple yourself to a damaging block such as hellstone or meteorite, and place heart statue close by on a 1 second timer, equip the cross necklace and do not have the obsidian skull or the obsidian shield equipped, as this would take away the benefit from receiving the damage from the hellstone or meteorite. Note: this is an effective method of dealing with most bosses in the console version.Cursed bullets will also be good to have because it can lit Ocram with cursed flames
  • By far, the easiest way to farm Ocram is to grapple yourself to hellstone or meteorite ore, but using a cobalt shield (dungeon item) can also work. Have a Rainbow Rod, Magical harp and Mana Flower with potions, and not have obsidian items equipped. The Rainbow Rod deals tons of damage to Ocram and the Magical Harp obliterates his servants.
  • only considering the fact that Ocram has high damage, Ocram is not very hard with the right kind of equipment.

Trivia

  • Reading backwards Ocram you get Marco, the name of Ocram's programmer.
  • Ocram seems to be very Zerg-like in appearance, possibly a reference to the Overlord, Corruptor, or Brood Lord from the StarCraft universe.
  • Ocram also seems to resemble the unmasked heads of Yautja from the Predator franchise.
  • Ocram is currently the only boss exclusive to the console version.
  • Ocram's shape is reminiscent of the alien invader from the Space Invaders franchise.
