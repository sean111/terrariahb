Ocram is an exclusive Boss in the Console Version of Terraria. He is summoned with the Suspicious Looking Skull at Night. As of the console release, Ocram is the most difficult Boss in the game.

Attacks

Ocram's attack style is very similar to that of Eye of Cthulhu. Like the Eye of Cthulhu, it charges repeatedly at the player while summoning Servants of Ocram (which act similarly to Servants of Cthulhu). Additionally, it fires numerous red laser beams. After its health is halved, it will, like several other bosses, switch into its second form. However, it will still continue to summon servants, unlike the Eye of Cthulhu. It will also constantly charge at the player(s), and the laser changes to a more damaging blue laser, with accompanying Demon Scythes. When Ocram is summoned you will get a message saying "Ocram has awoken!"

Drops

Once defeated, Ocram drops Souls of Blight and Adamantite Ore. There is also a 1 in 3 chance it will drop a single piece of either Dragon armor, Titan armor or Spectral armor.

Notes

  • Crystal Bullets are recommended for the fight with the Unreal Megashark, as his servants do not have much health.
  • Melee is not recommended for the fight, due to you being barely able to touch him. However, it is effective if you can dodge the servants of Ocram and repeatedly melee attack. Use an Excalibur or Tizona, and have powerful armor.
  • A Legendary Excalibur is recommended to kill his servants quickly for the hearts they drop.
  • The full Hallowed Armor melee set and all Accessories reforged to warding (+4 defense) would give you 70 defense. Coupled with the Obsidian Shield and Ironskin Potion, one would have 80 defense.
  • Ocram can only be summoned on a Hardmode world. Attempting to use the Suspicious Looking Skull on a world not in Hardmode will do nothing.
  • Ocram has the lowest defense for his second form of all the hardmode bosses, not counting The Destroyer, as it has no second form.
Anomaly: Sometimes your attacks will not work, as his hitbox is a bit off.
