The Dark Lance is a spear that can be found in Shadow Chests in the Underworld.

When used, it gives off light emitting particles that linger for a while, which is useful for fighting in the dark. It can pierce through enemies, and even hit the same enemy multiple times.

Notes

  • This weapon is useful for finding caves due to its long reach and particles.
  • Though it can hit the same enemy multiple times, as of 1.0.6, it appears that attacking a single enemy will only allow a single hit. Hitting multiple enemies at once allows the same enemy to be hit multiple times still, however.
  • Like all spears, this weapon cannot be reforged with the Legendary modifier.

History

  • 1.0.6:

    • Speed increased from average to fast.
    • Can now spawn more than once per world.
  • 1.0.5: Introduced.
