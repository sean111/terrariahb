The Jungle Chest is one of the Dungeon Chests. It is locked and must be unlocked with the Jungle Key before it can be looted. It will always contain the Piranha Gun.

Like all storage items, Jungle Chests can be freed with a Pickaxe once emptied, and picked up by the player for placement and use elsewhere.
