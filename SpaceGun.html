The Space Gun is a ranged Magic Weapon that auto-fires very quick green-colored lasers instead of bullets (which do not require the player to lead their targets). It consumes 8 Mana per shot, and can be fired repeatedly by holding down the attack button. The lasers give off light and can hit up to three enemies, making it good against small crowds and in dark places.

A full set of Meteor Armor has the unique effect of making the Space Gun cost no mana to fire.

The Space Gun is considered a magic weapon rather than a Gun, so its best possible Modifier is Mythical. It cannot acquire Ranged modifiers.

Notes

  • With full Meteor armor, the Space Gun deals 19 damage and costs 0 mana.
  • The Space Gun doesn't count as a firearm, so if the Arms Dealer is killed and the player doesn't have another firearm, he will not return until a replacement is found.
  • If shot repeatedly with Meteor armor on while mana recharging, it won't recharge until the gun stops firing.

Trivia

  • It looks similar to the Lego Star Wars laser gun.
