The Space Gun is a ranged Magic Weapon that is an upgraded version of the Flintlock Pistol. It costs 8 Mana to fire, and can be fired repeatedly by holding down the attack button. It shoots very quick lasers instead of ammunition, which do not require the player to lead their targets. The lasers give off light and can hit up to 3 enemies, making it good against small crowds and dark places.

Notes

  • With full Meteor armor, the Space Gun deals 19 damage and costs 0 mana.
  • The Space Gun doesn't count as a firearm, so if the Arms Dealer is killed, and don't have another firearm, he will not return until a replacement is found.
  • If shot repeatedly with Meteor armor on while mana recharging , it won't recharge until the gun stops firing.
  • Contrary to popular belief, although the Space Gun DOES seem to shoot lasers, they do NOT travel through glass.
Bug: As the Space Gun has no sale price in 1.1, it is Reforgeable for a single copper coin (1), though no coins are consumed. This can be done repeatedly until a desired set of bonuses is obtained.
