The Space Gun is a ranged Magic Weapon that auto-fires very quick green-colored lasers instead of bullets (the lasers travel faster than bullets, requiring less "lead" aim to targets). It consumes 8 Mana per shot, and can be fired repeatedly by holding down the attack button. The lasers give off light which can be handy to help illuminate dark places, and can pierce several (up to two) enemies, making it good against small crowds (so even though the damage may be less than other ranged weapons, the multiple-piercing-hits is a sort of multiplier).

A full set of Meteor Armor has the unique effect of making the Space Gun cost no mana to fire.

The Space Gun is available in the mobile version of Terraria.

The Space Gun is considered a magic weapon rather than a Gun, so its best possible Modifier is Mythical. It cannot acquire Ranged modifiers.

Notes
