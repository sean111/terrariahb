The Gills Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Gills. The buff grants the player the ability to breathe underwater instead of in air. This last for 2 minutes, but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon. Under its effects, the player will begin to drown when he otherwise wouldn't, and vice-versa.

Notes

  • You can breathe in air by equipping the Fish Bowl into the helmet area (not social) in your Inventory. This would usually cause the player to start losing breath, but as the effects are reversed while this buff is active, you will breathe normally.
  • Since they are treated the same when it comes to breathing, this potion also allows breathing when submerged in lava, though you will still take damage from the Lava unless you also have Obsidian Skin active.
