Glass is crafted from Sand Blocks at a Furnace. Glass can be placed as a block and is an ingredient in producing various items. Unlike most blocks, it cannot be broken by a pickaxe -- a hammer must be used instead.

Notes

  • When hit with a lower grade hammer, the glass will show a crack.
Anomaly: Striking any block of glass without breaking it has a chance of randomly changing the sprite. This can cause a "crack" in clear glass, but can just as easily turn a "cracked" block clear, or just change to a different cracked sprite. This can be used to create large structures of flawless glass; since the "hit count" of a block is reset if anything else is hit, one can alternate between blocks or between a block and another hammerable object until the desired sprite is achieved.
  • However, the "glasstructure" will only remain flawless until you restart the world (singleplayer: tested; multiplayer: non-tested)
  • Before 1.1, when destroyed the glass would shatter and be unrecoverable.

    • 1.1: Can now be used to craft Glass Wall. Now is recoverable when broken with a hammer.
