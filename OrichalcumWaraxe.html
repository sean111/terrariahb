The Orichalcum Waraxe is a mid-tier hardmode axe that is superceded by the Adamantite Waraxe/Titanium Waraxe and preceded by the Mythril Waraxe. It unlike the Orichalcum Chainsaw is swung overhead and can be more effective in fighting monsters as opposed to the Orichalcum Chainsaw. Compared to the chainsaw, it will have the same power, but not the same speed.
