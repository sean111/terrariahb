Mythril Bars are crafting subproducts made from 4 Mythril Ore. They are necessary to make the Mythril Anvil, which is in turn needed to make most high-end equipment. The Mythril Drill is the first tool characters will acquire that is capable of harvesting all blocks in the game.

Mythril gear seems to resemble Medieval style arms and armor.
