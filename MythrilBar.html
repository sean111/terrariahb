Mythril Bars are crafting subproducts made from 4 Mythril Ore. They are necessary to make the Mythril Anvil, which is in turn needed to make most high-end equipment. The Mythril Drill is the first tool characters will acquire that is capable of harvesting all blocks in the game (excluding Chlorophyte Ore and Lihzahrd Brick).

Mythril gear seems to resemble Medieval style arms and armor.Crafting all Mythril items including the anvil requires 130 Bars, or a total of 520 ore.
