Golden Bullets are one of 6 types of craftable ammunition for bullet-firing weapons. They may be crafted at a Work Bench once the Arms Dealer begins to sell Empty Bullets after Hard mode has been enabled. Like the Flask of Gold, it inflicts the Midas debuff on enemies, causing them to drop 10%-50% more Coins when killed.

Notes

  • These Bullets may still be crafted Pre-Hard mode, provided that the player has obtained the necessary items for crafting them.
  • The cost per round is 37 . This means a stack of 999 costs 3 69 63 .
  • Gold Dust may be purchased from the Merchant once the player has defeated the Wall of Flesh; provided that the player has a Merchant moved into a suitable living space, and met the 50 requirement for doing so.
  • These bullets pair very well with the Sniper Rifle as it causes an on-death effect, and due to the Sniper's high damage and lower ammo consumption, one could get a lot more money out of a stack of Golden Bullets.
    • In addition, using one of the three Shotgun type weapons is especially effective, since it fires multiple bullets for the cost of one.
  • Works very well on worms as it gives them the debuff multiple times.
