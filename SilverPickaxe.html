The Silver Pickaxe is an early game pickaxe, being stronger than the Iron Pickaxe and weaker than the Gold Pickaxe. It has an alternate ore version, the Tungsten Pickaxe, which is noticeably stronger as it can mine Meteorite, while the Silver Pickaxe cannot. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine a majority of blocks and ores.

For list of modifiers, see reforging.
