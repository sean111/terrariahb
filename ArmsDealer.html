Arms Dealer

Arms Dealer
Statistics
Type Town
Max Life 250

This is a town NPC. You may need a home to get him/her

The Arms Dealer is an NPC vendor that will spawn once the following criteria have been met:

  • There is an empty house.
  • You have at least one bullet firing Gun or Bullet in your inventory (not just stored in a Chest).

The easiest way to acquire a qualifying item is to smash either Shadow Orbs or Demon Hearts in the Corruption or the Crimson. They may drop a Musket or an Undertaker, respectively.

If the Arms Dealer is killed, he will only respawn if the above conditions are met again. This means you must place a Gun or Bullet in your inventory. Having them stored in Chests will not fulfill this requirement, and neither will magic guns such as the Space Gun or the Laser Rifle.

Names

The arms dealer may have any of the following names: DeShawn, DeAndre, Marquis, Darnell, Terrell, Malik, Trevon, Tyrone, Willie, Dominique, Demetrius, Reginald, Jamal, Maurice, Jalen, Darius, Xavier, Terrance, Andre, Dante, Brimst, Bronson, Tony, and Darryl.

Notes

Bug : While you have the trade window up, his stock will not change even when the requirements are met. Closing the window and bringing it back up fixes this.

Trivia

  • The Arms Dealer is very arrogant compared to the other NPCs and seems to have an observant personality, often noticing you (apparently) intend to either buy the Minishark or steal one of his weapons. This, when coupled with the way he speaks, may support that he is based off the traditional gangster. He also seems to have a rivalry with the Demolitionist, as he says his products are better than "fireworks". He also has a crush on the Nurse according to his quotes
  • The name Brimst may be a reference to Brimstone one of the ingredients of gunpowder.
  • The name Dante may be a reference to the main character of the Devil May Cry games.
  • The name Marquis may be a reference to the guns and ammo broker of Borderlands if read Mar-kiss.
