The Hallowed Repeater is the strongest and fastest repeater, aside from the console-exclusive Vulcan Repeater. It has the same white and gold color scheme as other Hallowed gear. It has the highest base damage of any ranged weapon in the game (not true anymore) aside from the Star Cannon, though it has a lower rate of fire than the Megashark or Clockwork Assault Rifle. It benefits greatly from the Archery Potion and Magic Quiver, pushing its top potential damage-per-shot above the Star Cannon.

The Hallowed Repeater is a good weapon for ranged players who prefer to use easy-to-acquire ammunition. Hellfire arrows can be easily crafted or purchased directly from the Demolitionist once hardmode has been enabled, and many arrows such as Cursed Arrows have a chance to be recovered after use.

The Hallowed Repeater's best possible Modifier is Unreal.
