Hallowed Repeater is the strongest and fastest crossbow in-game. It is white and gold in color. It has the highest base damage of any ranged weapon in the game aside from the Star Cannon, though it has a lower rate of fire than the Megashark or Clockwork Assault Rifle. It is the best weapon that benefits from the rather powerful Archery Potion, pushing its top potential damage-per-shot above the Star Cannon.

The Hallowed Repeater is a good weapon for ranged players who prefer to use easy-to-acquire ammunition. Hellfire arrows can be easily crafted or purchased directly from the Demolitionist once hardmode has been enabled, and many arrows such as Cursed Arrows have a chance to be recovered after use.
