This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Platinum armor is a stronger alternative to Gold armor. It takes a total of 90 Platinum Bars to craft a full suit.

It consists of a Platinum Helmet, Platinum Chainmail and Platinum Greaves.

A full set grants a basic 16 defense plus a set bonus of 4 defense, which equals a total of 20 defense, 4 more than gold, making it noticeably stronger. It is a viable alternative to Shadow armor or Crimson armor for entering Hardmode.
