Hallowed bars are ingots used to craft high-end armor/weapons in the game. They are dropped by the Hardmode bosses, namely The Twins, The Destroyer, and Skeletron Prime. This can make them rather hard to obtain or farm.

Before 1.2, crafting Hallowed Armor and gear required Souls, as well as the Cobalt, Mythril, and Adamantite versions of the item in question. With the release of 1.2, crafting Armor and weapons such as Excalibur has become dramatically easier.

To craft all Hallowed items, including all 5 Light Discs, excluding a Pumpkin Moon Medallion, costs 170 Hallowed Bars.

Tips

If you are farming hardmode bosses for money, crafting Gungnirs and selling them is the best way to make money, as their base price is 30 gold; it allows for about 2.5 gold per bar.

Trivia

  • Hallowed bars and Shroomite bars are the only bars to have no ore.
  • It is also the only bar to have no crafting recipe.

History

  • 1.2: Introduced. They replaced the Souls in crafting recipes for all Hallowed armor/Hallowed gear, except for the console items which will require other items to craft in addition to souls.
