Iron Bar

Iron Bar
Statistics
Type Crafting material
Max stack 99
Sell 3
Iron Bars are smelted from Iron Ore using a Furnace. 3 Iron Ore is required to craft one Iron Bar.

Iron Bars are used to make Iron weapons, Iron armor and Iron tools. Iron items are better than Copper items but worse than Silver items.
