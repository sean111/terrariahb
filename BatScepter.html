This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Bat Scepter is a magic weapon which summons bats and drops from Pumpking. The scepter appears to fire between 1-3 projectiles per shot, and may be held down instead of repeatedly clicking. The bats will home in on enemies, but unlike the Wasp Gun / Bee Gun, the projectiles will not bounce off walls, instead, they just disappear.

Though the damage and speed on the tooltip appears to be similar to that of the Leaf Blower and the Magnet Sphere, it will more often than not fire multiple projectiles at once, thus bringing its overall damage per second above these two weapons and most other weapons in the game against smaller groups of enemies. While wearing Spectre Armor, this weapon functions similarly to Vampire Knives with extremely high life steal.

Notes

  • Like bees, the bats cannot enter water.
  • Bats will not start homing in on an enemy until they have traveled a certain distance. Since bats are fired in a random cone pattern, this restricts how many bats can hit an enemy in smaller, more confined areas, such as hallways in a Dungeon.
Anomaly: This weapon cannot gain positive magical modifiers when reforged, leaving the best prefix as Godly. This is likely due to its almost nonexistent mana cost.
Anomaly: When an enemy is taking damage from the Rainbow Gun, the bats will not damage the enemy until the rainbow dissipates, after which bats will all attack at once for a large amount of burst damage.
