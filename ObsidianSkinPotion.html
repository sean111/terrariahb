The Obsidian Skin Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Obsidian Skin. The buff grants immunity to lava, Hellstone and Meteorite for 4 minutes (but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon). You can, however, still drown in lava.

A Water Walking Potion is a viable alternative to an Obsidian Skin Potion for some purposes, as it will allow you to stand on the surface of lava without taking damage.

Notes

You may also use this with the Gills Potion so that you can breath in lava.
