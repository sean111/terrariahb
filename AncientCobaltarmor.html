The Ancient Cobalt Armor is a very rare drop from Hornet or Man Eater. It is based off of the cobalt armor that was the prototypical mage armor before it was replaced with the Jungle armor in 1.0.3. Aesthetically, it is the same looking armor as its namesake, but it has the exact same stats as the current Jungle Armor. It is obtained the same way it was in 1.0.3, as a rare drop from Hornets and Man Eaters. Before 1.0.3, the Cobalt Armor pieces aided in mana regeneration, and the set bonus reduced mana usage of 16%. Much like the Cobalt Armor before it was changed to Jungle Armor back in 1.0.3, it gives an after-image effect when the full set is worn, much like the Shadow armor, Necro armor, and the 1.1 Cobalt armor.

Notes

Anomaly 1.2.1.2: If you mix and match Ancient Cobalt and Jungle armor pieces, you will not get the set bonus.
Bug 1.2.1.2: Does not grant the increased magic critical strike chance.
  • The armor has the same sprite and method of being aquired as Cobalt Armor had before 1.0.3, thus the phrase "Ancient" in it.
  • It is possible to obtain more than one piece of armor from a single enemy.
