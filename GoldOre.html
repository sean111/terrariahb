Gold Ore is an early game ore, which spawns in the Underground and Cavern biomes, as well as on Floating Islands. Its primary use is to make Gold Bars, which can be used to make the Gold tier of equipment, as well as crowns, Thrones, and several other items. The equivalent of Gold Ore is Platinum Ore, which will sometimes replace Gold on a world.

Trivia

  • In real life, gold is one of the softest metals, but in Terraria (as lampshaded by the Goblin Tinkerer) it is harder than such heavy-duty metals as iron or steel.


Notes

Anomaly: Even though gold and platinum bars and ores are of equal value, platinum coins are worth 100 gold coins.
