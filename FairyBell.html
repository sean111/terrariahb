The Fairy Bell is a Hardmode magic item that grants the player the Fairy buff, which summons a glowing creature resembling a Pixie that follows the character and provides light (much like the Shadow Orb, though faster, brighter, and colored). The color of the Fairy is chosen at random from blue, green, and red, and depending on the Lighting Mode, will cast a matching color of light. The Fairy Bell consumes no Mana as of 1.2.

If the fairy gets too far away from the player, it will teleport to their location. The fairy can pass through objects. A new random color can be chosen by re-summoning the Fairy.

The Fairy is faster and brighter than the Shadow Orb, and so can be considered a direct upgrade.

Trivia

  • The fairy could be a reference to some games from the Legend of Zelda series, specifically Phantom Hourglass, where players had access to 3 different colours of Fairies, depending on what "Section" of the Triforce they were working with: Power, Wisdom, or Courage.
  • The colors of the fairies could be a reference to the three goddesses (from which the colors of the Triforce/the faries in Phantom Hourglass come) in Zelda: Nayru, Din, and Farore.
  • This could also be a reference to Walt Disney's Peter Pan and other cassical neverland tales, where fairies can be "summoned" using a Bell.
