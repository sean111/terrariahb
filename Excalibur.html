Excalibur is the strongest sword in-game, and can be swung continuously like the Muramasa, Cobalt Sword and Phasesaber. It is white and gold in color, and it emits a particle effect. It is part of the Hallowed set of Armor/Weapons.

The Enchanted Sword seems to be an enchanted Excalibur.

Tips

  • The player is unable to turn while attacking with it, similar to the War Axe of the Night and The Breaker. It is advisable to briefly release the attack button between swings when the player wishes to turn around.
  • Excalibur has a lower knockback than the Mythril Sword and Adamantite Sword that were used to craft it, but equipping a Titan Glove dropped from Mimics greatly increases the knockback, making it a superb melee weapon for mobs in general.
  • When used with a Hallowed Armor set, the swing speed is greatly increased, giving it excellent DPS having good worth as a PvP weapon.
  • It uses the attributes of all the swords you use for crafting it. It gets the Cobalt Sword's speed. The Mythril Sword's shape. and the Adamantite Sword's power and range

Trivia

  • Excalibur is the mythical sword of King Arthur from Arthurian legends.
Anomaly: When reading a Sign or a Gravestone, the player cannot continuously swing.
