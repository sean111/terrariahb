The Illuminant Bat is a Hardmode enemy. It is the Hallowed version of the Giant Bat, and is slightly stronger. It only spawns in the Underground Hallow (cavern layer under the hallow biome). Just like its name suggests, the Illuminant Bat emits a small amount of light, making it quite easy to spot.
