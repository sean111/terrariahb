The Demon Bow is a Bow crafted from Demonite. It is an upgrade over the Gold Bow and the Platinum Bow, being stronger and significantly faster. It, along with its Crimtane counterpart, the Tendon Bow, is the last bow the player will be able to craft until reaching The Underworld, as the focus shifts to guns for the next part of the game.

Note: On the iOS version, can drop from the presents, apparently. (Bug?)
