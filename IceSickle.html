The Ice Sickle is a weapon randomly dropped by Armored Viking in the Underground Snow Biome. When used it shoots a moving projectile that will either shatter by colliding with blocks or reach a fixed distance from the player. This projectile will then spin in place, hitting multiple enemies in a similar fashion to the Vilethorn before disappearing after a couple of seconds. This makes it a very effective weapon for close-range area control, as most enemies will be knocked back and take high damage by trying to pass through the hovering projectile. It's also potent against slow bosses like the Wall of Flesh or The Destroyer that take multiple hits from each projectile.

The only downside is that the projectiles will not travel very far before halting in midair, effectively making it function as a ranged weapon with a very short range.

Notes

  • Like the Death Sickle, the range of the projectile produced by the weapon goes further as melee speed is increased.
  • There is a limit to the amount of projectiles that may be present on the screen, but it can only be reached through >+40% melee speeds.

Trivia

The name "Ice Sickle" is a pun of the word "icicle".
