Console-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console version of Terraria.

The Vulcan Repeater is a top tier Repeater, which looks like a purple Mythril Repeater. It fires Vulcan Bolts, which explode on impact, dealing damage to nearby enemies.

Since they explode, Vulcan Bolts cannot be retrieved.

The explosion can damage the player and 6 bolts will kill the player even with full dragon armor. Caution should be used when firing downward or at close targets.

Occasionally, a Vulcan Bolt will travel a short distance before splitting into two separate bolts.

Notes

  • Players should take caution when moving or pointing it straight down or directly up, as the explosions do massive damage (around 70 a shot) plus the fact that it has a fast speed. It may kill you in a few hits (depending on your armor)
Anomaly: It sometimes shoots two arrows at once however still uses a single arrow. Possibly due to bolts splitting as soon as it is fired.

Trivia

  • Likely a reference to the Roman god of fire and blacksmithing, Vulcan.
