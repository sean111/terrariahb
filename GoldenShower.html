The Golden Shower is a magic weapon which shoots a stream of golden Ichor from the player in an arc, similar to the Aqua Scepter. If an enemy gets hit by the stream, they become afflicted with the Ichor Debuff, which lowers defense. The stream also pierces through enemies, making it easy to give multiple enemies the debuff at once. When used, the Golden Shower will spray three times per "use", and the player can change the direction of aim between these sprays - one shot can effectively be used to debuff enemies on both sides of the player. Enemies hit by the Golden Shower will glow gold in color. Since the stream of Ichor is affected by gravity, it can easily be used to shoot around vertical corners.

The Mythical prefix provides the optimum overall experience when using the Golden Shower.

Notes

  • The Golden Shower's fast speed and ability to pierce make it a great item for fighting large groups of enemies.
  • The defense reduction on this item makes it great to attack bosses with, as one can attack the enemy with it and equip a stronger weapon to deal even more damage.
  • This weapon is probably the best spell for taking out a worm-like enemies before obtaining a Harp as its ability to pierce infinitely along with it being able to reduce defense allows this weapon to deal a lot of damage in a short period of time.
  • As of 1.2.1.2 , the Destroyer is immune to this spell's debuff, but the piercing effect still remains viable when fighting him.
  • When used with the Crystal Storm, Cursed Flames or Magnet Sphere, this spell can set up devastating combos against entire groups of enemies.
  • This item is one of the highest magic DPS items in the game (not even considering the enemies defense that it lowers).
  • The Golden Shower can be used as a substitute for the Hunter Potion since enemies hit by it will glow.
  • Enemies can only receive a single debuff at any given time.
  • The Golden Shower actually fires long and fast gravity-affected projectiles. It is possible to have the same projectile hit the same spot twice if fired straight up.
  • Because of how easily-obtained this spell is early in Hardmode, getting it should be a priority as soon as you beat the Wall of Flesh, considering how effective this spell is until late in the game.


Trivia

  • A golden shower is a slang term for the act of urinating on top of, or above, another individual. Since this spell involves spraying yellow fluid at an enemy (and travels in an arc), this is probably an intentional double-entendre.
