PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Beenades are a type of explosive dropped by the Queen Bee boss, which act similarly to the Grenade. Upon exploding, a swarm of Bees are released, which will follow the nearest enemy. Bees appear to stay until there are no more enemies left to attack, or they have been killed. Bees from a Beenade cannot enter water.
