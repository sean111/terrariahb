The Magnet Sphere is a Hardmode magic weapon which casts a glowing sphere where the player aims. It is obtained as a rare drop from the Blue Armored Bones, Rusty Armored Bones, Hell Armored Bones or Bone Lee in the Hardmode Dungeon.

When cast, it fires a cyan sphere that travels in a straight line. The sphere will rapidly shoot beams of energy at the closest enemy as it travels through the air. It will disappear if the central orb hits a solid object or has gone 52 blocks, or if it has been in the air for 7 seconds. If the player casts another sphere while there is already one in the air, the previous one will fade away.

Notes

  • Sphere Fires projectiles at a rate equal to a use time of 9.
  • The Magnet Sphere is one of the few projectiles which doesn't destroy grass or vines upon contact. This makes it useful for killing enemies in decorative grasslands, as the grass will not be destroyed.
    • The beams it fires, however, DO break vines/grass.
  • The sphere moves further when it has a modifier reducing speed

Tips

  • This weapon is great for dealing with large hordes of enemies because it auto-targets the next enemy after its target has been killed.
  • This weapon is best used in conjunction with the Spectre Armor, since it does a great amount of damage very fast, meaning an important healing capability.
  • After you cast a sphere, you can focus on other things while the sphere does its damage.
  • It is not advised to use the Magnet Sphere in an enclosed space as the sphere will not stay onscreen long enough to deal a great amount of damage.
  • The Magnet Sphere does not appear to have the "auto-targeting" laser effect in PvP. If the sphere contacts another player it will still damage them however
  • As with most other magic items, the best modifier for the Magnet Sphere is Mythical.
