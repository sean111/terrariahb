Purification Powder is a thrown consumable item that can be used to clear blocks of Corruption and The Crimson. It releases as a cloud spread with a very short range comparable to that of a Broadsword, and produces a fair amount of light when thrown. It is the counterpart to Vile Powder. Purification Powder is purchased from the Dryad for 75 .

Purification Powder returns Ebonstone and Crimstone to Stone, Ebonsand and Crimsand to Sand, Red Ice Blocks and Purple Ice Blocks to Ice Blocks and corrupted grass as well as crimson grass to normal grass.

Notes

  • Purification Powder does not clear the Corruption's thorns, which can continue to spread Corruption. Thorns must be cleared via a Weapon or Tool, or by walking through it (though it causes players damage on contact).
  • Along with Dynamite and Bombs, Purification Powder is (prior to Hardmode) one of the only methods of tunneling through Ebonstone/Crimstone to reach Shadow Orbs/Demon Hearts without having to first defeat the Eater Of Worlds or the Brain of Cthulhu (which drops the Shadow Scale or Tissue Sample required to craft the Nightmare Pickaxe or Deathbringer Pickaxe).
