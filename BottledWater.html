Bottled Water is crafted by hand from Bottle while standing near a body of water. This will not lower the water level. It is not required to be directly in a body of water to create this item; you only have to be close to it, like other crafting stations.

Bottled Water heals you for 20 Health instantly when used, and triggers the Potion Sickness debuff. This will not give you the empty Bottle back. Bottled Water is also used as material to craft a wide variety of Potions.

Notes

  • A common mistake is to try and use the bottle on the water, like a bucket. This will not work. You must go to the crafting menu (default key: Esc), while standing near water.
