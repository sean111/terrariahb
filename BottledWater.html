Bottled Water is crafted by hand from Bottle while standing near a body of water. It also does not lower the water level. This allows for the creation of bottled water farms. It is not required to be directly in a body of water to create this item, instead you only have to be close to it, like other crafting stations.

Bottled Water heals you for 20 HP instantly when used, but does not give you the empty Bottle back. It is also used as material to make every a wide variety of alchemical substances.
