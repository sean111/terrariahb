This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Flask of Cursed Flames causes your melee attacks to inflict the Cursed Inferno debuff on enemies. The flask of cursed flames is crafted at the Imbuing Station (bought from the witch doctor). This effect is applied by all weapons that deal melee damage, includes Scourge of the Corruptor, Paladin's Hammer, and Vampire Knives.

This item currently does not apply the debuff in player vs. player scenarios.
