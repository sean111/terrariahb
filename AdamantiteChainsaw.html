The Adamantite Chainsaw is an axe-type tool crafted using Adamantite Bars. With a Godly prefix and a Warrior Emblem, it deals 51 damage and attacks extremely quickly, for a massive damage output, about 100 damage every second. Like the previous tier chainsaws, it was used to craft the once-legendary Hamdrax. Following 1.2 on PC, it is no longer used to craft what is now known as the Drax.
