Statistics
Type Summoning
Max stack 1
Rarity Green
Use time 44
Tooltip Summons the Frost Legion

The Snow Globe can be obtained from opening presents in hardmode worlds, but can be used in any world. The chance of getting a Snow Globe is 1/14 or 7.14%. When used it summons the Frost Legion, an event very similar to Goblin Invasion. There are three kinds of enemies that will be summoned:

  • Snow Balla, which throws snow balls
  • Mister Stabby, who has a knife
  • Snowman Gangsta, with a Tommy Gun.

The Frost Legion should only be summoned once the player has acquired hardmode gear; they are considerably stronger than the Goblin invasion.

Notes

Snow Globes can be used to summon the Frost Legion any time of the year, not just during the Christmas period when presents are obtainable.
