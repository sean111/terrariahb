Glowing Mushrooms can be found on blue grass, in deeper parts of the Underground. Harvesting these mushrooms has a chance of dropping a Glowing Mushroom and may also yield Mushroom Grass Seeds. They emit their own light, making them quite easy to find. Sometimes it can be generated underwater. Unlike mushrooms, they will spawn even if there's a wall, allowing players to make safe Farms with no monster spawns. If the conditions are right, giant glowing mushrooms will spawn amongst the small ones. These can be harvested with an axe to yield several Glowing Mushrooms and Mushroom Grass Seeds.

In addition, using the Mushroom Spear or the Hammush will create spinning Glowing Mushrooms in the air which damage enemies until they disappear.
