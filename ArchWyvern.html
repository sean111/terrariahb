Arch Wyverns are large, console-only, Hardmode enemies that spawn at high altitudes and look similar to Wyverns. They resemble oriental dragons, are long and green, and follow the Worm AI.

Notes

  • If you are above or close to your house and are fighting a Arch Wyvern, and you use Magic Mirror to teleport back, the Arch Wyvern will follow you to your house/spawn point.
  • It is possible to kill the Arch Wyvern while its head is off the map, which can result in the loss of its drops.
  • Dodging the Wyvern is very easy. Simply move to the side when the Wyvern is coming, and it will not follow due to it's Worm AI motions. Angel or Demon Wings make this very easy.
