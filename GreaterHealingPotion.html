The Greater Healing Potion is a potion that can be acquired once entering Hardmode. It heals 150 health of a character on use, and is the most potent of all healing items.

Consumption will trigger the Potion Sickness debuff.

This item is dropped by The Twins, The Destroyer and Skeletron Prime.

Notes

Unlike other potions (Lesser and Normal/Regular), Greater Healing Potions cannot be combined with a Greater Mana Potion to make a restoration-type potion.
