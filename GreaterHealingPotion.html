Greater Healing Potion is a Hardmode potion that heals 150 health. It is currently the most potent of all healing items.

Consumption will trigger the Potion Sickness debuff.

This item is dropped by The Twins, The Destroyer, Skeletron Prime, Plantera, and Golem

Notes

  • Unlike other potions (Lesser and Normal/Regular), Greater Healing Potions cannot be combined with a Greater Mana Potion to make a greater restoration potion.
  • Also, unlike the others, it is not made from the lower-tier potion.
