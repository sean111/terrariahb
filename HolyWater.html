Holy Water is a Hardmode consumable weapon that transforms a small area into The Hallow when thrown. It can be used against enemies, but deals relatively little damage compared to other Hardmode weapons. Holy Water is the Hallowed counterpart to Unholy Water, which spreads Corruption.

Notes

  • Compared to using Hallowed Seeds directly, Holy Water spreads Hallow instantly to multiple blocks of varying types, and offers more uses per Hallowed Seed.
  • The item, including its sprite and the way it's thrown, is possibly a reference to the Castlevania item of the same name.
