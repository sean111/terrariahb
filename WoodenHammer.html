A Wooden Hammer is in the first tier of Hammers, just below the Copper Hammer. Like all hammers, it is used to remove background objects such as Walls. It can also be used to break Life Crystals, making it a must have item early in game.

Notes

  • Due to its low damage and very slow swinging speed the Wooden Hammer is the weakest weapon in Terraria.
  • Apparently due to the low base damage, no modifier that affects damage (other than "Broken") can be given to the Wooden Hammer. This means that the best it can get is either "Agile" or "Massive," though this is debatable.
