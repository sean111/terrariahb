Magic Dagger is a golden dagger dropped by Mimics. It is a magic weapon that can be thrown as many times as the user wishes (until out of mana) without having to worry about running out. Daggers thrown have a creamy yellowish glow, and follow the same physics as regular Throwing Knives. They can be used as temporary glowstick-like light sources.

Notes

  • It may be a reference to the many Castlevania games, where a magic dagger can be found and used until the player is out of hearts.
  • Despite the tooltip implying that it is a single dagger you can launch multiple magic daggers at the same time.
  • Compared to other weapons, the magic dagger sells for a fairly large amount of coins (20 gold), and provides an easy method of making money, should the player own one and not need it.
  • In the same way as selling the dagger, reforging it will cost a large amount of gold too (1 platinum).
