The Magic Dagger is a Hardmode magic weapon that operates exactly as the Throwing Knife, but uses Mana, instead of consuming stacks of the item. It also causes more than double the damage. A single Magic Dagger can be thrown limitlessly whenever Mana is available, and is never consumed. Magic Daggers also produce a small amount of light. The Magic Dagger is dropped by Mimics.

The Magic Dagger's best possible Modifier is Mythical.

Notes

  • Despite the tooltip implying that it is a single dagger that must "magically return" before being thrown again, multiple Magic Daggers can be thrown in succession without waiting for them to impact.
  • The Magic Dagger's sell price is much higher than most Hardmode magic weapons (20 ), which also causes its Reforge cost to be inordinately high (1 ).

Trivia

  • The Magic Dagger may be a reference to the many Castlevania games, where a magic dagger can be found and used until the player is out of hearts.
