General

A snowball is a ranged weapon that can be crafted out of snow blocks. Due to the short range it is relatively weak if used in combat although the knockback is strong enough to defend yourself against single enemies.Snowballs can also be used as Ammo for the Snowball Cannon and the Snowball Launcher.

Notes

Snowballs are affected by gravity, so they are ineffective against enemies at further ranges.
