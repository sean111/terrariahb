A Slime Crown is an object that crafted at a Demon Altar with 1 Gold Crown and 99 Gel. The Slime Crown summons the King Slime. The slime crown can be used anywhere unlike the conditions for the King Slime appearing on its own. But if you do wait for it to appear on its own and then use the Slime Crown to try to summon another King Slime your player will die.

Notes

  • The Slime Crown is a fast way to farm the Ninja Clothes, given you have already have enough resources to make a Slime Crown.
  • It is less resource consuming to use a Battle Potion and hold a Water Candle in the outer 2/3 of the world in order to spawn King Slime.
  • Using a Slime Crown while King Slime is still alive will cause the player to die.
  • It is best to use the Slime Crown in an area with just flat land and no obstructions as this could make the battle easier - the King Slime spawns blue slimes and it may prove difficult.
Bug: Occasionally, King Slime will not be spawned after using Slime Crown. This is confirmed to happen in both Multiplayer and Single Player.
