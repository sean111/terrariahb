The Corruption Chest is located within the Dungeon, requiring a Corruption Key to unlock. The chest has a 100% chance to contain a Scourge of the Corruptor, a melee based weapon, as well as other miscellaneous items.

Notes

  • It is impossible to have a Corruption Chest and a Crimson Chest both naturally spawn in the same world simultaneously.
