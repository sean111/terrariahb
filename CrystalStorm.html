The Crystal Storm is a magic weapon. It fires small crystals at a rate slightly faster than that of the Minishark, with each shot costing five mana. The crystals will bounce off of walls, and lose velocity as they travel. Their speed does not, however, affect their damage output. The crystals also provide a small amount of light. This spell is highly effective against bosses due to its high damage, no ammo requirement, and low mana cost, as well as in caves and other small areas due to the ricochet effect of its shots. This power can be further improved with magic-buffing accessories such as the Sorcerer Emblem. In apearance, the item looks like a book (similar to the Cursed Flames, Water Bolt, and Demon Scythe spells) with a pink cover featuring a blue, crystal-esque design.

It is created at a Bookcase using a Spell Tome, Crystal Shards and Souls of Light.

Trivia

  • Crystal Storm seems to be the Hallowed counterpart of the Corruption's Cursed Flames.
  • Like arrows, the Crystal Storm projectiles move slower while you are in water. In contrast they do not speed up upon exiting water, leading to a much shorter range.

Notes

  • Contrary to popular belief, the Crystal Storm does not have the ability to pierce. This misconception is most likely from its high rate of fire and ricocheting ability.
  • It is one of the best spells for fighting bosses.
  • Crystal Storm is superior to Magical Harp when piercing and repeat hits are not needed.
Bug: It is possible to shoot through 1-block wide walls.
