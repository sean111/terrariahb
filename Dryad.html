This is a town NPC. You may need a home to get him/her

The Dryad is an NPC vendor that will spawn once the following criteria have been met:

  • There is an empty House.
  • You have killed one of the following bosses: Eye of Cthulhu, Eater of Worlds or Skeletron.

She will tell you the percentage of Corruption vs. Hallow in the game world. If the world has more Hallow than Corruption, she will say "Keep up the good work!". If there is more Corruption than Hallow, and at least 20% corruption, she will say "Things are grim indeed.". Otherwise she will say "You should try harder."

Names

The Dryad may have any of the following names: Alalia, Alura, Ariella, Caelia, Calista, Celestia, Chryseis, Emerenta, Elysia, Evvie, Faye, Felicitae, Lunette, Nata, Nissa, Tatiana, Rosalva, Shea, Tania, Isis and Xylia.

Anomaly: The Dryad and the Party Girl may both have the name Isis.

Notes

  • Even if your world has an equal% of Corruption and Hallow, she will still state that you are not trying hard enough.
  • Even if your world generated with Crimson, one can still buy Corrupt Seeds from her during a blood moon. This can be used to obtain Cursed Flames and other Corruption-specific items even in a world without Corruption by default.
  • Her quote talking about the Truffle and calling him a "fun guy" could be a reference to the old joke playing on the homophone of fungi.
Anomaly: The Dryad can move into an empty house even when you did not defeat a Boss monster. This can occur when you plant Hallow in a pre-Hardmode world.
Bug : If you clear/ purify a lot of corruption/ crimson/ hallow, then ask the Dryad for the status of your world, she will still say the same percentage unless you disconnect then re-connect to your world.
