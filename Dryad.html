The Dryad is an NPC vendor that will spawn once the following criteria have been met:

  • There is an empty House.
  • You have killed one of the following bosses: Eye of Cthulhu, Eater of Worlds or Skeletron.

She will tell you the percentage of Corruption vs. Hallow in the game world. If the world has more Hallow than Corruption, she will say "Keep up the good work!". If there is more Corruption than Hallow, and at least 20% corruption, she will say "Things are grim indeed.". Otherwise she will say "You should try harder."

Items sold

Once the Dryad has moved into a house, she will sell the following:

Inventory

Item Cost Availability
Purification Powder 75 While not a Blood Moon.
Grass Seeds 20 While not a Blood Moon.
Sunflower 2 While not a Blood Moon.
Acorn 10 Always available.
Dirt Rod 20 Always available.
Vile Powder 1 During a Blood Moon.
Corrupt Seeds 5 During a Blood Moon.
Hallowed Seeds 20 If Wall of Flesh is defeated.

Notes

  • Even if your world has 0% Corruption and Hallow, she will still state that you are not trying hard enough.
Anomaly: The Dryad can move into an empty house even when you did not defeat a Boss monster. This can occur when you plant Hallow in a pre-Hardmode world.

Trivia

  • The Dryad will sometimes talk about how the Merchant keeps trying to sell her an Angel Statue (see quote below). However, if you go and talk to the Merchant he will sometimes deny selling Angel Statues, saying they are "junk".
  • The Dryad appears to be wearing no clothing, with only vines/leaves covering her.
