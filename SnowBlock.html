Statistics
Type Block Crafting material
Max stack 250
Use time 14

Snow Blocks are found in the Snow Biome, and also dropped by all enemies of the Frost Legion. They are also created when a Snow Balla throws snowballs. These blocks, when placed, will make snow fall in the background, creating an artificial Snow Biome, which cannot be Hallowed or Corrupted.

Trees planted in Snow Blocks will be covered with snow and won't drop any Acorns when harvested.

Snow Blocks can also be crafted into Snow Bricks at a workbench, which can in turn be crafted into Snow Brick Walls
