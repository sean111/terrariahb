The Santa costume is a set of vanity items made up of the Santa Hat, the Santa Shirt, and the Santa Pants. It can be purchased from Santa Claus after defeating the Frost Legion.

These items are equipped in the Social slots as they do not provide anything but a change in visual appearance of the character.
