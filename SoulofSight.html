Soul of Sight is a Hardmode crafting material that drops from The Twins. Soul of Sight is essential for crafting most Hallowed magic weapons, including the Magical Harp, Fairy Bell, and Rainbow Rod. Crafting all items requires 85 Souls on PC (100 on consoles),meaning the player must defeat The Twins 3-5 times.

Tips

  • All types of Souls are very useful for selling as profit once your necessary items have been crafted.
  • To craft all items (including all 5 Keys), you need 85 Souls of Sight (the most of the 3 Hardmode Boss Souls), so you must fight the Twins at least 3 times.
