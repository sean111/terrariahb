An Autohammer is a crafting station used to convert Chlorophyte Bars into Shroomite Bars. It is purchased from the Truffle for 1 Platinum Coin. It has a unique smashing animation when placed. The animation shows the hammer constantly smashing what looks like chlorophyte into shroomite.
