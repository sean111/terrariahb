PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

This item drops from the Mimic that is disguised as a Ice Chest in the Ice Biome. Using it will summon a small Snowman to follow you. Jumping too high will cause the Snowman to jump into a tiny sled.

Summoning the Snowman does not destroy the sled, summoning it again while it is already summoned will simply bring it to the block you are standing on.

To stop it following you, right click the icon below your hotbar.
