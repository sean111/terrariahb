The Iron Pickaxe is an early game pickaxe, stronger than the starting Copper Pickaxe and weaker than the Silver Pickaxe. It has an alternate ore version, the Lead Pickaxe, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine the majority of blocks and ores.

Anomaly: Unlike the other base metal pickaxes (Copper, Silver, and Gold), the Iron Pickaxe requires only 3 Wood to craft.
