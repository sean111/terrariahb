The Copper Pickaxe is the first pickaxe the player will obtain, and is weaker than the Iron Pickaxe. It has an alternate ore version, the Tin Pickaxe, which is actually slightly stronger. It also has a -1 range attribute, meaning it can only mine 4 blocks to the sides and 2 blocks above and below the player. Newly created characters spawn with this item in their first inventory slot. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine a majority of blocks and ores.
