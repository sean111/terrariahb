This article is about the Werewolf enemy. For the buff of the same name, see Werewolf (buff).

The Werewolf is a hardmode enemy that spawns only during a full moon. In this time they become as common as Zombies, so be aware that you may get swarmed.

When a Werewolf attacks, it has a chance to give the Bleeding debuff. At close range, it will attack the player by leaping or lunging at them, closing distances quickly to catch unwary players off-guard. Werewolves can also open doors.

You can transform into one by wearing the Moon Charm during a full moon.

Trivia

Through common lore, the main weakness of werewolves is silver, but using this strategy in Terraria is not recommended because silver weapons deal low damage to werewolves as it is not programmed in. You will be better off using pre-hardmode endgame items.
