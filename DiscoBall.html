The Disco Ball is a novelty Hardmode light source that can be placed on a ceiling tile and will generate short pulses of colored light that cycle through red, green, and blue. It can be toggled with wires. It is sold by the Merchant for 1 after defeating Wall of Flesh.

Notes

  • The Disco Ball does not count as light source for house requirements.
  • Unlike most light sources, the Disco Ball will not produce any light in non-color light modes.
  • The Disco Ball's effect is much more noticeable in darker areas than when placed in a bright spot.
  • The Disco Ball's flashing is synchronized with the color cycling of rainbow bricks.
