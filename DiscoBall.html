The Disco Ball is an item sold by the Merchant for 1 gold coin after defeating Wall of Flesh. It can be placed on a ceiling tile and will generate random lighting effects nearby, simulating that of a Torch, but does not counts as light source for house requirements. It can be toggled with wires.
