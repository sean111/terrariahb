Snowman Gangsta is an enemy in the Frost Legion. It carries a tommy gun and shoots musket ball-like projectiles at the player at a slow rate. He can only shoot in one direction, so angling oneself above or below him is most effective. Trapping him in a water-filled pit seems to be effective.
