Slimelings are hardmode enemies that are similar to a Baby Slime. They are spawned in a pack of three upon the death of a Corrupt Slime. They have a chance to induce the Darkness debuff. Slimelings are like mini Corrupt Slimes.

Slimelings are immune to the Poisoned debuff.
