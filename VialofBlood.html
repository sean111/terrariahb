Vial of Blood

Vial of Blood
Statistics
Type Potion
Max stack 1
Rarity Orange
Use time 20 (fast)
Tooltip Summons a pet bat
Console/Mobile-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console and mobile version of Terraria.

The Vial of Blood summons a pet Bat when used. It grants the player the Pet Bat buff that lasts until the player manually cancels the buff or dies. Unlike other pets, the pet bat will deal a small amount of damage to enemies it happens to touch. Also, when a player is in a body of water, the bat will fly above it like any other type of bat.

Notes

  • Found in Golden Chests.
  • The bat summoned will loosely (if at all) follow enemies close to you and inflict 1-2 damage per contact with enemy.
  • Ignores walls, passing through to stay near the player
  • Immune to damage of all types, including lava
  • The bat summoned by the Vial of Blood looks very similar to the Jungle Bat.
  • As fast as the player while using fully charged Hermes Boots

History

  • Console-Release: Introduced.
