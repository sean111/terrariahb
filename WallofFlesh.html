The Wall of Flesh (WOF) is a Boss located in The Underworld. It is the final and most difficult Boss in regular mode. Beating it will trigger Hardmode.

Summoning

To summon the Wall of Flesh, a Guide Voodoo Doll must be thrown (or otherwise fall) into Lava in the Underworld while the Guide is alive, which sacrifices the Guide and spawn the Boss. If the Guide does not exist at the time, the Wall of Flesh will not spawn. With the Guide sacrificed, the Wall of Flesh will spawn and instantly cast the Horrified debuff on all players, which will last until the Wall or the players die.

Very rarely will the Wall of Flesh spawn randomly with no warning in the Underworld.

When you summon it, take note of your position. The Wall of Flesh will appear in the direction of the closest world edge. (If you are east of the center, it will appear to the east, and vice-versa)

The Wall of Flesh can be summoned and defeated multiple times in the same World.

Attacks

The Wall of Flesh has several attacks.

  • First and foremost, the Wall is very large, occupying the entire screen vertically, and continuously sweeps across the screen at a steady pace, forcing the player to keep moving away or suffer damage from The Tongue (see Trying to escape below). When preparing an Arena, keep in mind that the fight will take you many screens away, so any platform you build will need to be very long (ie. tile length reaching in the thousands) (Water Walking Potion is of tremendous use in case you find yourself at the end of your platform, to mitigate the Underworld's many Lava pools).
  • The Wall has small mouths called The Hungry, each attached by a long vein, which move towards and damages players. As the Wall of Flesh's main body takes damage, these mouths deal more damage, and become more resistant to attacks. It is therefore advisable to take care of them first. When they've suffered enough damage, they will detach and attempt to fly into players.
  • The Wall has eyes that will shoot lasers. As its health decreases, the lasers increase in damage, speed and frequency. Finally, it will spit Leeches out of its mouth, which chase players.

Trying to escape

If a player attempts to escape from the fight by physically leaving, or if they try to get behind the Wall of Flesh, the mouth will release The Tongue, which will drag them in front of the mouth and deal a large amount of damage while leaving the player immobilized in the process.

If you approach the Wall from behind (usually by having died and trying to catch up to the wall in order to finish it), the tongue will only appear if you are in reasonable proximity.

If you manage to avoid The Tongue and exit the Underworld or run far enough while the Horrified debuff is in effect, you will instantly die, with the message <PLAYER NAME> tried to escape. If you use the Magic Mirror to escape, death occurs after teleportation, so this can be used as a last-ditch attempt to conserve items/money - they will drop next to the spawn.

Killing it

The Wall of Flesh must be killed before it reaches the end of the map, otherwise all living players will die, and the Wall will despawn.

To destroy the Wall of Flesh, you must attack its eyes or its mouth. They all share the same life counter, so shooting at any of the three will deplete the boss' total health. The "wall" itself is transparent to weapons, and shooting the Wall of Flesh there will not deplete its health.

Unlike other bosses, this boss does not despawn if you die: it will remain alive until it reaches the other end of the map, and it is possible to go back to The Underworld after respawning to continue fighting it. Trying to summon it again before it despawns will simply destroy the Guide Voodoo Doll, and kill the new Guide if one has arrived already.

Aftermath

When the Wall of Flesh is killed, it leaves its drops in a floating, fully enclosed box of Demonite Bricks, so there is no danger of these drops falling into Lava. One of the drops is always the Pwnhammer, which becomes an essential tool.

After defeating the Wall, your world will enter Hardmode, and you will face tougher enemies. This will also generate two diagonal stripes of The Hallow and The Corruption in a "V" pattern that starts from the center of The Underworld all the way to the Surface (and higher, as Floating Islands in these paths will also be converted).

Notes

  • Take care to avoid accidentally having a Voodoo Demon drop its doll into Lava, as this will also summon the Wall of Flesh.
  • If you have problems getting the Guide to spawn, make sure there isn't a Goblin Army invasion or Frost Legion in progress on the map. Also, it also must be Day time for the Guide to respawn.
  • If either of the two diagonal stripes of The Hallow or The Corruption pass through an Underground Jungle, they will not overwrite it; this can cause small Hallows or Corruptions to spawn.
  • Despite being a Boss, the Wall of Flesh does not spawn the Dryad upon death. You will still have to kill one of the three other Bosses to spawn the Dryad in a new world.
  • If the Wall of Flesh reaches the end of the Underworld, any players still fighting the Wall will be killed, with the unique Death message, "(PLAYER NAME) was licked."
Bug: On worlds where there is a large space between areas, if a player dies from the Wall of Flesh, the Wall of Flesh's Eyes, Mouth, and Hungry will rise out of the Underworld with out the Wall itself, and you will not get the Horrified or the The Tongue debuffs. The Wall of Flesh will rise to the top of the map, and can still be defeated.
Bug: The Wall of Flesh may grab you and give you "The Tongue" condition but never let go. It will continue to rise vertically, until it reaches the top of the map. There is currently no known counter to this.
Bug: Sometimes, if the Wall of Flesh's mouth gets caught in a block (because the blocks are too steep for the wall to avoid them) and your character gets The Tongue debuff, rarely, your character will get stuck in those blocks for a while until the wall shoots another tongue.
Bug: If you drop a Guide Voodoo Doll in lava far enough away that you cannot see the lava it lands in, the Wall of Flesh may summon without eyes, mouth and The Hungry and can't be beaten. Any Player(s) behind the invincible Wall, or who leave the Underworld, will instantly be killed as if they were using a Magic Mirror.
Bug: Sometimes the Wall of Flesh does not drop a Pwnhammer. Hardmode will still activate, but another Wall of Flesh will have to be slain to get the hammer.
Bug: Rarely when in a server and when the player fighting the Wall of Flesh dies, all players in the server will be repeatedly killed.
Bug: Sometimes, when you defeat the Wall of Flesh, the message will display and the loot will appear but the mouth and body will still be alive.

Tips

  • Certain consumables such as Water Walking Potion, Gravitation Potion and Obsidian Skin Potion can make traversing the underworld much easier.
  • The Wall of Flesh's spawn location is determined by where the Voodoo Doll was thrown in relation to the center of the world. Thus, you can get the maximum space to fight the WOF by reaching the edge of the world and throwing the doll toward that edge.
  • The use of heart statues is recommended, as they provide hearts (healing 20 HP) every time they are activated.
  • Preparing the Underworld for the battle is advised. Having a reasonably long straight path with no danger of getting stuck or falling can make the battle significantly easier, and can even block lasers from the lower eye if the path is at the proper height.
  • Many bugs are present. In the worst case, the Wall of Flesh will give you "The Tongue" condition, rise up until it reaches of the top of the map, and there's no escape out of this. World backup is recommended.
  • Using rocket boots can be very helpful, as they will allow you to fly over most obstacles that you'll encounter while trying to avoid the Wall of Flesh.
  • Building a platform of Dungeon Brick, then throwing Dynamite at the Wall of Flesh, is an easy way to kill it.
  • Another good strategy is to create about 1,500 Meteor Shots and use a Minishark to shoot it.
  • One thing you can do is to have one player summon and fight the Wall of Flesh in the middle of the map, then have another player enter the Underworld from the other side. The player coming in from behind can attack from a distance, as being far enough won't activate the Horrified debuff or the Tongue.
  • To beat the hungry quickly, use a Reforged Demon Scythe.
  • If you have a long walkway, being in close range and shooting at the Wall of Flesh with a Harpoon (holding attack down so it auto-fires), is a rather efficient way of defeating the Wall of Flesh.
  • If you're willing to spend the time and effort required to obtain it and its Fallen Star ammunition, the Star Cannon can destroy the Wall of Flesh in around 50-70 well-aimed shots.
  • Inflicting multiple sections of the Wall of Flesh with the On Fire! or Cursed Inferno debuffs, using Bows or Repeaters and the appropriate Arrows, will deal damage much more quickly.

Farming

  • If you have a large supply of Guide Voodoo Dolls and are comfortable fighting the Wall without potions, you can once kill the Wall of Flesh repeatedly to collect its drops, which can sell for around 20 each time.

Trivia

Overlapping demonite boxes
  • The treasure box can intersect other blocks or treasure boxes without losing its contents.
  • If you start the battle in the same place multiple times, you can leave the treasure box intact to mark your record time.
  • It is possible to have the box spawn on you if you are overlapping the Wall of Flesh when you kill it.
