The Wall of Flesh (WOF) is a boss located in The Underworld. Beating it will trigger Hardmode.

Summoning it

To summon it, a Guide Voodoo Doll must be thrown (or otherwise fall) into lava in the Underworld; the Guide must also be alive at that point in order to let the summon be successful. With the Guide sacrificed, the Wall of Flesh will spawn and instantly cast the Horrified debuff on all players, which will last until the Wall or the players die.

When you summon it, take note of your position. The Wall of Flesh will appear in the direction of the closest world edge. (If you are east of the center, it will appear to the east, and vice-versa)

Attacks

The Wall of Flesh has several attacks. First, it has tiny mouths called The Hungry, attached by a long vein, which move towards and damages players. As the Wall of Flesh's main body takes damage, these mouths deal more damage, and become more resilient to attack. It is therefore advised to take care of them first. When they've suffered enough damage, they will detach and attempt to fly into players. Furthermore, the wall has eyes that will shoot lasers. As its health decreases, the lasers increase in damage, speed and frequency. Finally, it will spit Leeches out of its mouth, which chase players.

Trying to escape

If a player attempts to escape from the fight by physically going away or if they try to get behind the Wall of Flesh somehow, the mouth will release The Tongue, which will cling onto players, drag them in front of the mouth, and deal a large amount of damage while leaving the player immobilized in the process. If you approach the wall from behind (usually by having died and trying to catch up to the wall in order to finish it), the tongue will only appear if you are in reasonable proximity. If you try to exit the underworld with the Horrified debuff,or try to escape the Wall of Flesh by running,the player will instantly die, with the message <PLAYER NAME> tried to escape. As the damage caused by this effect occurs after the teleportation, when using the Magic Mirror,it can be used as a last-ditch attempt to conserve items/money - they will drop next to the spawn.

Killing it

To destroy the Wall of Flesh you must attack its eyes or its mouth. They all share the same life counter, so shooting at any of the three will deplete the boss' total health. The "wall" itself is transparent to weapons, and shooting the Wall of Flesh there will not deplete its health. Unlike other bosses, this boss does not despawn if you die: it will remain alive until it reaches the other end of the map. Trying to summon it again before it despawns will simply destroy the Guide Doll (and kill the new Guide if one has arrived already).

Aftermath

When the Wall of Flesh is killed, it leaves its drops in a fully enclosed box of Demonite Bricks so they don't fall into lava, although if they did, only the Coins and Hearts would be destroyed.

After defeating this boss, your world will enter hardmode and you will face tougher enemies. This will also generate two diagonal stripes of The Hallow and The Corruption somewhere, usually near your original spawn point. To visualize this, they will form a V or X. These lines extend from The Underworld to the top of the map, therefore creating biomes of Underground Hallow and Underground Corruption. Floating islands can also be turned into Hallowed and Corrupted variants. The generation of these lines effectively means players must take far more extensive measures to stop the spread of biomes.

Notes

  • The Wall of Flesh can be summoned again in hardmode. If, for some reason, you find yourself back in the Underworld, take care to avoid accidentally having a Voodoo Demon drop its doll into lava to avoid an unwanted summon.
  • If you have problems getting the guide to spawn, make sure that there isn't a goblin army invasion or Frost Legion in progress on the map. It also must be day time for the Guide to respawn.
  • If either of the two diagonal stripes of The Hallow or The Corruption pass through an Underground Jungle, they will not overwrite it, this can cause small Hallows or Corruptions to spawn.
  • Despite being a boss, killing the Wall of Flesh does not spawn the Dryad. You will still have to kill an easy mode boss to get a Dryad in a new world.
  • If the Wall of Flesh were to reach the end of the Underworld, any players still fighting the wall will be killed and the unique death message "(PLAYERNAME) was licked." will appear for all slain players.

Tips

  • Certain consumables such as Water Walking Potion, Gravitation Potion and Obsidian Skin Potion can make traversing the underworld much easier.
  • The Wall of Flesh's spawn location is determined by where the Voodoo Doll was thrown in relation to the center of the world. Thus, you can get the maximum space to fight the WOF by reaching the edge of the world and throwing the doll toward that edge.
  • The use of heart statues is recommended, as they provide hearts (healing 20 HP) every time they are activated.
  • Preparing the underworld for the battle is advised. Having a reasonably long straight path with no danger of getting stuck or falling can make the battle significantly easier, and can even block lasers from the lower eye if the path is at the proper height.
  • Many bugs are present (In the worst case the Wall of Flesh will give you "The Tongue" condition, rise up until it reaches of the top of the map, and there's no escape out of this!) so world-backup is recommended.
  • Using rocket boots can be very helpful, as they will allow you to fly over most obstacles that you'll encounter while trying to avoid the Wall of Flesh.
  • Building a platform of dungeon blocks then throwing dynamite at the Wall of Flesh is an easy way to kill it.
  • Another good strategy is to create about 1500 meteor shots and use a minishark to shoot it.
  • One thing you can do is to have one player fight the Wall of Flesh in the middle of the map and one on either sides to shoot at the Wall of Flesh because if you're far enough behind you wont get the horrified or tounge debuff.
  • To beat the hungry quickly, use a reforged Demon Scythe.
  • If you have a long walkway, being in close range and shooting at the wall of flesh with a harpoon(holding attack down so it auto-reuses) is a rather efficient way of defeating the Wall of Flesh.
  • If you're willing to spend the time and effort required to obtain it, the Star Cannon can destroy the wall of flesh in around 50-70 well aimed shots.

Trivia

Overlapping demonite boxes
  • The treasure box can intersect other blocks or treasure boxes without losing its contents.
  • If you start the battle in the same place multiple times, you can leave the treasure box intact to mark your record time.
  • It is possible to have the box spawn on you if you are overlapping the Wall of Flesh when you kill it.
