The Molten Fury is the strongest of the pre-hardmode Bows in the PC version. Wooden Arrows shot from this bow automatically turn into Flaming Arrows. This does not downgrade or set Unholy Arrows or Jester's Arrows on fire. The fire effect does not increase damage; however, it does cause the arrows to shed light and has a chance to light enemies on fire. Wooden arrows fired still have a chance to drop as Flaming Arrows.
