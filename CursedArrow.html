Cursed arrows are a type of arrow that have a chance to apply the Cursed Inferno debuff. Unlike Flaming Arrows, they do not go out when they hit water.

As with most other arrows, Cursed Arrows also have a chance not to break. When fired, they have a 1/6 chance to drop and can then be recovered. However, 2/3 of the time it will drop as a normal Wooden Arrow instead of a cursed one.
