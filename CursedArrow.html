Cursed arrows are a type of arrows that have a chance to apply the Cursed Inferno debuff. Unlike Flaming Arrows, they do not go out when they hit water.

As with most other arrows, Cursed Arrows also have a chance not to break and therefore to be collectible again. However, sometimes it will be just a normal Wooden Arrow instead of a Cursed one.
