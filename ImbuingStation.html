This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Imbuing Station is a crafting station that allows you to create Flasks. Flasks imbue melee weapons (which includes Boomerang-type weapons and any other weapon that inflicts melee damages) with special abilities for limited periods of time, although only one special effect will be active at any time, with the latest imbuement overriding prior non-expired imbuements. It can be purchased from the Witch Doctor.
