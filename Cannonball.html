PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Cannonball is for use with the Cannon, and you left click the cannon to make it shoot, right clicking adjusts the firing angle.It is bought from the Pirate NPC for 15 Silver.

Trivia

Despite being explosive, cannonballs do not inflict self damage.

History

  • 1.2.0.3: Price changed from 10 Silver to 15 Silver.
