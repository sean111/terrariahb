Shadow Orb (item)

Shadow Orb
Statistics
Type Potion Light Source
Max stack 1
Rarity Blue
Use time 19
Tooltip Creates a magical shadow orb
Sell 20

The Shadow Orb creates a slowly moving sphere which follows the player around and emits dim light. The orb can move through anything and casts light when submerged in water, but moves very slowly causing it to fall behind when the player is moving quickly. If it falls far enough behind, it will teleport to the player's location. It can be acquired from destroyed Shadow Orbs.

Controls

The orb can be moved up or down using the respective keys:

  • W - Up
  • S - Down

Notes

  • The Shadow Orb buff will stay active until the player dies, even if the item was removed from the player's inventory. Same applies for Pets, Minions, the Fairy Bell and the Wisp in a Bottle.
