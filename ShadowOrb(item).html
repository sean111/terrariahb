Shadow Orb (item)

Shadow Orb
Statistics
Type Potion Light Source
Max stack 1
Rarity Blue
Use time 19
Tooltip Creates a magical shadow orb
Sell 20

The Shadow Orb, when summoned, slowly follows the player around and emits a dim light. The Shadow Orb can move through any object, but has a very low speed, so it will fall behind when the player is moving quickly. If it falls far enough behind, it will teleport to the player's location.


Orb Movement:
The orb can be moved up or down using the respective keys
W - Up
S - Down

The Shadow Orb can be received from destroyed Shadow Orbs.

Notes

Anomaly: The Shadow Orb buff will stay active until the player dies, whether he keeps it in his inventory or not. Same goes for Pets, Minions, Fairy Bell and Wisp in a Bottle. This is intentional it used to be timed.
