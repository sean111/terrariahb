It takes a total of 60 Meteorite Bars (180 Meteorite) to craft a full suit of Meteor armor.

It consists of a Meteor Helmet, Meteor Suit and Meteor Leggings.

A full set reduces the mana cost of the Space Gun to zero. Each piece of the armor worn increases magic damage by 7%.

If the full suit is visible, the player leaves a trail of flames as they move.

This set, when used in conjunction with any other magical item, such as the Space Gun or Flower of Fire, can create a great casting character.
