This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Candy Corn Rifle is a rare drop from Pumpking. It uses Candy Corn as ammunition which can be purchased from the Arms Dealer after obtaining the weapon. The shots penetrate enemies, and can bounce off the ground and walls much like Spiky Ball Traps. This opens up a lot of damage potential with crowds of enemies. However, gravity affects the bullets quite a bit. While the gun has a high fire rate, the bullets do not stay airborne for very long. The bounce of the bullet may help the projectiles travel further. Candy Corn fired from the rifle will ricochet 4 times, and are destroyed on the fifth time after hitting a solid block, monster, or player in PvP.

Notes

  • The best modifier for the Candy Corn Rifle is Unreal.
  • In terms of DPS, the Candy Corn Rifle has 330 DPS (with candy corn ammo 397.5) compared to the Venus Magnum's 270, but the S.D.M.G. beats it with 350 DPS. However, the piercing ability of the candy corn may help on worm-type mobs such as The Destroyer and crowds of enemies.

Trivia

  • The gun and its ammunition is a reference to the popular Halloween Candy.
  • The weapon may also reference the webcomic Problem Sleuth, where one of the three main character uses the candy corn handgun as his weapon.
