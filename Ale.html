Ale is a potion that gives the player the Tipsy debuff, which increases melee damage, but lowers the player's defense (2 minute duration).
  • -4 defense
  • +2% critical hit on melee attacks
  • +10% melee damage
  • +10% melee attack speed

Because ale only requires mugs as an ingredient, which only takes 2 sand to produce, ale can be sold for 10 copper coins per unit of sand, making it more profitable than farming sand for bottles, although perhaps not as profitable as making potions from those bottles.
