The Book is a crafting material used only to craft the Bookcase and Piano. Books can also be placed, as decoration, on work benches, tables or wooden platforms. Books are obtained from the Dungeon's many shelves, where they can be freed with a Hammer.

Notes

  • The Water Bolt looks like other Books, and is hidden among those on the shelves of the Dungeon. It has a distinct sprite that no ordinary books have.
