The Mythril Drill is an alternative to the Mythril Pickaxe. Appearances and sounds aside, the two tools are functionally identical. However, unlike pickaxes, most drills cannot be reforged with size, speed, or knockback modifiers, making Demonic the best possible prefix. Drax is the one exception, as it can be reforged with knockback, allowing the Godly prefix.

Can mine any block except Chlorophyte Ore and Lihzahrd Bricks.
