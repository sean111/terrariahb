Rotten Chunks drop from Eaters of Souls, Devourers, and Corruptors.

Though the tooltip says it looks tasty, Rotten Chunks cannot be eaten. This is probably a joke.

Rotten Chunks are used much like Vertebrae for worlds that spawn with a Corruption biome instead of Crimson biome.
