Copper armor is the simplest craftable armor set in the game. It takes a total of 60 Copper Bars to craft a full suit, this translates over to 180 Copper Ore to craft all the necessary bars for this armor.

It consists of a Copper Helmet, Copper Chainmail and Copper Greaves.

A full set grants a basic 4 defense plus a set bonus of 2 defense, which equals a total of 6 defense.
