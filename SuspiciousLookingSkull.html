Console-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console version of Terraria.

The Suspicious Looking Skull is used to summon the final boss in the console version of Terraria, Ocram. Ocram is a very difficult boss and the player should prepare themself before the battle, because it will take a long time to regain the lost materials to fight Ocram again. You can't summon another Ocram if there is already one. Crafting the Suspicious Looking Skull takes a total of 29 Souls of Light, 15 Souls of Night, 20 Adamantite Bars (100 Adamantite Ores), 6 Lens, 30 Bones, 15 Copper Bars (45 Copper Ores), and 15 Iron Bars (45 Iron Ores).
