PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Shroomite Bars can be crafted by standing near an Autohammer with a Chlorophyte Bar and 15 Glowing Mushroom. These bars are so far used in the Shroomite armor and the Hoverboard. You need 810 Glowing Mushrooms and 54 Chlorophyte Bars to make the Shroomite armor; For the Hoverboard, you need 270 Glowing Mushrooms and 18 Chlorophyte Bars.
