Mushroom Grass Seeds are a type of Seed that has a chance to drop from harvesting Glowing Mushrooms, and can be used to grow your own Glowing Mushroom farm.

Requirements

Mushroom Grass Seeds must be planted on Mud Blocks in order to grow Glowing Mushrooms (can be planted and will spread above ground. Once the seeds have been planted the blue grass will spread. Mushroom grass does not require water.

Grass

Mushroom Grass spreads much slower than ordinary Grass or Corruption, at a speed comparable to Jungle Grass, which is only 3-5 tiles per day for each continuous section of mud blocks. The blue grass, as well as the mushrooms that grow on it, give off a nice level of light; less than a torch but enough to see clearly (when placed against a solid wall, the light will penetrate 2 solid blocks).

An interesting thing to note is that Mushroom Grass appears to spread quicker above ground.

Giant mushrooms

Giant glowing mushrooms can also spawn in player-grown Glowing Mushroom farms. They also require the same amount of vertical clearance as trees: 15 tiles.

The requirements for trees means less mushroom yield, since mud strips can't be placed densely on top of each other with just 1 tile of clearance as usual. Nevertheless, trees are aesthetically pleasing, have a high chance of dropping more Mushroom Grass Seeds, as well as multiple glowing mushrooms each, so they may be worth the trouble for some players. Giant glowing mushroom must be chopped down with an Axe. The giant glowing mushrooms are the easiest way to obtain mushroom grass seeds and if the ceiling is made large enough in a glowing mushroom seed farm, the may grow by them selves much more easily.

Purple Dye

Mushroom Grass Seeds can only be used to create Purple Dye on the Console Version.

Notes

  • The Mushroom Grass Seeds will be sold by the Dryad if she's moved into a Mushroom Biome.
  • It is possible to "convert" Jungle Grass Seeds into Mushroom Grass Seeds if the planted jungle grass is sprayed with Dark Blue Solution. This will turn the jungle grass into mushroom grass. This can be convenient if you've got a surplus of Jungle Grass Seeds and want Mushroom Grass Seeds instead.
