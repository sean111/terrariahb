Sunglasses are a vanity item equipped in the Social Helmet slot, and can be worn without losing set-bonuses from your armor (except for afterimages). Sunglasses will only change the appearance of your character.

Sunglasses do not offer any bonus stats to your character. Like Goggles, these do not cover the player's hair, allowing you to hide your helmet to show your hair. It can be noted that if you're inflicted with the On Fire! or Cursed Inferno Debuff, wearing the Sunglasses appears to lessen the particle effect on your character. This does not lower the amount of damage it does. Sunglasses currently cannot be dyed
