The Angler Fish is a water enemy which spawns in caves and in the jungle biome, but only after Hardmode has been activated.

The Angler Fish swims faster than some other fish when chasing the player. It has a chance of inflicting the bleeding debuff when it attacks. It also has a small light bulb which emits a very small amount of light.
