Crystal Bullet is an ammunition item crafted with 25 Musket Balls, and 1 Crystal Shard on the Mythril Anvil. These bullets shatter on impact with an enemy or solid block, splitting into 3 small shards that have limited range and deal 60% of the original bullet's damage. These shards are the crystal shards referred to in the tooltip; the bullet does not generate Crystal Shards.

Notes

  • The shards emitted by the crystal bullet on impact can damage enemies through walls.
  • When using this ammunition with the Shotgun, each projectile will split into shards on impact, potentially causing a great deal of secondary damage.
