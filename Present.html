Presents are special drops available only during the Christmas holiday season (December 15th-31st), that have a chance to be dropped by any enemy. They exist in three colors: Blue, Green and Yellow.

Content

  • Candy Cane Block - can be made into Candy Cane Wall
  • Green Candy Cane Block - can be made into Green Candy Cane Wall
  • Snow Globe - Summons the Frost Legion

History

  • 1.1.1: Introduced.
