The Mythril Chainsaw is an axe-type tool crafted using Mythril Bars. You can only damage enemies with the actual saw part of the chainsaw. Enemies at close quarters will not be damaged. Chainsaws are effective against the Wall of Flesh for their fast attack rate and high damage. On the console version, it is used to craft the Hamdrax. Following version 1.2 on PC, this is no longer the case.
