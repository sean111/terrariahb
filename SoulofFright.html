Souls of Fright are dropped by Skeletron Prime, the hardened version of Skeletron. The most notable thing that Souls of Fright are used for is the Flamethrower. To craft all of the recipes using Souls of Fright, a player must aquire 85 of them, meaning that they must beat Skeletron Prime 3-5 times.
