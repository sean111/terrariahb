The Unholy Trident is a magic weapon dropped by Red Devils in The Underworld during Hardmode. It fires fast magic tridents that pierce up to 4 enemies, but with a very slow firing rate. This weapon is very useful when fighting small waves on a flat area. The best Modifier possible is Mythical.
