The Armored Skeleton is an underground hardmode monster that resembles a skeleton in armor.

They have a chance to induce the Broken Armor debuff.

Notes

  • It is extremely inadvisable to enter the cavern layer before you have a set of Hardmode Armor, such as Cobalt or Palladium, due to these enemies being very common and having high attack and defense.
  • As of 1.2, Heavy Skeletons never spawn anymore, and are replaced by Armored Skeletons. This is due to the fact that certain enemies can now have different sizes, along with slightly changed stats, like defense, health, and damage. Prior to 1.2, Heavy Skeletons were larger versions of Armored Skeletons, with increased defense and damage.

History

  • 1.2.0.3: Now has a chance to drop its respective banner.
  • 1.2:
    • Health, damage and defense reduced from 340, 60 and 36 to 260, 40 and 28 respectively.
    • Now has a chance to drop Armor Polish and/or a Beam Sword.
  • 1.1: Introduced.
