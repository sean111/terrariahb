Environment Cavern
AI Type Slime
Damage 20
Max Life 90

The largest of ordinary slimes, the Mother Slime starts spawning when you reach the Cavern layer. They spawn 2-3 Baby Slimes upon death, and like all Slimes, drop coins and sometimes Gel.

Mother Slimes are immune to the Poisoned debuff.

Notes

  • Similarly to Baby Slimes and Black Slimes, the Mother Slime blends slightly with the background of the Cavern layer, making it difficult to detect. As such, it can often catch players off-guard.
  • Explosive weapons, especially Grenades work well when fighting mother slime. The explosion will almost certainty damage/kill the baby slimes it spawns upon death.
