Buffs

Buffs are special stats or visual bonuses which are granted to the player after consuming certain items. They last anywhere from 2 minutes to 10 minutes. Currently active buffs are shown below the hotbar, along with their remaining duration. You can right click the buff icon whenever to cancel it. Non-cancellable buffs are usually negative in effect and called debuffs.

If you die with any active buffs, they will be canceled out and need to be re-applied on respawn.

You can have up to 10 buffs active at once. After that, when you try to activate another buff, it cancels out the left-most buff in the bar. Activated buffs carry over from server-to-server, and from world-to-world.

Buffs can be easily activated with the buff hotkey, defaulted to B. When pressed, the first 10 buff-granting consumables (starting from the top-left) are used, giving the player their respective buffs. Using the hotkey while a particular buff is still active will not re-activate it or re-stack it.

History

  • Console-Release: Pet Slime,Pet Guinea Pig,Pet Bat,Pet Zombie,Pet Tiphia and Pet Werewolf buffs introduced.
  • 1.1.2: Bunny Buff introduced.
  • 1.1: Fairy, Clairvoyance, Merfolk, and Werewolf buffs introduced.
  • 1.0.6:
    • Orb of Light buff introduced to replace the Orb of Light spell.
    • Well Fed buff added.
    • Many buff durations adjusted.
  • 1.0.5: Introduced. 18 buffs added.
