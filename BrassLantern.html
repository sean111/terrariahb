A Brass Lantern is a small hanging Light Source much like the Chain Lantern. They are found in Blue Dungeons and cannot be crafted. They are placed like Chandeliers but take up much less space, which makes them ideal for small rooms or passages. Like all light sources, they can be toggled on/off with wires.
