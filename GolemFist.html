The Golem Fist is one of the many drops from the Golem. Functionally, it is an upgrade to the KO Cannon, firing a large fist identical to the one the Golem uses. It will fire forward at a rapid rate (Like the Harpoon, but in a straight line), and fighting closer to enemies is ideal for maximum damage with the fist. Neither its speed nor size can be modified, therefore the best prefix for the Golem Fist is Godly.
