This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Spectre Pickaxe is an endgame pickaxe, obtainable by crafting 18 Ectoplasms along with 18 Chlorophyte Bars at a Mythril Anvil. It is one of the best pickaxes in the game (second only to the Picksaw), being able to mine every ore and block except the Jungle Temple's bricks. It has the same power as the Drax, and has a bonus of +3 in range. Unlike the Drax, it cannot be used as an axe.

Notes

  • The Spectre Pickaxe's best modifier is Legendary.
  • Quick-Select will not switch to the Spectre Pickaxe if the targeted block is inside the increased range but outside the standard pickaxe-range. After the pickaxe is selected, further blocks can be mined. (1.2.0.2)
  • The Spectre Pickaxe is roughly 20% slower than the Drax.
Bug : May be dropped by Skeletron.

History

  • 1.2.1: Added requirement of Chlorophyte Bars to craft.
  • 1.2: Introduced.
