Console/Mobile-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console version or mobile version of Terraria.
Not to be confused with Honey Comb.

The Honeycomb summons a pet Tiphia when used. It grants the pet Tiphia buff that lasts until the player manually cancels it by right-clicking the buff's icon, or dies.

It can be obtained from Golden Chests.
