The Umbrella is a held item that can be found in surface chests. When used, the umbrella appears above your character and allows the player to fall at a slower rate. It prevents fall damage, and replicates the effect of the Featherfall Potion.

Trivia

  • Is likely a reference to the Nintendo game "Kirby". In said game you can obtain an umbrella of the same design, with the same usage.
  • Could also be a reference to Mary Poppins where in the beginning she flew in the wind with her umbrella.
