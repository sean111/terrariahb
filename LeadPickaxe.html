This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Lead Pickaxe is a second-tier pickaxe, being an alternative to the Iron Pickaxe. Like all pickaxes, it is used to mine a majority of blocks and ores. The Lead Pickaxe mines somewhat faster than a Copper/Tin Pickaxe. It also has slightly more raw digging and attack power than an Iron Pickaxe, as well as a slightly faster swing speed.
