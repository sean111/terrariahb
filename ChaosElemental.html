The Chaos Elemental is an enemy that spawns in the Underground Hallow. This enemy is capable of teleporting, not unlike enemies such as the Fire Imp and Goblin Sorcerer, but at a much more rapid rate, and these teleportations will normally be aimed at getting in melee range of the player, rather than staying away from him.

Notes

  • Chaos Elementals will teleport anywhere in a random position within 20 tiles from their target, but at least 4 tiles away, and only into a space that is at least 4 blocks tall and 3 blocks wide. They will not teleport into lava, although they can teleport above it and immediately fall in.
  • Chaos Elementals will not spawn near NPCs Towns.
  • Chaos Elementals are immune to the Cursed Inferno debuff.
Bug: Chaos Elementals can spawn in non-Hardmode worlds, if Pearlsand or Pearlstone blocks are placed in the cavern layer
Bug: Chaos Elementals that walk into a space that's 3 squares high and then try to jump will fall one block down into the floor, which can lead to them falling entirely through a one block thick floor.
