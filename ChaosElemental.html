The Chaos Elemental is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Underground Hallow. It runs around and teleports quickly and chaotically (hence the name), attempting to damage the player by making contact.

Like many Underground Hallow enemies, Chaos Elementals have a glowing pink afterimage.

Notes

  • Chaos Elementals will teleport anywhere in a random position within 20 tiles from their target, but at least 4 tiles away, and only into a space that is at least 4 blocks tall and 3 blocks wide. They will not teleport into Lava, though can easily fall into it after teleporting.
  • Chaos Elementals will not spawn near NPCs Towns.
  • Chaos Elementals are immune to the Cursed Inferno debuff.

Trivia

  • The Chaos Elemental is currently the only enemy capable of traveling via both teleportation and normal movement. All other teleporting enemies follow the Caster AI, which does not allow them to walk or jump. The Chaos Elemental also differs from Casters in that it does not fire ranged projectiles.
Bug: Chaos Elementals that walk into a space that's 3 squares high and then try to jump will fall one block down into the floor, which can lead to them falling entirely through a one block thick floor.
Anomaly: Chaos Elementals can spawn in non-Hardmode worlds if Pearlsand or Pearlstone blocks are placed in the Cavern layer.
