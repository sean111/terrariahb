Trivia

  • In real life, diamonds are a clear and usually colorless gemstone. In Terraria both the rocks they are mined from and the mined gems are cyan blue, mostly to increase their visibility. This suggests that diamonds in Terraria contain boron, as boron absorbs lower-energy red light, the light that streams through has a blue hue. Despite this, the two color specific items that can be crafted from it appear white.
