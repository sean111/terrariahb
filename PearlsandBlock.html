Pearlsand Blocks are the Hallowed form of Sand Blocks. Pearlsand can spawn Light Mummies and Hallow enemies, while normal sand only spawns normal Mummies and Desert enemies.

During Hardmode, any hallowed block will turn regular Sand Blocks that are up to three tiles away into Pearlsand.

Pearlsand can also be used as Ammunition for the sandgun.

Notes

  • Pearlsand, just like Ebonsand, cannot be used to make glass. Keep this in mind as the Corruption and Hallow slowly eat away at your Ocean and Desert Biomes if you intend to make potions in Hardmode.
  • Vile Powder can convert Pearlsand back to Sand, while Purification Powder converts Ebonsand similarly.
