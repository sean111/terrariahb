This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Abeemination is used to summon the Queen Bee. It can only be used in the jungle biome. Attempting to use it outside of the Jungle will have no effect and will not consume the item.It appears to be the crafting recipe's ingredients loosely formed into the shape of a bee.

Trivia

  • The name Abeemination is a portmanteau of the words "abomination" and "bee".
  • Abeemination uses all 3 blocks made by liquids colliding in its crafting recipe, those being honey block, crispy honey block, and obsidian.
