This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Staff of the Frost Hydra (also known as the Hydra Staff) is a magic weapon that has the ability to summon a stationary Frost Hydra for a minute at the cost of 20 mana, which has ranged summon attack with a base damage of 100, acting as a turret. Once cast, the player can switch to another weapon and the Hydra will remain. Unlike true minion summoning weapons the staff only supports one Frost Hydra at any given time, and it does not count towards the minion cap, but trying to summon a second Hydra will cause the first to disappear regardless of other minions. The high damage and tracking nature still makes the staff a valuable extra weapon.

The Hydra Staff is only found in a Frozen Dungeon Chest within the Dungeon, which must be unlocked with its corresponding Dungeon Key.

The best prefix for this weapon is "Mythical".

Notes

  • Hydra does summon damage, that means while it will use magic weapon bonuses it wouldn't activate, for example, the Spectre armor bonus.
  • The Tiki and Spooky Wood sets cannot increase the amount of Frost Hydras you can summon.
  • But the Spooky armor and the Papyrus Scarab both still boost the summon damage of the Frost Hydra.
  • The Leaf Crystal turret from wearing Chlorophyte armor will share aggro with the hydra, even if out of range of the targeted enemy. This means that when the hydra fires on an enemy, the leaf crystal will fire on the same enemy.
  • The Hydra can be placed in blocks and will drop to the lowest block below it when summoned into a cavern and could be used to hunt for unexplored caverns.
  • The Frost Hydra will not shoot at players in PvP, but if a player makes physical contact with the hydra, they will take damage equal to a hydra projectile. As the hydra can be placed anywhere a player can click, barricading oneself and/or hiding and placing Frost Hydras on opponents is a viable PvP strategy.
  • It can be used at a start of a battle to add some more firepower due to its long but not infinite summon period.
  • The Hyrda Staff is useful for invasions, such as a Solar Eclipse. You could leave the Hydra alone, then come back later to collect all the items the mobs dropped.

Trivia

  • The Hydra closely resembles a frozen version of Perfect Chaos from Sonic Adventure.
