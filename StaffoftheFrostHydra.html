This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Staff of the Frost Hydra (also known as the Hydra Staff) is a magic weapon that has the ability to summon a stationary Frost Hydra for a minute at the cost of 20 mana, which has ranged summon attack with a base damage of 100, acting as a turret. Once cast, the player can switch to another weapon and the Hydra will remain. Unlike true minion summoning weapons the staff only supports one Frost Hydra at any given time, and it does not count towards the minion cap, but trying to summon a second Hydra will cause the first to disappear regardless of other minions. The high damage and tracking nature still makes the staff a valuable extra weapon. Frost Hydra will vanish 60 seconds after casting.

The Hydra Staff is only found in a Frozen Dungeon Chest within the Dungeon, which must be unlocked with its corresponding Dungeon Key.

The best prefix for this weapon is "Mythical".

Notes

  • The remote capabilities of the Frost Hydra make it useful for battles and invasions, since it can be placed and left alone to slay enemies while the player does other things, such as making purchases, hiding, or fighting enemies in a different location.
  • Despite being able to get magic weapon Modifiers, the Frost Hydra deals summon damage, meaning it will not get the bonuses of being a magic item, but rather gets bonuses from items that refer to Minions.
  • A player can only ever have one Frost Hydra at any time, even when equipped with items that increase your max number of minions.
  • The leaf crystal from Chlorophyte Armor will target and fire at the same enemy as the Frost Hydra.
  • When the Hydra is placed in blocks, it will drop to the lowest block below it. This can be used to find unexplored caves underground.
  • The Frost Hydra will not shoot at players in PvP, but will still deal melee damage to them.
Bug : When using the Gravitation Potion, aim coordinates for summoning a hydra are inverted.

Trivia

  • The Hydra's attack will not trigger the healing effect of Specter Armor.
