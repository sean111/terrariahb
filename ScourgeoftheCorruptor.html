The Scourge of the Corruptor is a weapon always found inside the Dungeon's Corruption Chest, which in turn must be unlocked with a Corruption Key. When used, it fires a fast projectile dealing full damage, which will then split into 2-4 small Eaters of Souls on impact against a solid block or an enemy. The eaters have homing capabilites, can travel approximately 700 feet and bounce off walls up to 5 times before disappearing, and deal approximately half of the weapon's damage each. Using the Scourge costs neither mana nor ammunition, and both the main projectile and Eaters are unaffected by gravity and non-solid blocks. The latter have almost no knockback, but in great numbers can keep enemies "locked" in place.

While the player could consider it a ranged weapon at first sight due to its function, it is treated in-game as a melee weapon, just like Boomerangs since version 1.1 (many swords introduced in 1.2 also have ranged capabilities). Therefore, this weapon benefits from armor sets and accesories giving bonuses to melee, such as Turtle armor and the Fire Gauntlet, and not from sets giving ranged bonuses such as Shroomite armor. This also makes it valuable as a fall-back ranged weapon for melee-oriented characters.

As a result of its characteristics, this weapon is a lot stronger than it would seem from its stats alone: even if the player has bad aim, this weapon can quickly create a swarm of homing projectiles at no cost, allowing them to concentrate on dodging enemy attacks. However, the eaters' homing capabilities do not include pathing around obstacles, so the Scourge is best used in relatively open areas. Still, they can be used to great effect in caves and the Dungeon to attack around corners, by shooting at the intermediate wall from a safe place and letting the Eaters do the dirty work.

Notes

  • The word "javalin" (javelin) is misspelled in the tooltip.
  • Every accessory, armor, potion and Flask that affects melee weapons works on the Scourge of the Corruptor as well.
  • When using a full Frost armor set, both the main projectile and all eaters (although only when homing) will be affected, having blue flames, emitting light, and inflicting Frostburn.
  • Eaters will attack Mimics even before they break their disguise.
  • When shot at a one-tile thick wall from the right or below, there is a chance for the Eaters to spawn on the other side of it.
  • The best prefix for this weapon is "Godly".
  • Attack speed has no effect on the rate at which this weapon is fired, even though it changes the rate of the firing sound.
  • Tiny eaters cannot break cobwebs.
  • Anomaly: Despite the best prefix being "Godly", "Legendary" is capable of being placed upon it and is fully supported by the weapon.
  • Bug : Firing the Scourge of the Corruptor between the directions of 8 o'clock and 1 o'clock will very commonly cause the tiny eaters to pass through 1 block thick surfaces. Note that this doesn't happen when shooting in any other direction. (Please verify for other directions)
  • Bug : On rare occasions, firing the Scourge of the Corruptor in the direction of 11 o'clock at a 1 block thick surface will cause one of the tiny eaters to get stuck in the door or wall and remain stationary until it disappears (which is several seconds later).
