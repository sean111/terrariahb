The Fish Bowl is a vanity item equipped on the Social Helmet Slot. It can be crafted using two Goldfish and a Bottled Water.

When placed in the head equipment slot rather than the social slot, the player loses breath as if underwater. Using the Breathing Reed while wearing it will negate this effect. The Gills Potion will allow for normal breathing when wearing the bowl.

Notes

Wearing the Fish Bowl
  • When you walk, the two fish in the bowl spin around in a circle.
  • When the fish bowl is placed on a mannequin, you can slightly see the player's head, as if it was worn by the player
  • If your skin is, for example, green, your head through the fish bowl will still be seen as normal skin colour.
Bug 1.2.1.2: The fish in the bowl won't swim around while the player is holding a Torch/Glowstick, using a tool or weapon, or jumping during a Blood Moon.

History

  • 1.0.4: Introduced.
