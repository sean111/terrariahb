Mechanical Skull

Mechanical Skull
Statistics
Type Summoning
Max stack 20
Rarity Orange
Use time 44
Tooltip Summons Skeletron Prime

The Mechanical Skull is used to summon Skeletron Prime, a harder version of Skeletron. It can only be used during night time. Skeletron Prime must be defeated before the break of dawn or it will hit you for 9999 damage.

Notes

Bug: You can summon Skeletron Prime again after killing only his Prime Cannon, without having to kill his head.
  • The Back of the Skull shows a wind-up key, which is a reference to many toys that require such key to be activated.
  • The Mechanical Skull's texture is based off of the original Skeletron's head.
