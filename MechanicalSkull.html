Mechanical Skull
Statistics
Type Summoning
Max stack 20
Rarity Orange

Tooltip Summons Skeletron Prime

The Mechanical Skull is used to summon the Skeletron Prime (hardened version of Skeletron). It can only be used during nighttime and Skeletron Prime must be defeated before the break of dawn.
