Not to be confused with the Amber Mosquito.

Amber is a Gem. Besides Orange Torches, Amber cannot be used to make anything else the other gems can be made into, such as Grappling Hooks or Phaseblades, although it can be placed as a decoration like the other gems.

Unlike other gems, amber cannot be mined, and can only be acquired by feeding silt and slush to an Extractinator.
