Spectral armor is a top tier magic armor.

It consists of a Spectral Headgear, Spectral Armor and Spectral Subligar.

A full set grants a basic 40 defense, 150 increased maximum mana, 30% increased magical damage, 33% reduced mana use, 10% increased movement speed, and 25% magical critical strike chance.

Trivia

  • The Headgear is a recolored version of the Mythril hood, the armor is a recolored version of the robe and the subligar is a recolored version of the plumbers pants.

Notes

  • Spectral Armor is considered the sorcerer variant of the top tiers of console version armors.
  • Spectral Armor has a chance to be dropped by Ocram.
  • Curiously, although it is the "caster" variant of the top-tier armors, the Spectral Subligar's defense (15) is higher than that of the "warrior" variant (Dragon Greaves, at 14 defense).
  • Spectral Armor sells for less money than a third of the ingredients involved potential gold.
