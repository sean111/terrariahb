A Silver Bar is an item made of 4 Silver Ore. It is used to craft many items which include, but are not limited to, Silver tools, weapons and armor. For those that have made everything that needs silver you should know that nothing that you can make (including bars) will sell to merchants for more than you would get from the raw Silver Ore.
