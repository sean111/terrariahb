Type Crafting material

Silver Bars are the third tier of Bars. Most Silver items require that Silver Ore be crafted into Bars first. They are crafted using 4 Silver Ore at a Furnace.

Notes

  • Gold and Silver are worth more when sold as Gold Ore and Silver Ore instead of Bars.
