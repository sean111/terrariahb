Silver Bars are early game metal bars, used mainly to create the Silver tier of equipment. Its alternate ore equivalent is the Tungsten Bar. It can also be placed on the ground, like furniture.

Notes

  • Like other bars, Silver Bars may be placed like furniture, taking up 1 block of space. They can be placed either on the floor, or on top of other bars.
  • Silver, being nearly as strong as Gold and significantly more common, makes an excellent alternative during the early stages of the game.

History

  • 1.2: Now placable.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
