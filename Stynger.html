This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Stynger is a ranged weapon that drops off the boss Golem. The ammo it uses is Stynger Bolt which initially drops off the Golem as well, and can later be purchased from the Witch Doctor and Arms Dealer. The shots do an area of effect explosion which will deal damage to anything in the explosion radius. Arching shrapnel is thrown from the explosion, each causing additional explosions smaller than the original but with the same base damage.

Notes

  • The best Modifier available for the Stynger is Unreal.
  • The Stynger counts as a rocket-using weapon for the purpose of Shroomite armor.
  • The shrapnel explosion doesn't damage the shooter, allowing it to be used even point blank.
  • Stynger is surprisingly effective against the very hard boss it drops from, the Golem, being able to defeat it in about 30-120 seconds and 50-200 bolts.
    • It is also highly effective against the Destroyer and other segmented enemies, as shrapnel and explosions hit multiple segments at once.
  • Stynger is extremely effective during the Pumpkin Moon because its shrapnel and high damage slaughters large groups.
  • When used correctly, it can have one of the highest, if not the highest, DPS values in the game.
  • Due to the explosive attacks, the Stynger can often finish off enemies without having to aim too carefully. This is very convenient in situations where the player is stressed, unsure about the target's location (such as in dark areas), or simply wants to get rid of annoying monsters.

Trivia

  • The weapon design and operation could be a reference to the R.Y.N.O. of the Ratchet and Clank Games.
  • The handle portion of the weapon`s texture resembles the Laser Rifle.
