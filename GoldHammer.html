The Gold Hammer is an early game hammer, being stronger than the Silver Hammer, and weaker than The Breaker. It has an alternate ore version, the Platinum Hammer, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all hammers, it is used to remove background walls and create sloped tiles and half tiles. Despite being a tier below The Breaker, it has the same hammer power and is actually faster, making it a more efficient tool, although The Breaker is a better weapon.
