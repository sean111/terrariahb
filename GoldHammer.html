A Gold Hammer is a hammer of the fifth tier and is stronger than a Silver Hammer, but weaker than The Breaker. It is used to remove Furniture items and Walls. Note that the Gold Hammer has the same hammer power and a faster speed than The Breaker, so it is actually more efficient as tool than The Breaker.
