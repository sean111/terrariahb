The Red Hat is a vanity item equipped on the Social Helmet Slot. It has a 100% drop chance from the Clothier NPC.

The player's weapons won't affect NPCs, so one will need to lure an enemy to him or trap him, then kill him with lava. (One can do this easily with a Goblin Army) (Lava will destroy the hat, so be careful unless it's very thin in which case it will kill the Clothier but won't destroy the hat).
