A Poisoned Knife deals 13 damage and can poison enemies. Each 20 Poisoned Knives are crafted using 20 Throwing Knives and 1 Vile Powder. Poisoned Knives behave exactly like Throwing Knives except that they can be thrown slightly longer distances, have a slightly higher damage and a 50% chance to poison the target for 10 seconds.
