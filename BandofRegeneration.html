The Band of Regeneration is an Accessory that can be found in Underground Chests. While equipped, it regenerates 0.5 Health every second, even when the player is moving or being attacked (whereas the player's natural health regeneration slows or stops during those times).

Notes

  • Since players naturally regenerate health, the Band of Regeneration just improves that functionality, so that players will regen faster.
  • Because the player's natural Health regeneration slows or stops during movement or attacks, the Band is useful in that it regenerates Health at a constant rate regardless of those conditions.
  • The Band of Regeneration counters the Bleeding debuff caused by Werewolves and Angler Fish.

History

  • 1.1: Can now be found with prefixes that add various benefits, see the reforging page for more information.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
