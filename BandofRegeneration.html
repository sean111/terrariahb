The Band of Regeneration is an Accessory that can be found in Underground Chests. It regenerates 0.5 health every second.

Notes

  • Since players naturally regenerate health, the Band of Regeneration just improves that functionality, so that players will regen faster, and even if they've just been damaged.

History

  • 1.1: Can now be found with prefixes that add various benefits, see the reforging page for more information.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
