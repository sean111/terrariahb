The Pixie is a Hardmode enemy which hovers a limited height over ground, commonly spawning in The Hallow at the surface layer. It emits a fair amount of flickering light, and can inflict the Slow or Silenced debuffs. It always drops Pixie Dust, a common Hardmode crafting material.

Pixies are immune to all damaging debuffs except Frostburn.

Notes

  • They seem to never touch the ground, making lava traps completely ineffective against them.
  • They are still vulnerable to Traps involving mechanisms, like the Dart Trap, although it will take several shots to kill one.
  • Although they "fly", they are only capable of hovering about 4 tiles above the ground at most, basically limiting them to the same "jumping" range as a zombie. They are therefore vulnerable to pits to waylay them, or, if filled with traps, can easily be farmed for their dust so long as the player keeps an eye out for wraiths.

Trivia

  • The Fairy Bell summons a Fairy which looks similar to a Pixie, and serves as a light source to players.
  • It is possible for a Pixie to drop both the Megaphone and the Fast Clock upon death.
  • The Pixie was inspired by The Legend of Zelda's fairies.
Anomaly: Pixies seem to drop both the fast clock and the megaphone at the same time more often than either one individually
