This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Binoculars are a functional item to be held. They are not an accessory; rather, they take effect when they are held on the cursor or selected on the hotbar.

While held in this way, the view can be scrolled a short distance away from the character by moving the cursor. The view becomes centered not on the player, but on a point almost halfway between the player and the cursor. The minimap is also scrolled. They are a rare drop from the Eye of Cthulhu.

Notes

  • Has the same use as the Sniper Rifle.
  • If you are next to another Biome and you use the Binoculars to look to the biome next to you, the background and music for the previous biome will change according to the biome you are focused on without the player entering the biome themselves.
  • You can see monsters despawn if looking at them from a distance
