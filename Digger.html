The Digger is a wormlike enemy that appears underground in Hardmode. It seems the Digger is Giant worm's bigger brother due to matching textures and similar looking segments.

Bugs

  • As with most worms, sometimes the Digger will spawn with only a head, causing a one-hit kill and no drops.

Notes

  • As with all worm-type enemies, Digger can be usually killed with 2-3, or even one-shot, hits from a spear-type weapon.
  • Much like the World Feeder, if you catch multiple segments with the Cursed Inferno debuff, you can deal extremely high damage very quickly.
