The Silver Axe is an early game axe, being stronger than the Iron Axe, and weaker than the Gold Axe. It has an alternate ore version, the Tungsten Axe, which is actually slightly stronger. Like all axes, it is used to cut down trees, cacti and giant glowing mushrooms.
