The Dual Hook is an upgraded version of the Grappling Hook. It is one of the items that a Mimic can drop. With it you can shoot two hooks, one at a time, or both in quick succession. When one hook is already attached, firing the other hook will not detach the first one until it attaches. This means that if you attempt to grab a surface and miss, you will not fall off the surface you are already attached to. One hook has a red light, while the other has a blue light, both producing dim illumination. The blue hook will always be shot first, unless it is attached to a wall already. There can be two moving grapples at the same time. This can be used to re-shoot a grapple to prevent fall damage if the first misses. It also produces light, refueling the shine effect of Mythril armor and higher (making cave exploring much easier).

Because the Ivy Whip can't shoot more than one vine at a time, having three hooks has little noticeable benefit over the Dual Hook. However, only the Ivy Whip can suspend you between two walls, especially useful when mining close to lava, Meteorite, Hellstone, or Spikes.It also makes light when on a vertical 1X1 block and hanging under the Block.

The Dual Hook's range is 29 blocks, slightly longer than the Ivy Whip.

Notes

Bug: When using the item via the hotbar and clicking, firing the red hook renders the player unable to cast either hook again without dropping from the block grappled to. This is not affected when using the grapple hotkey.
Bug: The dual hook may sometimes pull the user through passages normally too small to fit through, if the player was able to grapple a block on the other side.
