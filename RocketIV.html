This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Rocket IV is a type of Ammunition sold by the Cyborg during a Solar Eclipse. Rocket IVs have a large blast radius (Slightly smaller than Dynamite) and, unlike other rockets, will destroy tiles, and should therefore be used with care.

Tips

  • If used in collaboration with the Grenade Launcher and ranger type accessories and armor, they can cause large amounts of damage.
  • Since Rocket IV's blast radius is so large, it's currently the fastest way of mining if it's used with the Rocket Launcher.
  • This is very useful on bosses with multiple parts or have minions.
