Mining armor is the lowest tiered armor set in the game.

It consists of a Mining Helmet, Mining Shirt and Mining Pants. Mining Shirt and Mining Pants are an uncommon drop by Undead Miners. Mining Helmet can be bought from the Merchant.

A full set grants a total of 3 defense (not a set bonus) and 20% increased mining speed.
