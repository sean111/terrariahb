Mining armor is the lowest tiered armor set in the game.

It consists of a Mining Helmet, Mining Shirt and Mining Pants. Mining Shirt and Mining Pants are an uncommon drop by Undead Miners. Mining Helmet can be bought from the Merchant. Despite being the lowest tier armor, the shirt and pants can be very time-consuming to collect, sometimes making them sought after by collectors. Usually, by the time one collects the full set (if they collect the whole set), they will have armor far stronger.

The Mining Helmet itself provides light when worn in the armor slot.

A full set grants a total of 3 defense (not a set bonus) and 20% increased mining speed.
