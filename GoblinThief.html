More advanced unit of the Goblin Army. Follows typical Fighter AI but moves at a higher speed than similar monsters. Approximately one out of every four goblins will be a Thief (23.7% chance to spawn).

Goblin Thieves are between the Goblin Peon and the Goblin Warrior in terms of difficulty. Goblin Thieves can jump quite high, and can open doors faster than other goblins.
