The Spectre Staff is a rare drop from the Ragged Caster, a hardmode dungeon enemy. The staff fires magic-based, homing projectiles that seek to destroy the nearest enemy. It has good burst potential, allowing the player to have a lot of projectiles in mid-air simultaneously. It has also the highest base damage of all magic damage weapons.

Unlike the projectile fired from the Ragged Caster itself, the projectile will disappear upon contact with any solid blocks.

Anomaly: Like all homing projectiles, the Spectre Staff does not work in PvP.
Bug : It can sometimes cause immense lag on servers if fired up and to the left while around multiple mobs.


Notes

  • The best prefix for this weapon is Mythical.
