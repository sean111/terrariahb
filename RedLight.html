Red Light is one of the three light items bought from Santa Claus. It emits a very dim light, and won't be noticeable in a decently lit up area. One advantage lights have over torches is that they can be placed on all four sides of blocks. Other than that, their use is purely aesthetic, although it does work as a light source for houses. Like most light sources, it can be toggled with wires.
