Feather is a crafting material that drops frequently from Harpies. Feathers are used in certain potions and ultimately in Wings, an extremely useful accessory that enables the player to fly temporarily, glide indefinitely, and negate fall damage. Additional types of feathers will drop from Hardmode monsters, allowing other types of wings to be crafted.

Other feathers

  • Giant Harpy Feather is a rare drop from Harpies and may drop along with the normal feathers.
  • Ice Feather drops from Ice Golems that spawn during blizzards (rain in other biomes) in the Snow Biome.
  • Bone Feather drops in the Dungeon.
  • Fire Feather drops from Red Devils in The Underworld.
