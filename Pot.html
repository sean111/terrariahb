Pot

Pot
Statistics

Pots (not to be confused with Clay Pots and Cooking Pot) are a common sight in Terraria. Pots usually appear underground, however pots can be found on the surface, generally only under overhangs or near caves. When smashed with any tool or weapon, they have a chance to drop Coins and/or a random common item. Pots cannot be picked up.

A Pot on the surface (See environment) of Terraria

Loot

The items a pot could contain varies depending on its location.

Surface

  • Lesser Healing Potion (Healing Potion if in Hardmode )
  • 50-150 worth of Copper or Silver Coins
  • 3-10 Wooden Arrows (Unholy Arrows or Silver Bullets if in Hardmode)
  • 1-5 Torches
  • 1-5 Glowsticks (if pot is underwater)
  • 3-10 Shurikens (Grenades if in Hardmode)
  • Special Potions (2.2%)
    • Ironskin Potion
    • Shine Potion
    • Night Owl Potion
    • Swiftness Potion
