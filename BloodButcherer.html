The Blood Butcherer is a sword crafted using Crimtane Bars. It is the Crimson variant of the Light's Bane, and as such can be used as a substitute for forging the Night's Edge. Although being noticeably more powerful than the Light's Bane, having 5 more damage and being bigger, it attacks slower.
