PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Ice Chest is a storage item found only in the Underground Snow Biome.

Loot

The Ice Chest contains one of the following rare items. Besides the common items listed here they have the common items depending on the height they were found: Surface, Underground or Caverns. If found in the Underground or Cavern Layer it contains Ice Torches instead of normal Torches.
