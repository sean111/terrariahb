Gold Bars are the last of the early game metal bars, used mainly to create the Gold tier of equipment. Its alternate ore version is the Platinum Bar. It can also be placed on the ground, like furniture.

Notes

  • Like other bars, Gold Bars may be placed like furniture, taking up 1 block of space. They can be placed either on the floor, or on top of other bars.
  • In real life, gold is a very soft and flimsy metal that would be very unsuitable for making tools or armor. This is even made fun of in one of the Goblin Tinkerer's quotes: "YES, gold is stronger than iron. What are they teaching these humans nowadays?"'
Anomaly: Even though gold and platinum bars and ores are of equal value, platinum coins are worth 100 gold coins.
