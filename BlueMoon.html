The Blue Moon is a Flail weapon that can be found in Dungeon Chests. When used, it will emit light around it. This flail is stronger than the Ball 'O Hurt but weaker than the Sunfury.

Upon reaching its maximum distance the Blue Moon quickly retracts. Like boomerangs and other flails, throwing the weapon behind a line of enemies will force it to retract through them all, dealing damage to every one of them. This is especially effective in dealing with Goblins, clumps of enemies, and large enemies.

The in-game animation of the Blue Moon.

Notes

  • Like most flails, the Blue Moon is a melee weapon even though it can strike enemies at a distance.
Anomaly: Though the Blue Moon is of Green rarity and more damaging than the Ball O' Hurt, (which is Blue rarity) they both sell for 54 .
Bug: A "ghost sword" effect can be observed. There is no visible item in hand, but it can make contact with and damage enemies even when the ball of the flail is out of reach.
Bug: In the console version. there is a "glitch" which is if you jump on a skybridge made of blocks and not platforms and aim down, sometimes it will go through the bridge.

History
