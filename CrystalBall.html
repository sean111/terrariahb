Buy/Sell 10 /2

Purchased from the Wizard for 10 gold. When Placed, if used it provides Clairvoyance Buff, increasing magic ability. The buff lasts for 10 minutes. The Crystal Ball is never consumed when used and any player may use it to give themselves the buff. The buff increases your stats as follows:

  • +20 maximum mana
  • +5% to Magic Damage
  • +2% Magic Critical Hit chance
  • 2% reduced mana usage
