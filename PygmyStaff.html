The Pygmy Staff is a Summoning weapon that summon a pygmy minion that will fight enemies for you using a ranged spear attack. The staff is dropped by Plantera.

The best possible modifier for this weapon is Mythical.

Notes

  • If the player travels fast enough (faster than the Pygmy can run) or the player uses their wings, the Pygmy will start flying after the player while standing, sitting, or hanging on its spear.
  • Using the Slime Staff will replace your pygmy, and vice-versa.
  • When you have the Tiki armor equipped, the number of summoned pygmies increase. The Tiki armor can be purchased from the Witch Doctor, but only when you have the staff in your inventory.

History

  • 1.2.1: Damage increased from 25 to 27.
  • 1.2: Introduced.
