The Archaeologist's suit is a set of vanity items made up of the Archaeologist's Hat, the Archaeologist's Jacket and the Archaeologist's Pants. These items are all supposed to be equipped in the Social slots as they only provide a visual appearance.

While the other two pieces are crafted, the Archaeologist's Hat is dropped by Doctor Bones.

Crafting both the suit and pants requires 150 rotten chunks, or 30 pieces of leather.

It is possible to craft these items in a world that has the crimson instead of corruption by importing from another world or purchasing Corrupt Seeds from the Dryad.

Notes

  • To create both the jacket and pants, 150 rotten chunks are required to make the 30 pieces of leather. (75 rotten chunks each)
  • It is possible to obtain the Archaeologist's suit in a world that spawns with Crimson due to the fact that you can obtain Corrupt Seeds from the Dryad.

Trivia

  • It is very likely that these items are reference to the Indiana Jones series of movies due to word play. (i.e.; Doctor Bones, Doctor Jones), and due to the fact that he is an archaeologist.
  • The hat sells for more than the other clothing like many armor/vanity helmets
  • When the archaeologist's hat is dyed green, it looks like a leprechaun hat
