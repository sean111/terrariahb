The Archaeologist's suit is a set of vanity items made up of the Archaeologist's Hat, the Archaeologist's Jacket and the Archaeologist's Pants. The hat is dropped by Doctor Bones.

These items are all supposed to be equipped in the Social slots as they only provide a visual appearance.

Trivia

  • It is very likely that these items are reference to the Indiana Jones series of movies due to word play. (i.e.; Doctor Bones, Doctor Jones).
  • More specifically, they are modeled after the outfit of the main character from the indie action adventure game Spelunky, who is himself modeled after Indiana Jones.
