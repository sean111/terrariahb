Slimer is a Hardmode enemy that only spawns in The Corruption and Underground Corruption. It starts off as a flying ball of slime, and when "killed" for first time it loses its wings and becomes a groundborne slimer. Slimers are actually two kinds of enemies put together, half bat, half slime, which counts as two sets of health.

Notes

  • The wings on the first form of Slimer are almost, if not an exact, look-a-like to Demon Wings.
Anomaly: When "killed" for the first time, it can drop a heart or star.
Bug : The slimer can spawn without wings and can still fly.
Bug : When it spawns it can die as soon as the player sees it.
