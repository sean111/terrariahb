Chair

A chair is a piece of furniture (can also be used as a crafting station) that can be placed on the ground.It counts as a comfort item, which is one of the required elements in order to make a House for NPCs. Additionally:

  • Table and Chair together form a miscellaneous crafting station, where Watches (and in the console version, the depth gauge) can be made.
  • A Chair and Work Bench are needed to craft Goggles and Sunglasses.

All chairs are 1 block wide, 2 blocks high, and must be placed on top of a solid block or Wood Platform.

Note: Currently all chairs except the Wooden Chair and Toilet are PC-Only items.
