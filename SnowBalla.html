Snow Balla is an enemy in the Frost Legion. It throws snowballs at the player that form Snow Blocks on impact with other blocks, similar to the attack of the Antlion. The Snow Blocks can also spawn in the air if Snow Balla throws it into the sky. If it falls in a pit, it is possible for it to trap itself.

Notes

Bug: When Snow Balla kills you with his projectiles, the death message states you were killed by sand, like if you were hit by an Antlion.
Anomaly: When Snow Balla throws snow into an underground tunnel, sometimes it may snow, very slightly.
