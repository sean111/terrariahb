The Cursed Torch is a Hardmode, green-colored version of the Torch. Unlike a standard Torch, the Cursed Torch can be held while under Water or Lava, and is placeable in both. It produces slightly less light than a standard Torch. Cursed Torches produce green-colored light (in a colored Lighting Mode).

Notes

  • Due to its similar color, the Cursed Torch is easily confused with the Green Torch, though the Cursed Torch's sprite bears a brighter shade of green, both when in use and in an Inventory slot.
  • Cursed Torches produce much more light than Green Torches, which are extremely dim.

Tips

  • Cursed Torches offer a more permanent lighting solution for underwater lighting than Glowsticks, and are slightly brighter.
