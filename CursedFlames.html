This article is about the magic weapon. For the crafting material, see Cursed Flame.

The Cursed Flames is a Hardmode magic weapon that auto-fires ricocheting green fireballs that move quickly, are affected by gravity, and can set enemies on fire with the Cursed Inferno debuff.

Cursed Flames auto-fire at a rate of about four per second. They can ricochet up to four times, dying on their fifth impact. Unlike most ricocheting projectiles, Cursed Flames gain velocity with each ricochet. They are also highly luminous. Fireballs can penetrate enemies, but only once, and will die on their second enemy hit. Cursed Flames can also travel in Water.

Cursed Flames are similar to the Flower of Fire, though with a higher velocity.

Notes

  • The Laser Rifle is more efficient and accurate than the Cursed Flames, but lacks the Cursed Flames' gravity, pierce, and rebound, has lower damage and knockback, and doesn't cause the (almost) un-extinguishable Cursed Inferno debuff.
  • The Flower of Fire deals more blunt damage, and still inflicts the (albeit weaker) On Fire! debuff.
