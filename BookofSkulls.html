The Book of Skulls is a spell dropped by Skeletron. It shoots skulls with a flame trail, causing damage. Upon hitting a wall the skull will disappear.

Notes

  • When fired it seems to have the burning skull sprite as in 1.0.2.
  • Due to its high damage and piercing ability, Book of Skulls remains a viable spell even through early hardmode.
  • The skulls' decent knockback and slow speed means that certain enemies can be "juggled" by a skull, meaning that they can be hit, knocked back, and then hit again by the same skull multiple times before the skull gets off target. This mechanic can be used well while in the dungeon due to the close quarters.
  • The skull can pierce 3 enemies and hit a fourth, hitting the same enemy multiple times does not count towards the 3 pierce limit.
