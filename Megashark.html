The Megashark is a ranged, ammo-based weapon. It is a stronger version of the Minishark, and with 23 base damage it is arguably the strongest bullet-based weapon in the game. The weapon fires at insanely fast speed and has a 50% chance to not consume ammo. It is one of the best weapons for use against hardmode bosses.

Notes

  • The bullets seem to not always align with the barrel of the gun when firing, resulting in a spread of shots.
  • Despite its complex recipe and the almost end-game materials required, the Star Cannon still tops out as the biggest direct damage dealer; the Megashark simply uses cheaper ammo and fires more rapidly. Note that this weapon deals more DPS, however, if you use crystal or cursed bullets, not including the damage reduction from defense.
  • If this weapon is combined with the ranged set bonus for Hallowed Armor or Adamantite Armor, the observed ammo use reduction is multiplicative, and as a result, the Megashark will use 62.5% less ammo under these conditions (i.e., a full stack of 250 bullets will yield about 667 shots on average).
  • The Minishark, a main component in the crafting of the Megashark, is actually worth more than the upgraded gun.
