The Megashark is a ranged, ammo-based weapon. It is a stronger version of the Minishark, and with 24 base damage it is arguably the strongest bullet-based weapon in the game. The weapon fires at insanely fast speed and has a 50% chance to not consume ammo. It is one of the best weapons for use against hardmode bosses.

Notes

  • The bullets seem to not always align with the barrel of the gun when firing, resulting in a spread of shots.
  • Despite its complex recipe and the almost end-game materials required, the Star Cannon still tops out as the biggest direct damage dealer; the Megashark simply uses cheaper ammo.
  • If this weapon is combined with the ranged set bonus for Hallowed Armor or Adamantite Armor, the observed ammo use reduction is multiplicative, and as a result, the Megashark will use 62.5% less ammo under these conditions.
