An Acorn is a kind of Seed used to plant a Tree. Acorns can only be planted on normal green grass and hallow grass, at which point it will create a Sapling.

Acorns are purchased from the Dryad or randomly obtained by cutting down Trees. Corrupt and Jungle Trees, however, do not drop Acorns.

History

  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
