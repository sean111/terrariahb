Acorns are a type of Seed used to plant Trees. Acorns can only be planted on regular green grass, hallow grass, or snow, where they will create Saplings that will grow into Trees, under the right conditions. Acorns are purchased from the Dryad, who may arrive after you defeat a boss, and are randomly dropped when cutting down Trees. Corrupt, Crimson and Jungle Trees, however, do not drop Acorns.

Notes

  • Planting an acorn will grow the matching tree for whatever Biome it is in.

History

  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
