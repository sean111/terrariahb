An Acorn is a kind of Seed used to plant a Tree. Acorns can only be planted on regular green grass, hallow grass or snow, at which point it will create a Sapling.

Acorns are purchased from the Dryad or randomly obtained by cutting down Trees. Corrupt, Jungle and Snowy Trees, however, do not drop Acorns.
