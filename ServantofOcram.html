Servants of Ocram are weak flying enemies that are summoned by Ocram.

Notes

  • During Ocram's second form, Servants of Ocram still spawn.
  • The servants' spikes are extremely lethal when they close in, because the attacking AI is similar to that of Eaters. Once they slow down, they will infinitely spin, even with the Hallowed Armor they can cut you down in seconds.

Tips

  • Because they act much like the Servants of Cthulhu you may want to periodically switch to a close-ranged weapon to deal with them.
