Servants of Ocram are weak flying enemies that are summoned by Ocram.

Tips

Because they act much like the servants of Cthulhu you may want to periodically switch to a close-ranged weapon to deal with them.

During Ocram's second form Servants of Ocram still spawn (Not to be confused with Servants of Cthulu)

Also, in Ocram's second form, when there are many more than in his first form, it is a necessity to kill them, because of the laser storms Ocram casts. A weapon such as a glaive, light disc, or magical harp can keep them away at a distance and collect their hearts.

The servants' spikes are extremely lethal when they close in, because the attacking AI is similar to that of Eaters. Once they slow down, they will infinitely spin, even with the Hallowed set and warding armor, they can cut you down in seconds.
