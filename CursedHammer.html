The Cursed Hammer is a somewhat rare enemy from the Underground Corruption biome. These cursed hammers have a simple attack pattern; they spin several times, then fly straight at the player. Any knockback cancels their attack and forces them to spin again.

Cursed Hammers can inflict the Cursed debuff, like their Hallow counterpart the Enchanted Sword.

Their immunity to lava makes them troublesome when soul farming in the Underground Corruption.


Trivia

  • Most likely a tribute to Castlevania, where swords do the same attack pattern.
  • Cursed Hammers & Enchanted Swords are exactly the same with the exception of appearance & environment.
