This information (or parts of it) applies only to the console and mobile version of Terraria.

Dragon armor is a top tier armor, which resembles a recolored Jungle armor.

It consists of a Dragon Mask, Dragon Breastplate and Dragon Greaves.

A full set grants 60 defense, 15% increased melee damage, 25% increased critical strike chance, 21% increased melee speed and 31% increased movement speed. In earlier versions the Dragon Mask could be found in Golden Chests and is considered one of the rarest armor parts in the game along with its matching breastplate and greaves.

Notes

  • Dragon Armor is a recolored version of the Jungle Armor. This is rather ironic, as the Jungle Armor was meant to benefit predominantly Magic users, while the Dragon Armor benefits predominantly Melee users.
  • Dragon Armor is considered the warrior variant of the top tiers of console version armors.
  • Dragon Armor leaves behind an afterimage similar to the Cobalt, Hallowed, Shadow, Necro armor and the Ninja Clothes Vanity item.

Trivia

This might be a reference to Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, where the best type of melee armor is Dragon Bone/Scale. This may also be a coincidence.

It is a recolored version of Jungle armor, although Jungle armor is for magic but the Dragon armor is for melee
