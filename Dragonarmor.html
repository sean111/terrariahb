Dragon armor is a top tier armor, which resembles a recolored Jungle armor.

It consists of a Dragon Mask, Dragon Breastplate and Dragon Greaves.

A full set grants a basic 60 defense, 15% increased melee damage, 25% increased critical strike chance and 10% increased movement speed. It also gives a 21% increased melee and movement speed set bonus, for a total of 60 defense, 15% increased melee damage, 25% increased critical strike chance, 21% increased melee speed and 31% increased movement speed. Types of dragon armor can be dropped by Ocram or any other various armors.
