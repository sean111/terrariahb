This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Nano Bullets are one of 6 types of craftable ammunition for bullet-firing weapons. They may be crafted at a Work Bench once the Arms Dealer begins to sell Empty Bullets after Hardmode has been enabled.

These Bullets may still be crafted Pre-Hardmode, provided that the player has obtained the necessary items for crafting them.

Shooting an enemy with a weapon using Nano Bullets will give it the confused debuff and cause it to flee, providing that the enemy is not immune.

This item currently does not apply the debuff in player vs. player scenarios.

The cost per round is 23 . This means a stack of 999 costs 2 29 77 .
