The Bone Wand is a Wand that was added in the 1.2 update. It places Bone Blocks at the cost of one bone per block. The placed blocks remain bone blocks even if later mined by a pick and do not return to normal bone. It drops from Angry Bones, Dark Casters and Cursed Skulls in the Dungeon. Despite the name, it does not use any Mana.
