Feral Claws are an accessory that can be found in Chests contained in Shrines that are located in the Underground Jungle. Feral Claws increase your melee Attack Speed by 12%, and stack with all other attack speed bonuses.

Notes

With a good modifier, the feral claws can increase melee attack speed by up to 16%.
