Corrupt Slime is a hardmode enemy which have a chance of inflicting Darkness when attacking and spawns three Slimelings upon death. This enemy is similar to a Mother Slime except it is much stronger. It also drops twice as much gel than other slimes. It is recommended to fight these inside the underground corruption since the Slimelings can drop additional Souls of Night.
