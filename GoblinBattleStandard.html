The Goblin Battle Standard is an item that summons a Goblin Invasion when used. This can be useful for farming Spiky Balls. The Goblin Battle Standard is consumed when used, so one must be crafted for each invasion the player wishes to summon.

As stated below, the Goblin Battle Standard takes Tattered cloth and wood. Tattered cloth is obtained by killing Goblin Scouts which spawn at the end two thirds of the world and wood is obtained by chopping down trees.

Notes

  • Using the Goblin Battle Standard when no players with at least 200 max Health are present, or when a Goblin Army is already approaching, will waste the item and have no effect.
  • Careful visual inspection of the banner shows a faint image of a Goblin Sorcerer.
