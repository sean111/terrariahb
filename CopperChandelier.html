The Copper Chandelier is a piece of Furniture that can be placed on ceilings. It is the equivalent of a tin chandelier for worlds generated with Copper Ore. It functions the same as other chandeliers. It can sometimes be found unlit in underground cabins. It is a valid light source for NPCs.
