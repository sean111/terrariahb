It takes a total of 135 Cobwebs and 150 Bones to craft a full set of Necro armor. It consists of a Necro Helmet, Necro Breastplate, and Necro Greaves. A full set grants a basic 16 defense and a 20% chance to not consume ammo. In addition, each piece increases ranged damage by 5%.

If the full suit is worn, the player leaves an afterimage while running (just like full Shadow Armor). In addition, the sound the player makes when taking certain types of damage will be the same as for a Skeleton.

Notes

  • Its set bonus (20% chance to not consume ammo) can be used in conjunction with the Sandgun to create infinite sand.
  • The afterimage is only visible when no items are worn in the Social Slots, or if the full armor is in the Social Slots.
  • When used with the Megashark it produces a 60% chance not to consume ammo.
Bug: Each Necro armor piece lists a 4% ranged damage increase, but gives a 5% increase.
Bug: The skeletal damage noise does not work when hit with melee damage.
Bug: Afterimage appears to not wear the face part of the helmet.

History

  • 1.1:
    • Amount of bones needed for crafting increased from 90 (25/35/30) to 150 (40/60/50).
    • Graphics variant for female characters added.
  • 1.0.6: Necro armor stats altered:
    • Added ranged damage increase and new set bonus.
    • Removed set bonus of 30% increased movement speed.
    • Defense of all pieces reduced by 1.
  • 1.0.3: Armor graphics changed.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
