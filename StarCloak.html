Star Cloak

Star Cloak
Statistics
Type Accessory
Max stack 1
Rarity 4
Tooltip Causes stars to fall when injured
Sell 2

Dropped by a Mimic. While equipped, stars will fall when injured. The stars that fall are exactly the same attack as the Starfury with the exception of using no mana, and causing around 32 damage, double that of the Starfury's stars.

Note: The stars will fall even if an enemy does not damage you, such as falling damage. The stars will fall even underground.

History

  • 1.1: Introduced.
