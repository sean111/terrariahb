The Trash Can is a storage item which can hold up to 20 items. Trash Cans can only be harvested with a hammer when empty.

Trash Cans are a Chest with a different look.

Tips

  • The trash can requires four times as much iron as a Chest. Therefore, unless used for the different aesthetic effect, chests or Barrels should be used instead.
  • The Trash Can can be used to differentiate relatively "useless" stored items from those of value.

History
