Dungeon Guardian

Dungeon Guardian

Damage 9000

The Dungeon Guardian guards the Dungeon against intruders. If a player tries to break into the Dungeon without killing Skeletron, this enemy will appear and attack. If hit, the Dungeon Guardian's massive damage will slay any player instantly. Deep caves and Corruption chasms near the Dungeon should be avoided until Skeletron is slain, as merely being close to the Dungeon at a certain depth is considered a break-in.

Dungeon Guardians are immune to the Poisoned, On Fire!, and Cursed Inferno debuffs.

Notes

  • The Dungeon Guardian has the same appearance as the Skeletron Head.
  • Originally, Skeletron Head was used to guard the Dungeon. Killing all Skeletron Heads would defeat Skeletron, so the Dungeon Guardian was created to circumvent this unintentional behavior.[1]
  • It's possible to kill the Dungeon Guardian, although it will take almost 9999 hits because of his absurdly high defense. If you try to kill him, it's recommended to use a fast shooting ranged weapon, like the Minishark or Megashark and a Gravitation Potion. High Defense and damage is unimportant as one hit will always kill you and you will only be able to deal 1 or 2 damage maximum, anyways. Killing a Dungeon Guardian is strictly an exercise in masochism and repetitiveness, as it will not drop any loot or money, nor a corpse.
  • Until Skeletron is defeated, Dungeon Guardian will be the only enemy that appears, meaning it is possible to spawn up to the maximum amount of Dungeon Guardians allowed as there are mobs allowed.
  • If you look very closely at some dungeon walls you can see the dungeon guardian.This only works if Skeletron isn't defeated.
