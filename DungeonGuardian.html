Dungeon Guardians are enemies that guard the Dungeon against intruders. If a player enters the Dungeon without first killing Skeletron and travels below zero depth (the horizontal midline of the map), one or more Dungeon Guardians quickly fly at the player, dealing 1,000 damage each, effectively killing any player that they touch instantly. Dungeon Guardians are large, very fast, and travel through all blocks.

Dungeon Guardians are immune to the Poisoned, On Fire!, and Cursed Inferno debuffs.

Notes

  • Deep caves and Chasms near the Dungeon should be avoided until Skeletron is defeated, as there is the risk of accidentally entering the Dungeon by digging.
  • To be considered in the Dungeon, you must be in front of a dungeon wall. Simply having a large number of dungeon bricks on-screen will not count as being in the Dungeon.
  • Killing a Dungeon Guardian is possible, though it would take thousands of hits due to its absurdly high defense. Combined with the fact that you generally need to contend with multiple Guardians at once, victory is extremely unlikely unless you prepare carefully for the encounter.
  • Entering the Dungeon while fighting Skeletron will still spawn the Dungeon Guardian.
  • Another method is to use a teleporter wired to a 1-second timer along with a hall of spike ball traps. When executed properly, the Dungeon Guardian(s) will chase you as you teleport back and forth, taking damage from the spike balls. This method is effortless and saves bullets and gravitation potions.
  • A full set of Frost Armor is also very good, considering the fact that it is not immune to the Frostburn debuff.
  • Using Vampire Knives to shoot more than one knife at a time infinitely may be a good idea, although these will need to be obtained from another Dungeon's Crimson Chest.
  • Using a Black Belt or Master Ninja Gear can save your life on accidental mishaps a ninth (~11%) of the time.
  • The Rod of Discord is capable of teleporting you out of sticky situations, but should not be overused due to the Chaos State debuff.
  • The buff from the Titanium armor can sometimes help if the dungeon guardian hits you after the buff activates.

Trivia

  • Originally, actual Skeletron Heads were used to guard the Dungeon. Killing all Skeletron Heads would count as defeating Skeletron, so the Dungeon Guardian was created to repair this unintentional behavior.[1]
  • If you continue going down into the Dungeon, and manage to dodge the first Dungeon Guardian, more may appear and eventually, quickly kill you due to their immense speed.

Bugs

  • If you teleport out with a Magic Mirror at the exact moment that a the Dungeon Guardian hits you, a death message will be displayed but you will survive.
