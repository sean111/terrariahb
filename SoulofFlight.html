Soul of Flight is a Hardmode crafting material used to craft Wings, Accessories that grant flight and slow-fall. Souls of Flight are dropped by Wyverns.

To craft all 13 sets of Wings, a player must acquire 260 Souls of Flight, so they must kill 26-52 Wyverns. Only one is necessary for flight. Their effects do not stack with each other.

Souls of Flight are dropped at the position of the Wyvern's head upon death, and float in midair rather than fall (like all Souls). This may confuse players as to whether the Souls were dropped, if they kill an offscreen Wyvern, or a Wyvern inside of blocks.
