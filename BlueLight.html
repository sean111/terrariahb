Christmas lights are decorative light sources that resemble Christmas string lights and emit very dim light. They can be placed on all four sides of blocks, and can be toggled with wires. Although too dim to serve as functional light sources, they will fulfill a House's light source requirement. Christmas lights are sold by Santa Claus.

Christmas lights are available in three colors that are each sold separately.
