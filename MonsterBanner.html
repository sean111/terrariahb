Monster Banner

Monster Banners are vanity items that are dropped randomly by various mobs, similarly to Bosses and their corresponding Trophies. Almost every mob in the game drops a banner. Each banner corresponds to the mob it was dropped from, although not every variety of a mob drops its own unique banner. Each banner has a 0.5% chance to drop from its corresponding monster. There are currently 87 different Monster Banners.
