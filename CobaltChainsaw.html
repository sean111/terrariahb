The Cobalt Chainsaw is an axe-type tool crafted using Cobalt Bars. It is the weakest chainsaw. Chainsaws are effective against the Wall of Flesh for their fast attack rate and high damage. Prior to 1.2, it was a material used to craft the Hamdrax, which following the update is now known as the Drax and is no longer used to craft it; it still retains this function on the console version.
