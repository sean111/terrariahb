PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

A Cooked Marshmallow grants the player the Well Fed buff and is created by holding a Marshmallow on a Stick over a Campfire. It takes roughly 10 seconds to cook.

Notes

  • It is the only craftable potion item that cannot be easily mass created through the crafting bar.
  • An easy way of mass-creating these is to fill up your inventory with dirt blocks, leave one slot with a Marshmallow on a Stick in your hotbar and one slot for Cooked Marshmallow, dropping a lot of Marshmallow on a Stick right next to a campfire and then standing on top of the Marshmallow on a Stick pile while holding the one in your inventory over a campfire. This way you'll pick one up immediately after the one you held out is done and start cooking the next one.
  • Normal Marshmallows cost 1 silver coin. Cooked marshmallows sell for 2 silver coins. If a player is patient enough, he or she could make easy money this way, and the only cost to them is time and wood.
