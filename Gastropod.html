Gastropod is an enemy that appears in The Hallow at night. It shoots pink lasers that are extremely fast and thus difficult to dodge. Shooting the Gastropod while it is preparing its laser will cause it to retreat back into its shell and not shoot the charging shot. Gastropods' range makes them difficult to run past and ignore without taking a fair amount of damage while doing so.
