PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Flare is an item, used as ammo for the Flare Gun. It emits a low-level red-tinted light that will stick to surfaces it comes into contact with and will continue to function underwater. It can be found in chests or be purchased from a merchant once the Flare Gun has been obtained. The Flare is almost identical to the Sticky Glowstick in function, and will stick to blocks and fall from broken blocks in the same manner. The only keydifference is that Flares, both red and blue, will burn for 10 minutes, twice the amount of time a Glowstickwill last. Flares cannot be recovered once they have burned out, but they work underwater; therefore they are very useful for exploring caves. If the flare gun has at least one flare, the flare gun can emit light, and can be used as a lantern underwater.
