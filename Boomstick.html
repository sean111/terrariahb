The Boomstick is a gun found in Jungle Shrines. The Boomstick fires a spread of three shots, but only consumes one bullet to do so. All three shots have a base power of 12, yielding 19 damage with Musket Balls and 21 damage per shot with either Meteor Shot or Silver Bullets. Highly effective at close range, hitting an enemy with all three shots can do around 60 damage, butchering most non-hardmode enemies. Due to the spread of the shots and the slow weapon speed, the Boomstick loses efficiency at long range.

Notes

  • Due to the lower health of non-hardmode enemies, the Boomstick tends to be more efficient than the Shotgun at long range.
  • When using the Boomstick with Meteor Shot it will do only the damage of 1 bullet even if you hit the enemy with more than 1 bullet; making the Boomstick less effective with Meteor Shot.
  • Despite it being located in jungle shrines, it seems to be significantly more rare than any of the other items you can find in the jungle shrines.

Trivia

This weapon's name is a reference to the film Army of Darkness.
