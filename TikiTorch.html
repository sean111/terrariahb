The Tiki Torch is a stationary light source that must be placed on a flat surface, and can not be held like most other light sources. It has an advantage over Torches in four ways:

  • It can be placed on wooden platforms.
  • It is by far the best selling item made out of wood or gel. Three tiki torches convert 1 gel and 10 wood to 3 silver coins.
  • It makes a good look for an island paradise if you wish to create one.
  • It can not be collected by accidentally clicking on it.

Like torches, running water douses tiki torches and knocks them loose.
