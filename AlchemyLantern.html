Alchemy Lantern
Statistics
Type Furniture
Placeable True
Dimensions 1 wide2 high
Max stack 999
Use time 14
Tool Pickaxe

Alchemy Lanterns are small a hanging Light Source much like the Chain Lantern, and cannot be crafted. They give off a faint greenish light when hung. They are placed like Chandeliers but take up much less space, which makes them ideal for small rooms or passages. Can be placed underwater. Can be toggled with wires.

Alchemy Lanterns can be found hanging in some dungeons.
