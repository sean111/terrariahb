Mister Stabby is an enemy in the Frost Legion. It chases the player and occasionally stops to do a brutal stabbing animation. Using a long spear or a ranged weapon is the best way to fight him.

History

  • 1.1.1: Introduced.

Trivia

  • The name of this mob could be a reference to the Mister Stabby Flash series.
