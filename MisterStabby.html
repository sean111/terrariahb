Mister Stabby is an enemy in the Frost Legion. It chases the player through most terrain that it was encountered, and occasionally stops to do a considerably long stabbing animation. Arguably, the best way to combat its speed and rapid bouncing is to use a spear or a spammable ranged weapon.

When attacking the player, he will dash and bounce around the terrain, making it difficult to hit him.

Trivia

  • The name of this mob could be a reference to the Mister Stabby Flash series.
