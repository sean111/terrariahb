The Adamantite Glaive is a spear crafted using Adamantite Bars and, on the console version, is one of the materials to craft Gungnir.The Adamantite Glaive is the fifth most powerful spear in the PC version of the game, beaten by Titanium Trident, Gungnir, Mushroom Spear, and Chlorophyte Partisan, and the third most powerful spear on the console version, the most powerful being Tonbogiri.

While having less damage and speed than its Adamantite Sword counterpart, it gains effectiveness because of the ability to attack through multiple enemies and at a very long range, while moving in any direction. With practice, spears become very effective melee weapons.
