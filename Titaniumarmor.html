This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

This Titanium Armor set is the alternative to the Adamantite Armor set. Like many Hardmode armors, it has three different headgear/helmet options, allowing the player to focus on magical, melee or ranged combat. Crafting the full set requires 54 Titanium Bars, made from 270 Titanium Ores.

Mechanics

The full armor set provides a buff named Shadow Dodge. Whenever you strike an enemy, be it with a melee, ranged, or magic weapon, this buff will activate and remain active for 20 seconds. While this buff is active the next time you would normally take damage, you instead become invulnerable for 2 seconds. The buff has an internal cooldown of twenty seconds, meaning you may not activate it less than twenty seconds after the buff ended.
While Shadow Dodge is active it creates two semi-transparent clones of the player on either side of the players current position. They will mimic any actions you make and are merely a visual indicator as to when you have this buff. You may right click the buff icon to dispel it at anytime.

Notes

  • The clones will copy all aspects of the player but the face, including armor, colors, weapons, and accessories.
  • The length of invincibility is affected by the Cross Necklace and any item built from it.
  • This effect will also block fall damage, but will not prevent debuffs such as the poison from dart traps.
  • Most dyes don't affect the color of this armor much with a few exceptions like Intense Rainbow Dye.
Bug : There is a glitch where if the shadow dodge buff is the 10th buff on the buff bar below the hotbar, the player will be invincible until all buffs wear off.
