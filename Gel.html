Gel is a common crafting material, dropped by most Slimes, as well as Slimed Zombies and Gastropods. Gel is a vital crafting material due to it being an ingredient for both Torches and Lesser Healing Potions, and is used regularly throughout the game. It is also used as ammunition for the Flamethrower, meaning that it can be stored in the ammo slots instead of the main inventory.
