Gel is a common crafting material. It is dropped often by most Slimes, with the exception of Dungeon Slimes and Lava Slimes. In Hardmode, it is also dropped by Gastropods.

Gel is an ingredient in Torches, a crucial and often-used item throughout the game. In Hardmode, Gel is also used as ammunition by the Flamethrower. Gel can thus be placed in the Inventory's Ammo slots (both before and after entering Hardmode).
