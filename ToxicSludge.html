The Toxic Sludge is a Hardmode slime enemy that appears in the Underground biome in place of normal slime enemies. They deal a vastly increased damage from normal slimes, and they also have a very high chance to afflict the player with Poison. They drop gel like the other slimes. They spawn under the same conditions as black slimes.

Trivia

  • The appearance of the Toxic Sludge appears to be a homage to similar enemies from the SNES game Yoshi's Island.
  • They look a bit like "Hairy Beast" (resembling the two teeth)
