The Jungle Spores are used to craft Jungle armor, the Blade of Grass, Thorn Chakram, and the Ivy Whip. They appear as little yellow dots and are dropped by the glowing orbs in the Underground Jungle. Jungle Spore Plants drop 1 to 3 Jungle Spores. They can be farmed in an artificial underground jungle by planting Jungle Grass Seeds (see that article for more information).
