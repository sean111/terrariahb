High Velocity Bullets are one of 6 types of craftable ammunition for bullet-firing weapons. They may be crafted at a Work Bench once the Arms Dealer begins to sell Empty Bullets after Hard mode has been enabled.

These Bullets may still be crafted Pre-Hard mode, provided that the player has obtained the necessary items for crafting them.

High Velocity Bullets cost 17 per round. This means a full stack of 999 bullets costs 1 69 83 .

Notes

  • This bullet will not increase the players Attack Speed.
  • Velocity is the speed at which a projectile travels through space once fired from a weapon. Higher Velocity will make hitting distant, fast moving targets easier for the player; as less movement tracking is required.
  • Cogs may be purchased from the Steampunker once the player has defeated The Twins, The Destroyer or Skeletron Prime and there exists a house for her to move into.

Trivia

Despite being named a "High Velocity" Bullet, its listed velocity is lower than all other custom-made bullets. However, when actually fired, it will move through space slightly faster than other custom made Bullets.
