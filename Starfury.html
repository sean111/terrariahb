The Starfury is a Magic Broadsword that summons a volley of 3 stars from the sky when swung, which fall in the direction of the mouse cursor and cause damage to any enemies hit. The mouse button can be held down for continuous swings and star spawns.

The sword itself deals a base damage of 16 as a melee weapon, while the stars deal an additional 15 damage each and can hit up to 2 times, for a total of 75 possible damage if all make hits. It consumes 16 Mana per swing, and can not be swung without sufficient Mana. It can only be obtained from Chests on Floating Islands.

Tips

  • The Starfury works well in conjunction with Mana-regenerating or Mana cost-reducing items like the Jungle armor.
  • When exploring, the Starfury can be used to reveal the locations of adjoining caves because of the light it provides when colliding with blocks.
  • The stars converge on the point where you clicked your mouse, so it is possible to get all 3 stars to hit a single enemy, or to hit a group of enemies by aiming above them, allowing the stars to diverge again.
  • It is possible to use Starfury to shoot through walls by standing next to the wall and aiming towards the bottom of the screen in the direction opposite the target. Note that this is somewhat inaccurate and may not work if there are obstructions between the edge of the screen and the target.
