The Iron Broadsword is an early game metal broadsword, being stronger than the Copper Broadsword. It has a slightly stronger alternate version in the Lead Broadsword. Like all broadswords, this sword has an arc motion, which is more useful for killing flying and jumping monsters than short swords.
