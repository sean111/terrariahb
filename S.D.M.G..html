The S.D.M.G (Or Space Dolphin Machine Gun) is a straight upgrade to the Megashark, with the same fire rate and ammo usage bonus, except with a noticeably higher damage output. It also gives a +5% critical hit chance.

Notes

  • The S.D.M.G is currently unobtainable without using an inventory editor or item spawner.[1]
  • If used with the Chlorophyte Helmet and the Shroomite Breastplate it will have a 90% chance to not consume ammo.
  • The S.D.M.G is very strong if used with Chlorophyte Bullets, as they are homing bullets and deal 15 damage.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference from the game "Edge Of Space" (hence the subtext 'It came from the edge of space'), in which Terraria is also referenced.
  • S.D.M.G. is an acronym for Space Dolphin Machine Gun.
  • This may be a reference to "Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy", as it is a dolphin from space.
  • This may also be a reference to the game "Dolphin Olympics", where you play as a dolphin trying to jump into space.
