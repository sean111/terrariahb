The S.D.M.G (Or Space Dolphin Machine Gun) is a straight upgrade to the Megashark, as well as a considerably close competitor with the Chain Gun, with a slightly faster firing rate and same ammo usage bonus, except with a noticeably higher damage output. It also gives a +5% critical hit chance. It is currently unobtainable from the game, and the only way to get it is via an inventory editor.

Notes

  • If used with the Chlorophyte Helmet and the Shroomite Breastplate it will have a 68% chance to not consume ammo.
  • The S.D.M.G is very strong if used with Chlorophyte Bullets, as they are homing bullets and deal 15 damage. On top of the S.D.M.G's 35 damage, they give a total of 50 damage per shot.
  • The S.D.M.G is also strong with Crystal Bullets due to their Crystal Shards.
  • Rumored to be added in the 1.3 patch.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference from the game Edge of Space (hence the subtext 'It came from the edge of space'), in which Terraria is also referenced.
  • S.D.M.G. is an acronym for Space Dolphin Machine Gun.
  • Space Dolphins are a race of creatures in the DC Comics universe, favored by the character Lobo.
  • It may also be a reference to the book series Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. In the book series, dolphins fled the Earth when they discovered that earth was scheduled to be destroyed.
