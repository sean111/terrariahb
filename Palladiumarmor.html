This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Palladium armor is an armor set which requires Palladium Bars and is the alternative to Cobalt armor. Like all Hardmode-material armors, the Palladium armor has three different headgear/helmet options, allowing players to focus on magical, melee or ranged combat.

Upon a successful attack, the armor will grant you the Rapid Healing buff, which heals 15 health over 5 seconds. Further attacks will refresh the duration.

Crafting a full set of Palladium Armor requires 135 Palladium Ore.

Notes

  • While not the largest or quickest heal, it can be very useful for the beginning of Hardmode, especially when used with ranged weapons to safely regenerate while avoiding further harassment.
