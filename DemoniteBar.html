Demonite Bars are a mid-game tier of Bars. Crafting most items involving Demonite requires that Demonite Ore be crafted into Bars first. Among the items they craft is the Nightmare Pickaxe (which also requires Shadow Scales), a Tool crucial to game advancement. Demonite Bars are crafted from 4 Demonite Ore at a Furnace.

To craft all Demonite equipment, you would need 110 bars which would require 440 Demonite Ore, as well as 56 Shadow Scales
