Demonite Bars are used to create unholy weapons, or together with Shadow Scales to make nightmare tools or Shadow armor. Demonite Bars are crafted from Demonite Ore, which is dropped by the Eye of Cthulhu and the Eater of Worlds; and also can be mined, but it is a rare find, it can be found very randomly throughout the world in an average group of 3, and at the bottom of corrupt pits, in groups of around 4. Mining it from the ground isn't the best way to get demonite. Fighting bosses can yield a large amount of it, especially the Eater of Worlds .

Demonite Bars have moderately high value, so selling excess bars for profit may be a good idea. Note that selling raw Demonite Ore will yield the same payoff.
