Oil Rag Sconces are a small hanging Light Source much like the Chain Lantern, and cannot be crafted. They are placed like Chandeliers but take up much less space, which makes them ideal for small rooms or passages. Can be toggled with wires. They are one of the randomly generated lanterns that can spawn in the Dungeon.

History

  • 1.2: Introduced

Notes

Sconse (as it is spelled in game) is a misspelling of sconce.
