The Goblin Tinkerer is an NPC, who can be saved by the player after a Goblin army has been defeated. He will randomly spawn in open areas in the Cavern layer while you are exploring. The Dungeon contains a large number of open areas and is the fastest way to force him to spawn. Talking to him in this state will free him, and he will wander the area until he gets killed by any nearby enemy or hazard, or until you move far enough from him. After that, he will move into an available house like any normal NPC. Note that if he gets killed, you WON'T have to unbind another one (same with the Mechanic and Wizard.)

Goblin Tinkerer has the option to reforge a weapon, tool, or accessory for a variable price in Gold.

Notes

  • If a Goblin Tinkerer dies in the caves after being freed, he will respawn like all other NPCs.
  • The name might be a reference to the Goblin Tinker, a neutral goblin hero in the game Warcraft III.
  • If the Bound Goblin dies before being unbound, he will respawn in the same place in short time, if the player leaves his spawnpoint out of sight.
  • As evidenced by some of his quotes, the Goblin Tinkerer has a crush on the Mechanic.
  • He, along with Santa Claus and the Wizard, are the only NPCs that don't have new quotes during a Blood Moon

Trivia

The "Yo, I heard you like rockets and running boots, so I put some rockets in your running boots." quote is a reference to Spectre Boots as "rockets" means Rocket boots and "running boots" means Hermes boots. This line is also a reference to the popular internet meme of Xzibit from 'Pimp my Ride'.

One of the Tinkerer's names, Dalek, along with other game elements, references the British television series, Doctor Who.

A quote he says, "I thought you'd be taller," is probably a reference to Star wars, When Luke rescues Leia and she asks "Aren't you a little short for a stormtrooper?"
