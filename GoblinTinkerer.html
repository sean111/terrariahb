The Goblin Tinkerer is an NPC, who must be saved by the player after a Goblin army has been defeated. His spawn location is completely random, but he has been observed to spawn mostly in the Cavern layer. He will randomly spawn near you while you are exploring. Talking to him in this state will free him, and he will wander the area until he gets killed by any nearby enemy or hazard, or until you move far enough from him. After that, he will move into an available house like any normal NPC.

Goblin Tinkerer has the option to reforge a weapon, tool, or accessory for a variable price in Gold.


Once the Goblin Tinkerer has moved into a house, he will sell the following:

Notes

  • If a Goblin Tinkerer dies in the caves, he will not need to be found again, instead he will respawn like all other NPC's
  • For those having trouble freeing bound people, autopause has to be off. Right-clicking them will free them.
  • The name might be a reference to the Goblin Tinker, a neutral goblin hero in the game Warcraft 3.
  • If the Bound Goblin dies before being unbound, he will respawn in the same place in short time, if the player leaves his spawnpoint out of sight.
  • As evidenced by some of his quotes, the Goblin Tinkerer has a crush on the Mechanic.
