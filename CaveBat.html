Cave bats are the variety of bat found only Underground. They cannot fly underwater.

History

  • 1.0.6: Health reduced from 25 to 16; damage reduced from 15 to 13.
  • 1.0.5:
    • May no longer enter water and doesn't make a splash sound when hitting water.
    • Now have a specific death sound effect.
    • Health reduced from 45 to 25; damage reduced from 25 to 15.
  • 1.0.4: Introduced.
