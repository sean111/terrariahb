Wooden Arrows are the weakest Arrows in the game. They are used as ammunition for all bows, and are also the base of the crafting recipes for many, more powerful types of arrows. A fired Wooden Arrow has a 50% chance of being recoverable after hitting an enemy or a wall.

Wooden Arrows shot from the Molten Fury appear to be Flaming Arrows, but they do not do additional damage. If recovered, these arrows have a 1 in 3 chance of becoming actual Flaming Arrows. Otherwise they are reverted to Wooden Arrows.
