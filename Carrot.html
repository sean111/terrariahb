Carrot

Carrot
Statistics
Type Potion
Max stack 1
Rarity Orange
Use time 19
Tooltip Summons a pet bunny

The Carrot summons a pet Bunny when used. It grants the Pet Bunny buff that lasts until the player manually cancels it by right-clicking the buff's icon, or dies. The color of the bunny's fur is defined by the shirt color chosen for the character at the point of their creation.

It is only available to players who have purchased the Collector's Edition of Terraria new characters created by them will spawn with it. While it can be traded, players who do not have the Collector's Edition will not be able to use it.

Bug: When the Carrot is placed in a chest and the player exits the world and comes back, the player will have the Carrot again AND one in the chest.
