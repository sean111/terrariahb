Console only content
The information only applies to the console version of the game.

The Cabbage summons a pet Guinea Pig when used. It grants the Pet Guinea Pig buff that lasts until the player manually cancels it by right-clicking the buff's icon, or dies.

It can be obtained from Golden Chests.
