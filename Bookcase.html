The Bookcase is a crafting station used for the creation of three Hardmode Magic Weapons: Crystal Storm, Cursed Flames and Golden Shower. Until Hardmode it can be used as decorative Furniture, and fulfills a House's Flat Surface Item requirement. It can be crafted using twenty Wood and ten Books gathered from the Dungeon.

There are five additional versions found by way of looting the Dungeon and Underworld.

Trivia

  • The Bookcase actually shows 13 books stored, similiar to how a placed book shows 2-3 books.
  • Although it is not required to make it, Water Bolt's unique sprite is visible in the bookcase (a blue book with white/brown band).
