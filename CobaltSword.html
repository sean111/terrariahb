The Cobalt Sword is a sword crafted using Cobalt Bars and is a crafting material for the Excalibur in the non-PC versions of the game. It bears a strong resemblance to the Muramasa and can be swung continuously, also much like Muramasa and Phasesaber. It can also swing faster than the Mythril Sword and the Adamantite Sword.
