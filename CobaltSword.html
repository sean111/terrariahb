The Cobalt Sword is a sword crafted using Cobalt Bars and is one of the materials to craft Excalibur. It bears a strong resemblance to the Muramasa and can be swung continuously, also much like Muramasa and Phasesaber. It can also swing faster than the Mythril Sword and the Adamantite Sword.
