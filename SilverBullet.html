The Silver Bullet is a bullet item used by any gun that can use Musket Balls. They can be found in Chests and are also available from the Arms Dealer once Wall Of Flesh has been defeated, or during any Blood Moon. In hardmode, Silver Bullets replace Wooden Arrows that drop from pots. They deal 2 more damage than Musket Balls, though they can't be used to craft Meteor Shot. Their damage is equal to that of Meteor Shot, but with greater knockback and without the pierce effect. Despite their folk lore, Silver Bullets do not deal extra damage to Werewolves.
