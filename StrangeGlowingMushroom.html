PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Strange Glowing Mushroom is purchased from the Truffle NPC, and, when used, spawns a miniature version of the Truffle. The pet emits a small amount of light, similarly to Glowing Mushrooms. If the player moves too far away from the pet or begins to fly, the pet will disappear, reappearing at your position (or when you land).
