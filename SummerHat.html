The Summer Hat is a vanity item equipped on the Social Helmet Slot. It is sold by the Clothier NPC (arrives after Skeletron is defeated) for one gold coin, but only during the daytime.

Trivia

  • The Summer Hat may be a reference to the signature straw hat of Monkey D. Luffy, the protagonist of One Piece.
