Bones are thrown consumable weapons can also be used as a crafting material. They are used to craft the Necro armor, bone-themed furniture, Piano, and Mechanical Skull. Bones are dropped by Angry Bones, Dark Casters, and Cursed Skulls in the Dungeon.

When used as weapons, Bones have a 50% chance of being retrievable after use, even if they hit enemies. They are greatly affected by gravity, and arc similarly to Throwing Knives and Shurikens. Of the three consumable throwing weapons, Bones have the least range (Throwing Knives having the greatest).

Bones can be placed with a Bone Wand, turning them into Bone Blocks.

Crafting a full set of Necro Armor requires a total of 150 bones.
