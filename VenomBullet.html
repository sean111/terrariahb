PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

This item currently does not apply the debuff in player vs. player scenarios. Currently the second strongest, by base damage, bullet in the game (the first being Chlorophyte Bullets), it inflicts Venom upon the target, and unlike the Chlorophyte Bullets both materials are easily obtained from NPCs, making it somewhat better choice for players who do not wish to build a Chlorophyte Ore farm.

The cost per round is 23 . This means a stack of 999 costs 2 29 77 .
