The Gungnir is the strongest, largest, and fastest spear in the game. It has an unusually large size for a melee weapon, and can hit enemies from distances comparable to that of a Wooden Boomerang. It can deliver 3 to 4 hits per use. Like all Hallowed Melee weapons, when wielded it emits a good deal of its own light and a sparkle effect. It is part of the Hallowed set of Armor/Weapons.

Like all Spears, the Gungnir's best possible Modifier is Godly. It cannot obtain the Legendary Modifier.

Notes

  • The Gungnir is the most expensive item in the game, and therefore is also the most expensive item to Reforge. Without any existing Modifiers, a Reforge costs 1 50 . Reforging a Godly Gungnir to have a different Modifier costs 3 17 44 46 , the highest reforge cost of any item in the game, followed closely by a Mythical Magic Dagger, and Mythical Ice Rod (which each cost 3 9 84 71 ).

Tips

  • It's a fairly effective weapon against The Destroyer, but requires beating Skeletron Prime to be crafted (Soul of Fright).

Trivia

  • In Norse mythology, Gungnir is the spear of the god Odin.
