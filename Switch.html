The Switch is an activation mechanism sold by the Mechanic. It can also be found in the Dungeon connected to Chain Lanterns, and can be harvested with the Hammer.

The Switch can be placed anywhere a torch can be placed: on top or on the sides of blocks, or on the sides of Wooden Beams. It is activated by right-clicking on it.

Despite its name, the Switch does not need to act as an on/off mechanism. When wired to Dart Traps, for example, right-clicking the Switch will always cause the Trap to shoot, the same as a Pressure Plate would, no matter which position the switch happens to be in.

Notes

  • The Switch is identical in operation to the Lever, except that the Lever can only be placed on floors, while Switches can be placed on floors or walls. The Lever is also easier to see and provides a larger clicking target, whereas Switches can be more difficult to spot and click on due to being only a single tile in size (which may actually be a benefit in PvP, should a player wish to hide a trap mechanism).
