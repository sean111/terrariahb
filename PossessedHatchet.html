This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Possessed Hatchet is a throwable melee weapon that returns to its owner after a certain distance, similiar to the Boomerang. When thrown, the hatchet moves extremely fast and homes in on nearby enemies. It has a 12.5% chance to drop from the Golem in the Lihzarhd Temple. As with all modifiable boomerangs, the best prefix for the Possessed Hatchet is Godly.

Notes

  • The Possessed Hatchet is capable of dealing over 150 points of damage, and over 300 upon scoring critical hits.
    • Very short usage delay makes it one of the most powerful melee weapons in the game.
  • Cannot Autoswing
  • Bears the ability to chase after targets automatically.
  • It's a melee-damage weapon, and as such it, and the monsters it hits, are affected by Flasks.
  • When compared to the Paladin's Hammer, it is identical in damage, but the knockback and speed are inferior. However, many players will prefer the hatchet because of its homing capabilities.
  • The Paladin's Hammer can pierce multitude of enemies in a single throw, allowing one to devastate multiple enemies at once. The hatchet however cannot.


History

  • 1.2: Introduced.
Bug : May drop from Skeletron.
Bug : If the Possessed Hatchet is thrown, and there are two or more monsters in following range, it will stay suspended in the air, and it will be unusable until it returns. You can get it back by waiting for it to hit a monster, or by walking into it. This is because the hatchet does not know which one to hit, so it stays spinning in the air until only one monster remains in reach.
Bug : Throwing at a one block thick incline that has been hammered into slopes can cause the hatchet to pass clean through it. The hatchet will chase after enemies after going through.
Bug : When the Possessed Hatchet is thrown, if a lot of constant damage is dealt to the target, such as a Slime summoned by the Slime Staff, the Possessed Hatchet will spin around the target until the constant damage has ended. .
