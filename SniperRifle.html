The Sniper Rifle is a ranged weapon dropped by Skeleton Snipers in the Dungeon if the world has entered Hardmode and Plantera has been defeated.. It fires a fast, powerful bullet which can kill most enemies in one shot. It also has the added capability of "zooming out", a function identical to the Rifle Scope and the Binoculars, where the screen scrolls approximately half the distance the mouse moves. It increases your critical hit chance by 15%.

Musket Balls and Silver Bullets shot from this weapon gain the effects (but not damage) of High Velocity Bullets.

Notes

  • The Sniper Rifle bears the ability to 'zoom' in/out, similar to the Rifle Scope accessory, However if the Rifle Scope is used along with the Sniper Rifle the vision increase will be very minor(6 blocks increase). The scope can be activated by holding down the right mouse button and moving the mouse around.
  • The Sniper Rifle, with the right multipliers, can deal extremely high damage at long range.
    • The highest recorded critical hit with this weapon is 1106.
  • Although The Sniper Rifle pairs nicely with explosive ammo (killing multiple targets, compensating for the low fire rate) one must be very careful, as self damage from the sniper explosive rounds are easily enough to 2 shot most players.
  • It can use any of the bullet types listed for a normal gun like the Handgun.
  • You can use Chlorophyte bullets with this gun. This way, you can kill your enemies without even directly aiming at them.
  • When zooming with Rifle Scope accessory, your view will be increased by 30 tiles.
  • 426 base damage is possible (Ruthless) with correct gear and modifiers on accessories. This include the bonus given from the Shroomite armor when standing still. This is said to max out to approx 1098dmg crit.
  • When zooming with Sniper Rifle the players minimap will be updated. Same with Rifle Scope and Binoculars
  • The aim of the Sniper Rifle becomes detached from the cursor between 220 and 290 becoming as bad as several cursor-widths difference at around 260. This can make aiming difficult to learn when the target is below you.
  • This weapon pairs very, very well with Golden Bullets because they cause an effect that happens only on-death. Thus, with the Sniper's high damage and, as a result, lower number of bullets required to kill an enemy, it is very cost efficient to use it in addition to golden bullets.
  • It is possible to shoot through walls one block thick if the bullet is traveling at the correct angle.

Trivia

  • This weapon can be thought of as the long-awaited upgrade to the Musket, due to their similar sprites, and both having relatively high base damage and low speed stats relative to their tier.
