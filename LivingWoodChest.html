This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Living Wood Chest can be found inside hollow Living Trees.

Loot

Besides one of the items listed here, they also contain the common items of surface chests. They have a mossy appearance and usually contain 3-6 stacks of items.
