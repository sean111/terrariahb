The Silver Chandelier is a piece of Furniture that can be placed on ceilings. It is the equivalent of a tungsten chandelier for worlds generated with Silver Ore. It functions the same as other chandeliers. It can sometimes be found unlit in underground cabins. It is a valid light source for NPCs.
