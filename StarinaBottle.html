This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Star in a Bottle is a low light producing item that can be placed on ceilings, like Chinese Lanterns or any of the Chandeliers. This item is also available light source for purposes of NPC housing despite its low light level. It is fairly easy to make as sand and stars can be found commonly.
