Cobalt Bars are crafted from 3 Cobalt Ore. It is the first tier of the three hardmode ores that the player is able to obtain.

Cobalt equipment, as the name would suggest, is colored deep blue. Certain items in the set have a distinctly Japanese theme, the spear being a naginata and the headgear variations resembling Japanese headdresses. To craft all Cobalt equipment (and five Light Discs) the player needs to acquire 170 bars, or a total of 510 ore.
