Cobalt Bars are crafted from 3 Cobalt Ore. Cobalt is the first tier of the three Hardmode ores that the player is able to obtain.

Crafting all Cobalt equipment requires 145 bars, or 435 ore.

Notes

  • The Cobalt Shield is not related to Cobalt ore, and is not craftable from Cobalt bars; it can only be found naturally in the Dungeon's Gold Chests.
  • Cobalt is very valuable, so extras can be collected and sold to make Money. Cobalt has the same value whether it is sold as Bars or raw Ore.
  • Cobalt equipment, as the name would suggest, is colored deep blue.
  • The full armor set with the helmet gives only 1 more defense than Molten Armor, which is 25. Cobalt is 26, however, but the set bonus is different.

Trivia

  • Several Cobalt items have a distinctive Japanese theme, the spear being a naginata, the sword somewhat resembling a Katana, or the katana-like Muramasa, and the headgear variations resembling Japanese headdresses.
