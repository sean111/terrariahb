The Eater of Worlds is one of thirteen boss enemies. It is a giant Corruption-themed worm (resembling a Devourer, but much larger) with eyes. It is summoned using the Worm Food from within The Corruption, or by destroying 3 Shadow Orbs.

The Eater of Worlds is not a single target, but a string of 50 body segments with individual life counters. When any inner segment is destroyed, it splits into multiple smaller worms, each with their own head and tail. Each section of the Worm must be killed in order to defeat the entire boss.

Fighting the Eater of Worlds is the only way to obtain Shadow Scales, which are required to craft the Nightmare Pickaxe, The Breaker, and Shadow Armor. Defeating the Eater of Worlds also produces usable quantities of Demonite Ore. Individual segments drop some of these when killed, with a larger bonus drop upon defeating the entire entity.

If your world generated Crimson instead of Corruption, this boss will be replaced by the Brain of Cthulhu.

Notes

  • Individual segments cannot survive on their own. Killing one segment, when that part only consisted of two segments, will kill both.
  • The different sections of the Eater of Worlds are not equivalent: A Head section will deal significantly more damage than the Body or Tail, but has far less life.
  • Trying to summon the Eater of Worlds with Worm Food while another is still alive will instantly kill the player. Summoning additional Eater of Worlds by breaking additional Shadow Orbs does work and will not kill the player.
  • If the player leaves The Corruption, the Eater of Worlds will disappear.
  • Like all Worms, the Eater of Worlds is immune to Lava.
  • The Eater of Worlds has a total of fifty body segments, 1 head, 48 body segments and 1 tail. This brings its total HP up to 7,485.

Tips

Player being "Loop Attacked" by the EoW
  • Farming the Eater of Worlds is one of the most effective ways of making money prior to Hardmode. Mid-game armor and weapons will make the fight easy, and selling its drops will yield 9 to 11 each time, on average. After hardmode, is also the easiest way of farming soul of night. Every eater killed will give you 4-5 souls, and lots of hearts it drops. It makes you nearly invulnerable to all the other enemies that may spawn. The eater can also be summoned while killing other bosses to heal yourself fast.
  • Weapons that pierce for multiple hits are effective, due to the Eater's large size (eg. Flails, Spears, Grenades, Spiky Balls, Vilethorn). The Harpoon is also effective, and is best used when being charged by its head.
  • When you kill a section of the Eater of Worlds, they will drop hearts if the player is injured, as if it was another enemy. The near-constant hearts will heal the player.
Bug 1.1: Eater of Worlds might disappear even if you fight it in the middle of the corruption. This might happen if it goes too far away from the player.
