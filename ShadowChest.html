The Shadow Chest is a storage item found only in the Underworld, generally on top of or within an Obsidian tower, and contains rare items. All Shadow Chests are initially locked, requiring a Shadow Key to open. A single Shadow Key will open unlimited Shadow Chests (unlike Golden Keys, which are consumed upon use). Each Shadow Chest contains one high-tier item of "Orange" rarity, along with other standard Underground loot.

Like all other naturally-occurring Chests, Shadow Chests can be freed with a Hammer after being emptied, and picked up by the player for placement and use elsewhere.

Notes

  • The Dark Lance, Flower of Fire, Flamelash, and Sunfury can only be found in Shadow Chests.
  • The Flamelash spawns very rarely, and some worlds are generated with no Flamelash at all.
  • Very rarely a world may spawn without a single Shadow Chest.
Bug: If a Shadow Chest has an item in it and an Active Stone Block is underneath it, a power source can turn the Active Stone Block off to drop 5 Shadow Chests and vice versa with the original Shadow Chest still intact. It may also be hit with a hammer for the same effect.
Bug: Very rarely, a Shadow Chest has a part of its sprite looking like a normal chest and, you can open it up without a Shadow Key. Once you break it, it will drop a normal Chest.
Bug: Very rarely, in the Xbox 360 version, when approaching the Shadow Chest your game will crash.

History

  • 1.0.6: Introduced. Contains some items previously dropped by Underworld enemies.
