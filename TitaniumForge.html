This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

A Titanium Forge is a crafting station that incorporates the same functions as a regular Furnace and Hellforge with the additional bonus of being able to smelt Titanium, Chlorophyte and Adamantite ores into their respective bars. Although it appears to have glass over the front of the fire, it does not require Glass to craft. It still gives off fire particles like the Furnace and Hellforge.

The Titanium Forge's ability to smelt ores is identical to that of the Adamantite Forge, the latter being intended for worlds that generate Adamantite Ore instead of Titanium Ore.
