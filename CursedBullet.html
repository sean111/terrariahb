Effects of Cursed Bullets

Cursed Bullets are a Hardmode ammunition item. Enemies struck by Cursed Bullets are always inflicted with the Cursed Inferno debuff, which causes damage over time and cannot be extinguished by Water (unlike the On Fire! debuff).

Cursed Bullets have a high base damage for bullets, though they lack any multi-enemy damage effects (as Meteor Shot and Crystal Bullets have).
