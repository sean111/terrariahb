A Sign is a Decorative piece of Furniture that can be placed on the ground or wall and then edited to leave a message that anyone else can see. The message can be as long as 10 lines, and at 48 characters per line there are 480 characters available in total. These can be used for directions, adventure texts, store signs, or anything else you can imagine.

Once crafted, they can be placed in three ways: standing, hinged, and hanging.

  • Standing signs can be placed on top of any block or platform.
  • Hanging signs must be placed under a block.
  • Hinged signs are placed on the left or right sides of blocks.

Notes

Bug : When editing a sign, entering a digit will also cause the Hotbar item associated with that number key to be selected.
Anomaly: If you have the Compass equipped, and there is no text on the sign, while editing, you can see your coordinates.
Anomaly: When editing a sign, pressing the Inventory key will exit the sign without saving any changes made. This also applies to Tombstones. Single-player only.
Bug : For the console version, when you use the "<" and ">" keys, the game crashes.
Bug : For the console version, signs cannot be edited in multiplayer. This also applies to Tombstones. Multiplayer only.

History

  • 1.2: Stack limit increased from 250 to 999.
  • 1.0.5: Destroying a placed sign while you are reading it will no longer cause your character to become frozen.
  • Pre-Release: Introduced.
