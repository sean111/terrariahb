The Wizard Hat is an item equippable in the Helmet slot that increases all magic damage by 15% and adds 2 points of defense. The Wizard Hat is dropped by Tim. It is not to be confused with the Wizard's Hat.

Wearing the Wizard Hat alongside any gem-augmented Robe provides a set bonus of +10% magic critical strike chance. Any leg-slot equipment can be worn with the set bonus intact.
