The Rocket Launcher is an explosive ranged weapon. It is dropped by Skeleton Commando, which only spawns in the Dungeon after Plantera is slain. The Rocket Launcher uses Rockets for ammo that can be purchased from the Cyborg after defeating Plantera.

Notes

  • The Rocket Launcher cannot obtain modifiers that alter velocity or speed, making Godly the best available.
  • All rockets have splash damage that will harm the player if fired too close. It will not damage other players unless PvP is enabled.
Anomaly: The splash damage of the rocket launcher that hurts the user cannot hurt the Skeleton Commandos that originally use it when they fire.
  • The Ruthless Rocket Launcher has the same stats as the Grenade Launcher.
