The Piranha Gun is a ranged weapon added in Update 1.2. It is found inside Jungle Chests, which can only be found in Dungeons. This weapon fires a piranha-shaped projectile that latches onto an enemy to do continuous damage while the attack button is being held. The fire pattern is very similar to the Chain Knife, but with a much longer range. The projectile will proceed to attack another enemy (if any is near) when it has killed its target, otherwise it has to travel back to the gun before it can be fired again.

Notes

  • The projectile seems to return to the player once it is a certain distance away from the shooter. Thus, firing the weapon in the direction the shooter is moving will greatly increase the range of the projectile, while firing the weapon backwards while running will decrease the range.
  • Like most other ranged weapons, the best prefix this can obtain is Unreal.
  • This weapon is useful in dispatching teleporting enemies, since the piranha will lock on to and follow the target each teleport.
  • The piranha can damage enemies other than the one it is currently attacking, should they touch it.
  • The first enemy the piranha attaches to must be in direct line of sight with the shot. The piranha will automatically hone in on nearby enemies if they are in range once the initial enemy is dead, regardless of line of sight.
  • The piranha will not latch onto other players in PVP.
  • The Piranha Gun does not receive any particular bonus from any of the three helmets for Shroomite armor.
  • The Piranha Gun is massively useful in stunlocking enemies; certain enemies and bosses or boss segments such as the Golem's fist can not attack or fire whilst being damaged, thus, the Piranha Gun nullifies their attacks. Especially good for eliminating Paladins as a player can safely fire at them then hide behind a wall whilst the piranha does its work.
  • The Piranha Gun is great for eliminating swarms of enemies as you can hide behind walls whilst it jumps around between enemies in a wide open space, thus eliminating everything in a room from the safety of your point.
  • Some users may find the Piranha Gun is useful for support. It can be easily used to constantly damage bosses without focusing on aiming projectiles or going in close for melee attacks, allowing the user to focus on dodging the attacks of the boss and to hold out for any debuffs to clear such as Potion Sickness.
  • The Piranha Gun is very effective against Dungeon Guardian as well as the Wall of Flesh.

Bugs

Bug : Occasionally, projectiles will fly through walls if it hits an enemy next to it. This seems to happen when the enemy dies in the very first hit and there are no additional targets found, such as against lone pre-hardmode enemies.
Bug : The Piranha Gun will follow Blazing Wheels and Spike Balls in the Dungeon forever in a circle until you release the mouse button.
Bug : The Piranha may occasionally disappear or never return to the gun, thus not allowing you to switch weapons. Exiting the game re-entering, dying, or using a Teleporter will reset the Gun, or if you manage to kill the enemy the piranha was last on via Thorns Potion or Turtle Armor.

History

Trivia

  • This gun is likely a reference to a weapon used by the character Vector from Despicable Me. It's a bit easier to get the ammo back in, though.
