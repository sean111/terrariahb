The Handgun is a gun that fires bullets. Its base damage is 14, meaning it does 21 damage with Musket Balls and 23 damage with Silver Bullets or Meteor Shots. The Handgun is obtained from Chests found in the Dungeon. It can be upgraded to the vastly superior Phoenix Blaster with 20 Hellstone Bars. It is recommended that this be done as soon as possible, as the Phoenix Blaster is one of the best pre-Hardmode ranged weapons in the game.
