Sold by the Cyborg NPC during the Blood Moon for 2 and 50 Has a small blast radius and it will destroy tiles.

Tips

Since it has a very small blast area (about 6 tiles in diameter) it's useful for precision tunneling where a Bomb would be more messy or hard to aim.
