A Demon Torch is a type of Torch found naturally in The Underworld's ruined houses. It can be retrieved and used like a normal torch. It is not purchasable or craftable.

Demon Torches appear purple-colored, but produce light that cycles smoothly between blue, purple, and red repeatedly (noticeable only when a colored Lighting Mode is chosen). As it cycles colors, its light level also varies from that of a regular Torch to significantly dimmer levels.

Trivia

  • The Demon Torch and the Disco Ball are the only Light Sources whose color changes.
