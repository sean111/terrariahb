Magic Power Potion is a buff potion which buffs the player with Magic Power. The buff increases damage done by magic weapons by 20%. This lasts for 2 minutes, but can be cancelled at any time by right-clicking the icon. The buff stacks with any bonuses granted from equipped armor.
