The Harpoon can be dropped by any goblin during a Goblin Army and acts in a similar fashion to a flail, returning to the player after hitting a wall or enemy. However, unlike other flails, the Harpoon deals ranged damage.

It fires a heavy projectile that falls quickly which is useful for trying to eliminate enemies on the other side of hills or similar areas. It also has a high knockback, making it effective as improvised home defense. The arc makes the Harpoon effectively similar to that of arrows fired from a bow, but with a slower re-fire rate and effectively infinite ammunition. Another upside to the harpoon is that it will continuously fire if the attack button is held down, unlike other flails.

In addition, its fire rate seems to be dependent on how long the harpoon itself is out of the weapon rather than being based on a set time, meaning while it will take a long time to reload after being fired a long distance, if it is fired close to the player it will refire quickly upon returning to the gun. This provides an effective way of juggling enemies against walls or along flat surfaces, as its low cooldown allows it to rapidly and repeatedly strike enemies until they die.

The projectile also deals damage to multiple enemies when retracting. Firing a harpoon over a cluster of zombies will hit all of them caught in the retraction path and, oddly enough, knock them away from you, making it a good weapon for crowd control.

Notes

  • The Harpoon is destructive on all pre-hardmode bosses. The Eater of Worlds can be killed without taking damage if you are cautious, Skeletron takes a reasonable amount of damage per hit, and the Eye of Cthulhu can be farmed effectively using these techniques. You don't even need to make an arena or use gold armor!
  • The wall of flesh can be defeated with this weapon as well very efficiently. Destroying all the hungry with other weapons such as swords or flails is recommended beforehand though. If close to any one of main parts to the wall of flesh(eyes and mouth), you can hold down the button aiming at them with the harpoon moving backward on a flat walkway doing large amounts of damage per second, killing the wall of flesh with little problems. This weapon even one-shots the leeches the mouth produces while doing so.
  • It is good to obtain this weapon early as it is efficient on almost every pre-hardmode boss. This even fairs well in early hardmode, but not against hardmode bosses.
  • Is extremely effective on pre-hardmode common enemies(usually 1-2 hits on these enemies[e.g. zombies, soul eaters, etc.])
Bug: When facing to the right, and firing, the projectile goes behind your character for a moment, which can damage close enemies behind you.
