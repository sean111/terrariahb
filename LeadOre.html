This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Lead Ore is found from the surface level and deeper, and appears as a dark blue ore. It naturally spawns as an alternative to the Iron Ore in some worlds. It creates Lead Bars, which can be used to make lead anvil, tools, and armor, as well as Chains, Buckets, and other iron items.
