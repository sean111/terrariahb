The Stake Launcher is a Repeater-like weapon dropped by Mourning Wood that, as the name suggests, shoots stakes. Stakes can later be purchased from the Witch Doctor and Arms Dealer. It is easily one of the most, if not THE most, powerful ranged weapons obtainable, given its extremely high damage (base of 100 with the stakes), powerful knockback, high velocity, automatic fire, cheap ammunition, and the fact that said ammunition can pierce up to ten times. The only downside would be the fire rate, which is relatively slow in comparison to weapons like the Megashark.

Notes

  • The weapon can use the zoom from the Sniper Scope.
  • The weapon type is classified as Arrow by Shroomite Armor set bonuses, as well as by the Magic Quiver.
  • This is likely to be a reference to vampire hunters, as they normally use stakes to kill vampires.
    • Adding to the vampire hunter reference is that it does obscene amount of damage to Vampires, which often, if not always, kills them in one shot (unless you reduce the Stake Launcher's damage on purpose). While equipped with ranged builds, you can easily deal well above 5000 critical strike damage to Vampires.
  • This weapon is very effective against The Destroyer because the stakes can hit multiple segments and do incredible amounts of damage.
  • Despite the name and the fact that bonuses are given to the shroomite headgear, the stake launcher can zoom out when having the sniper scope equiped.
Anomaly: The Stake Launcher's reforging costs do not appear to be correct; to reforge from base costs 37 when it should cost 37 50 . With the Unreal Modifier (the best one), its reforge cost is way overpriced at 1 62 28 56 when it should only cost around 1 16 19 25 based on common value gains of items capable of receiving Legendary, Unreal or Mythical modifiers. The Unreal Stake Launcher's current reforging price is higher than even the Godly Gungnir (A weapon that, unmodified, is twice as expensive) at 1 58 72 23 .
