The Venus Magnum is a gun that is occasionally dropped by the Hardmode boss Plantera.

Arguably, it can be better at killing bosses in comparison to the Megashark, but is much more bullet-consuming due to not having an innate ability to save ammo. When ammunition efficiency isn't a concern, it may be better to use this over the Megashark due to its significantly higher base damage. The only downside is that it is not fully-automatic, which makes it more difficult to use to its full potential, compared to other hardmode weapons.

Musket Balls and Silver Bullets shot from this weapon gain the effects (but not damage) of High Velocity Bullets.

Notes

  • The best modifier for the Venus Magnum is Unreal.
  • The Megashark's base DPS (bullet damage and defense not included) is 230, as opposed to the Venus Magnum's 270.
  • Bullet damage helps level the playing field in the Megashark's favor, as it has a greater RoF. With a 10-damage bullet, this is upped to Megashark 330 DPS and Magnum 345 DPS.
  • This weapon is notably more effective than the Megashark against more heavily-armored (40+ defense) enemies, since it deals more damage at a slightly lower rate of fire. (Comparing against-40-defense, pre-equipment bonus and pre-bullet 30 DPS for the Megashark, and 112.5 DPS for the Venus Magnum. With a 10-damage bullet, that is 130 DPS to 187.5 DPS.)
  • These calculations assume you are actually capable of clicking 7.5 times per second, however. Otherwise, your DPS is a factor of your clicking speed.

Trivia

  • The Venus Magnum's design might be based on a Nerf gun, perhaps the N-strike Strongarm or the Vortex Praxis.
