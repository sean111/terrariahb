Hallowed armor is an armor set added in the 1.1 update. Like the other 1.1 armor sets, the helmet has three variations, one for each fighting class: melee, ranged and caster. It takes the full sets of Cobalt, Mythril and Adamantite armor along with 20 Souls Of Might, 20 Souls of Fright and 20 Souls of Sight to make this armor.

This armor combines the visual effects given by the armor sets used to craft it: an after-image (Cobalt armor), shine (Mythril armor), and pulsing aura (Adamantite armor).

Notes

  • As of version 1.1.1 it is the most powerful armor in the game and the hardest armor to get, since you need to defeat each of the Hardmode bosses first plus obtain the full Cobalt, Mythril and Adamantite armor sets in order to craft it.
Bug: You can create any part of the Hallowed Armor just using two types of armor. (e.g) You must have the three helmets (Cobalt, Mythril, and Adamantine) in your inventory so you can make the Hallowed Mask, but, before making the recipe, you can right click the Adamantine (Cobalt or Mythril works too) Helmet, so you will wear it, but you still can make the Hallowed Mask, if you craft it, you will just use the Cobalt and Mythril helmets, and the Adamantine helmet will not be lost. It works with any Hallowed Part. (Tested several times with Auto-Pause ON)
