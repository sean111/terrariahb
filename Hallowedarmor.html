Hallowed armor is an armor set crafted from the other hardmode armors. Like the other sets, the helmet has three variations, one for each fighting class: melee, ranged and caster. It takes the full sets of Cobalt, Mythril and Adamantite armor along with 20 Souls Of Might, 20 Souls of Fright and 20 Souls of Sight to make this armor. As a result, each of the hardmode bosses must be defeated at least once in order to obtain the required souls.

This armor combines the visual effects given by the armor sets used to craft it: an after-image (Cobalt armor), shine (Mythril armor), and pulsing aura (Adamantite armor).

Notes

Anomaly: The Hallowed mask is created with the Cobalt, Mythril, and Adamantite (Helmets), even though it intuitively should be the (Masks). This is because the melee version of the Hallowed set is a mask and not a helmet as in previous sets.
Bug: It is possible to create the Hallowed Armor and spend one less item (though you must have all items in your inventory). By shift-clicking an item into the trashcan, the craft menu will still show the hallowed armor and crafting it won't expend the item in the trash bin.
Bug: Shine effect does not work properly and only triggers when the character grapples to a ceiling from within a lit area to a darkened area. Slight particle effect is noticed when jumping left or right, not up, and does not trigger cascade.
