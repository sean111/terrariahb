Explosives (item) are a placeable bundle of Dynamite that can be remotely activated to explode with wire. They destroy the same blocks Dynamite does, that is, all but Dungeon Bricks, Hellstone (only until the Wall of Flesh is defeated), Cobalt, Mythril and Adamantite. They can be found naturally underground in rare and dangerous traps.

The Explosives' blast radius is very wide at ten blocks, destroying up to a total of 200 blocks, and their damage is high enough to despawn killed players' dropped items and prevent retrieving them. Explosives do around 250 damage to enemies.

Explosives cannot be placed on top of each other, however other blocks can be placed on them, and more Explosives can be placed on those.

Notes

Anomaly: Despite being made of three blue rarity items and one white rarity item, Explosives is only of white rarity.
Anomaly: Upon dying to a natural Explosives trap underground, the game will state that it was the player's explosives, though no one actually placed them.
Bug: On console version, supposedly explosives (If far enough away) can cause you to go back to the Xbox menu. This has yet to be proven, however.
Bug: On console version, Explosives are somehow able to be spawned in dungeon walls and can be triggered. Because of the bug with the dungeon bricks being unbreakable, this bug holds greater threat in the dungeon and can only blow away the walls if near the rim. This can be very bad if the trigger is outside of the rim.
