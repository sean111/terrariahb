Tuxedo is a vanity set made up of the Top Hat, Tuxedo Shirt and Tuxedo Pants.

These items are all equipped in the Social slots as they only provide a visual appearance.

While the other two pieces are crafted, the Top Hat is dropped by The Groom. Something strange about this vanity set, is that the Tuxedo Shirt behaves oddly when jumping or swinging a weapon. It doesn't cling to your character, but hangs in a box around him/her.
