Stynger Bolts are the ammunition used with the ranged weapon Stynger. It is one of the possible drops from the Golem boss and will always be accompanied with a Stynger drop as well. Additional ammo can be purchased from the Witch Doctor and Arms Dealer as long as the Stynger is in your inventory during the purchase.

The bolts explode on impact and release up to 5 pieces of explosive shrapnel that deal regular damage. The initial blast has a deceptively large radius of 5 tiles (comparable to Rocket III at 6 tiles), while the smaller shrapnel explosions have a radius of 3 tiles.

Notes

  • When using Shroomite Armor, Stynger Bolts qualify as Rockets.
