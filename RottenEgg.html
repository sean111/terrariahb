PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Rotten eggs are obtainable from Goodie bag. They are a thrown projectile that explodes when it hits any object and has no chance of being retrieved.

This thrown weapon is unique in that it can hit and kill friendly NPCs. The eggs may also damage other players, even if PvP is not enabled.
