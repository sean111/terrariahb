Shark Fin is an Item dropped from Sharks. It is used to craft the Hunter Potion and the Water Walking Potion at an Alchemy Station, as well as the endgame items Megashark and Neptune's Shell. Due to the fact that Sharks only spawn in the Oceans at the ends of a map, this Item is relatively difficult to farm.
