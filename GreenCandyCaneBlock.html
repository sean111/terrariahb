Candy Cane Blocks are found in presents dropped during the Christmas event. It is easy to obtain presents because any enemy can drop it; even Zombies. When placed individually, the color pattern may be switched, with red on the outside, and white as the main stripe, but they will always line up with each other. Candy Cane Blocks have no value other than as building material, and crafting Candy Cane Wall.
