This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Cobalt Pickaxe is an alternative to the Cobalt Drill, for those that prefer to use pickaxes, or find the drill's noise to be annoying. Appearances and sounds aside, the two tools are functionally identical (Except for that the Cobalt Pickaxe inflicts knockback, whereas the drill does not). However, unlike drills, pickaxes are reforged with any Melee modifiers, including size, speed, and knockback, making Legendary the best possible prefix.

Can mine any block except Adamantite Ore, Titanium Ore, Chlorophyte Ore, and Lihzahrd Bricks.
