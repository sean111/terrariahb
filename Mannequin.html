The Mannequin is a Decorative Furniture item that can be crafted using twenty Wood and can be placed on top of solid blocks or platforms, but not on other furniture. It can be used to hold Armor and Vanity items. It is not possible to place Familiar clothes on a Mannequin.

Notes

  • When the Auto-Pause option is turned on, armor can be placed on a Mannequin by placing it on a hotkey slot first, then selecting said slot and clicking on the mannequin.
  • When you place a Fish Bowl on the Mannequin it appears to have eyes.
Bug : In multiplayer games, if one player places armor on the mannequin by repeatedly clicking the mannequin with armor selected, while another player repeatedly removes the armor, there is a high chance of duplicating the armor.
Bug : In multiplayer games, if two mannequins are next to each other and one player places armor on the mannequin while another player's mouse cursor is on the same mannequin, the item will be duplicated.
Bug : If you select something on your hotbar, pick up a piece of clothing in your inventory, and attempt to place it on the mannequin, the item selected on your hotbar will activate. This can make it very annoying to place clothing on mannequins since with a high-tier pickaxe or drill you break the mannequin every time you try to place something on it. This can be avoided by selecting a weapon rather than a tool, however, the weapon will still fire.
Bug : If you put armor on a mannequin while there is already armor, the old armor will be lost!

Trivia

  • A total of 56 Mannequins (1120 Wood) would be needed if the player wants a separate mannequin for each armor set. (Including helm/mask/headgear.)
