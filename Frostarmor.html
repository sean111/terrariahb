This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Frost Armor is an armor set that grants the ability to inflict Frostburn on enemies with melee or ranged attacks when the whole set is worn, as well as causing all ranged and melee attacks to give off light - This makes it very useful for exploring caves.

Ice Golems will drop one piece of this armor every time they are killed; therefore, it takes at least three Ice Golem kills to get the full suit, and on average, five and a half times. Ice Golems spawn in the Snow biome during a Blizzard in Hardmode.

The Frostburn debuff is particularly devastating when used against worm enemies because it affects each segment, thereby multiplying the damage dealt by the number of segments.

Visually, wearing the set causes a "snow trail" to be left behind the player as they walk, similar to running with Hermes Boots. Also, taking damage causes the shattering sound heard when breaking through ice.

Notes

Anomaly: The set bonus applies to Traps and the projectiles shot by the Magical Harp.
Anomaly: Frost Armor causes the Frost Staff to take advantage of on-hit effects, and appears to be an unlisted set bonus. These include the Frostburn debuff from the set itself, as well as flasks and the Fire Gauntlet, which would normally only affect melee attacks.
Anomaly: If you Grapple to a floor, the snow "puffs" will come out of your feet infinitely until you stop

None of the bosses, except for The Destroyer, are immune to the Frostburn debuff, making this set useful against mechanical bosses.The Frost armor set can easily be farmed by creating a large one sided trench with a roof over the top of it with a one block hole in it. The Ice Golem will not be able to shoot you because of its 'laser' spawning off-center when you are above it. This is useful because Frost armor is stronger than most hardmode alternatives and is relatively easy to get using this method.

The Frostburn effect adds light to all melee/range attacks, which lets pickaxes and drills produce light.

This armor can create a deadly combination of fire and frostburn damage when combined with the Fire Gauntlet.

Trivia

  • If the Breastplate and Leggings are paired with a gold helmet, and you dye all pieces with Blue Flame and Black dye, you will get an afterimage and pulsing aura, akin to Hallowed armor. This was the armor Red used to wear, before his specific set was added.
