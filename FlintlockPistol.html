The Flintlock Pistol is a Gun that fires Bullets. Sporting a fast attack speed, it deals 10 damage by default plus the damage of the particular Bullet used: It deals 17 damage with Musket Balls, and with Meteor Shot or Silver Bullets deals 19 damage. It can be bought from the Arms Dealer.
