This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

The Ice Bow is a Hardmode Bow that drops from Mimics stylized as Ice Chests, which are exclusive to Underground Snow Biomes. It is slightly more powerful than the Hallowed Repeater, meaning it is the second strongest bow in the PC version damage wise, but also slightly slower. In addition, the mouse button cannot be held down for continuous fire.
All arrows fired by this bow are converted to special Frost Arrows, which travel faster with less of an arc as a result, but lose all special properties. However, unlike the craftable Frostburn Arrows, they don't inflict the Frostburn status.
This bow is similar to the Marrow, though it trades slightly lower velocity for higher power.

Trivia

  • This bow may be a reference to the bow carried by Ashe in the game League of Legends or Drow Ranger in Dota 2.
