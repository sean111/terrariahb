The in-game animation of Shadow armor.

Shadow armor consists of a Shadow Helmet, Shadow Scalemail and Shadow Greaves.

A full set grants a basic 19 defense, and a 15% increase in movement speed. Each individual piece worn gives a 7% increase in melee attack speed.

If the full suit is visible, the player leaves an afterimage while running (like full Necro Armor) and emits shadow particles. Also, the Rocket Boots will emit purple flames.

It takes a total of 60 Demonite Bars and 45 Shadow Scales to craft a full suit of Shadow armor, so if you fight the Eater of Worlds twice, it should be enough to make the full set.
