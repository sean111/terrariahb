PC-Only Content
This information (or parts of it) applies only to the PC version of Terraria.

Chlorophyte Bullets have a homing effect - if close enough, they will curve to hit an enemy, including being able to turn around corners and even do a 180 turn if need be, sometimes even managing to hit enemies behind your back opposite of where you're aiming.

Notes

  • Works well with shotguns as the homing effect nullifies spread.
  • Currently the highest base damage bullet in the game.
  • In PvP, the Chlorophyte Bullets will not home in on players.
