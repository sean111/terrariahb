Mummy

A player being approached by two Mummies.

The Mummy is a hardmode desert enemy. In appearance, it is a figure wrapped in gray cloth. It appears to follow the Fighter AI. Regular mummies spawn even during the day. All three types of mummies can spawn underground if there are enough sand blocks.

Dark Mummies are 150% faster than other Mummies. All mummies receive a 200% speed increase when they are reduced to 50% health or lower. Regular Mummies inflict Slow, Dark Mummies inflict Silenced and Darkness, and Light Mummies inflict Confused.

Dark and light mummies can easily be farmed for their shards by corrupting or hallowing a section of desert, or making an artificial desert. All that is necessary is a one-wide flat surface of pearlsand/ebonsand. However, don't have both surfaces too close from each other - as one cancels out the other.

Notes

  • The console version adds Spectral and Shadow Mummies, which have approximately the same stats as light and dark, but don't drop the shards needed for the Dao of Pow.
  • It is possible to receive both a Megaphone and a Blindfold at the same time from killing one Dark Mummy.
